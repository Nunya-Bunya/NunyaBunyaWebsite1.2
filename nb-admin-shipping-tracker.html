The Ship Rules

1. Define MVP before building. 2. MVP = smallest thing that proves the idea. 3. Ship in days, not weeks. 4. Track signal immediately. 5. 14-day rule: no signal = kill candidate. 6. Stop polishing shipped things. 7. One BUILDING item per business at a time. 8. BLOCKED = do something else. 9. Ask "Can I ship this today?" every session. 10. Revenue > everything.
