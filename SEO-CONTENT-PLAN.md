## Blog Post Template

Each post should include:
- **H1** with target keyword (naturally worded)
- **Meta title** < 60 chars, keyword-first
- **Meta description** < 160 chars with CTA
- **Canonical tag** pointing to the post URL
- **OG + Twitter tags** (matching existing blog posts)
- **Internal links:** min 2 to other blog posts + 1 to services.html
- **Inline CTA box** (matching existing blog-post-cta pattern)
- **Bottom CTA section** (matching existing "Want me to handle your marketing?" pattern)
- **Keep Reading section** linking to 2 related posts
- **Matomo tracking** (already on all pages)
