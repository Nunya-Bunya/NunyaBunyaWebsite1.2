The hard truth
📉

The Middle Class Is Shrinking

In 1971, 61% of Americans were middle class. Today it's 50% — and falling. The rungs are being pulled off the ladder.

🎓

Your Degree Won't Save You

A four-year degree costs $120K+ and gets you an entry-level role that AI now performs in seconds. The credential economy is broken.

🤖

AI Is Eating Entry-Level Jobs

The first roles to go aren't the top ones. Junior copywriters, analysts, coordinators, paralegals. AI does it faster, cheaper, and without HR.

🏦

The System Rewards Owners, Not Workers

Every efficiency gain goes upward. More output, same salary. The gap between employee and owner has never been wider — and it's accelerating.

The only move left is to play outside the system. Build something that belongs to you. Stop renting your time and start owning your output.
