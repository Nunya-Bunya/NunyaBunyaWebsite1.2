Three Things I'd Try

📍 Google

Get more people through your doors with better Google visibility

Your Google listing only has 10 photos, but places with 20+ photos get 40% more engagement from people searching for cafes and wine bars. I'd load up more shots of your food, drinks, and that cozy interior vibe — plus add an embedded Google Map to your website so people can find you easier when they're ready to visit.

🌐 Website

Turn your website visitors into customers who actually order

You've got online booking sorted, but there's no way for people to order food or drinks online for pickup. With your strong cafe side, you're missing out on the grab-and-go crowd who want to order their coffee and pastries ahead of time. Even just adding takeaway ordering could capture those busy professionals in your area.

⭐ Reviews

Show off what makes people love coming back

With over 2,000 Google reviews, you've clearly got customers who love the experience, but none of those testimonials appear on your website. Adding some of your best reviews to your homepage would give new visitors instant confidence that they're choosing the right spot for their coffee catch-up or wine night.

You've built something special that people genuinely love — now it's just about making it easier for more people to discover that and get through your doors. The foundation is rock solid, so these tweaks could really amplify what's already working.
