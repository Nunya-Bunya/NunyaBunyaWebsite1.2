03 The Client Journey (Client's POV)
1
Discovery
Finds NB via content, referral, or outreach. Downloads lead magnet OR books call.
2
Sales
Gets 5-email nurture (auto). Books discovery call. Human Ben runs call. Receives proposal. Signs + pays.
3
Onboarding
Welcome email. Nextcloud portal link. Intake questionnaire. Human Ben explains 3-phase process.
4
Opening Gambit (Week 1-2)
Audit + strategy delivered to portal. Human Ben presents findings to client.
5
The Fitout (Week 3-6)
Assets appear in portal as they are completed. Human Ben gets approval at checkpoints. Website, booking, and emails go live.
6
The Polish (Week 7-8)
Final package delivered to /08-Delivery/. Human Ben walks client through their complete system.
7
Ongoing
Monthly content + reports appear in portal. Human Monthly check-in call.

  • n8n workflows

  • MASTER PLANS
  • Cron jobs

  • How assets are generated
