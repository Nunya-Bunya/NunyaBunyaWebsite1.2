FAQ

Questions?
Answered.

Do I need any design software or subscriptions? +
Nope. You just need a web browser — Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge, whatever you use. The templates are HTML files. Open them, type in your content, and print to PDF. That's the entire workflow. No Canva, no Adobe, no Word, nothing.
What exactly do I send you to get started? +
Your logo file (PNG or SVG on a transparent or dark background), your primary and secondary brand colours (hex codes if you have them, or just describe them and we'll pick), your business name, ABN, email, phone, and website. That's it.
How long does it actually take? +
We quote 48 hours from when we receive your brand details, but in practice it's usually faster. You'll get a ZIP in your inbox with all 13 files ready to open.
Can I change the templates after I receive them? +
Yes, completely. They're HTML files — if you know a little HTML you can change anything you want. If you don't, we can make edits for you. Your brand.json file also means if you rebrand, we can update the whole kit quickly for a small fee.
What if they don't match my brand? +
If the templates don't look right, we fix them — end of story. We build to the exact brand details you give us. If something's off, we rebuild it. If after revisions you're still not satisfied, full refund, no questions asked.
Is this just for marketing agencies or any business? +
Any business. The templates cover invoicing, proposals, contracts, reporting, meeting notes, onboarding — every business needs these. Tradies, cafes, coaches, consultants, agencies, gyms, legal firms — it doesn't matter what industry you're in, you need professional documents.
