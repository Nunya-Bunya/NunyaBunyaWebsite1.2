About

The story behind
Nunya Bunya.

Ben Conner
Founder & Strategist — Nunya Bunya
Brisbane, QLD · Available across Australia

I'm Ben Conner. I started Nunya Bunya because I watched too many amazing businesses struggle with marketing — not because they were bad at business, but because they didn't have the right support.

Big agencies treated them like account numbers. Freelancers disappeared. Templates left them confused. There had to be a better way.

I'm a one-person operation by design — you get 1:1 attention, not an account manager who barely remembers your name. I handle strategy, creative, and execution. No hand-offs. No middlemen. And I'm obsessed with results — not vanity metrics, not pretty reports. Growth.

Every business deserves great marketing. That's the belief. That's what I do.
