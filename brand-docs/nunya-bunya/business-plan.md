# Nunya Bunya — Business Plan

## Executive Summary

Nunya Bunya is an AI-powered digital marketing agency based in Brisbane, Australia, serving small-to-medium businesses that are tired of paying too much for marketing that doesn't work. We replace expensive SaaS tools and guesswork with an entirely self-hosted, open-source marketing stack — giving clients enterprise-grade analytics, automation, and reporting at a fraction of the cost.

**Legal entity:** Nunya Bunya Pty Ltd
**Location:** Brisbane, QLD, Australia
**Founded:** 2025
**Founder:** Ben Alek Conner
**Stage:** Pre-revenue, operational

---

## Mission

To give small businesses the same marketing firepower as big companies — without the big company price tag. We use AI and open-source tools to deliver real, measurable results and charge fairly for it.

## Vision

To become the most trusted marketing partner for small businesses in Australia by proving that data-driven, AI-powered marketing isn't just for corporations — it's for everyone.

---

## Problem

Small businesses face three core marketing problems:

1. **They can't afford it.** Enterprise tools cost $500-2,000/month. Agencies charge $3,000-10,000/month. Most SMBs simply go without.
2. **They can't measure it.** Most have no analytics, no tracking, no idea which channels drive leads. They spend money and hope.
3. **They don't trust agencies.** They've been burned by agencies that promise the world, deliver a monthly PDF, and lock them into 12-month contracts.

## Solution

Nunya Bunya solves all three:

1. **Affordable:** Our entire marketing stack costs ~$16 AUD/month to host. We pass the savings to clients with competitive pricing.
2. **Measurable:** Every action is tracked — website visits (Matomo), email engagement (Mautic), link clicks (Shlink), deal pipeline (Twenty CRM). Clients get live dashboards, not monthly PDFs.
3. **Trustworthy:** No lock-in contracts. Cancel anytime. We show you the data — if it's not working, you'll see it before we tell you.

## Target Market

**Primary:** Small businesses in Brisbane/SEQ with 1-20 employees, $200K-$5M annual revenue, who currently do their own marketing or use a cheap freelancer. Industries: trades, professional services, hospitality, health & wellness, retail.

**Secondary:** Solopreneurs and personal brands who need a content + lead generation engine but can't justify a full agency retainer.

**Ideal Client Profile:**
- Has a website (even a basic one)
- Gets some traffic but doesn't know where from
- Has tried Facebook ads or Google Ads with mixed results
- Knows they need help but has been burned before
- Budget: $200-$1,000/week

## Competitive Advantage

1. **Self-hosted stack = lowest cost base in the industry.** We don't pay for HubSpot, Mailchimp, GA4, or Bitly. Our marginal cost per client is near zero.
2. **AI-augmented content production.** We use Claude, GPT, Gemini, and custom agents to produce content 5x faster than traditional agencies.
3. **Full transparency.** Clients see their own live dashboards. No black boxes.
4. **No lock-in.** Month-to-month. If we suck, fire us.
5. **Founder-led.** Every client works directly with Ben. No account manager runaround.

- Twenty CRM — sales pipeline
- Metabase — BI dashboards and client reports

- n8n — workflow automation

2. Ben reviews and edits

4. Performance tracked in Matomo + Metabase
