# Power Portraits — Business Plan

## Executive Summary

Power Portraits is a premium headshot and portrait photography studio in Brisbane, targeting professionals, creatives, and entrepreneurs who need images that match their ambition. We combine studio-quality photography with a directed, confidence-building experience that leaves clients feeling like they just stepped off a magazine cover.

**Location:** Brisbane, QLD (studio TBD — currently mobile/outdoor)
**Founded:** 2026 (pre-launch)
**Founder:** Ben Alek Conner
**Stage:** Pre-launch, building waitlist

---

## Mission

To give every professional a portrait that makes them proud to be seen. Not just a headshot — a transformation.

## Vision

To become Brisbane's most sought-after portrait studio, known for making ordinary people look extraordinary.

---

## Problem

1. **Most headshots are terrible.** Business owners use cropped selfies, 10-year-old photos, or photos shot by friends. They know it's bad but don't know where to go.
2. **Existing options are either too cheap or too expensive.** $50 mall headshots look like $50. Studio sessions cost $500-$1,000+ and feel inaccessible.
3. **The experience is awkward.** Most people hate being photographed because photographers don't direct them. They stand there stiffly and get stiff-looking photos.

## Solution

Power Portraits solves the experience problem:
1. **Professional direction.** Ben guides every pose, every expression. Clients don't need to know what to do.
2. **Premium quality at two price points.** Full sessions ($450) for those who want the works. Portrait Days ($149) for those who want great photos without the premium price.
3. **Fast turnaround.** 3-5 business days. No waiting months for edited photos.
4. **Commercial rights included.** Use the photos anywhere — LinkedIn, website, social, print. No usage fees.

---

## Target Market

**Primary:** Professionals in Brisbane who need headshots for LinkedIn, websites, or corporate use. Ages 28-55. Industries: finance, law, real estate, consulting, tech, health.

**Secondary:** Creatives, entrepreneurs, and personal brands who want distinctive, editorial-quality portraits. Actors, speakers, coaches, influencers.

**Tertiary:** Small businesses needing team photos, about-page headshots, or event documentation.

## Competitive Advantage

1. **Directed experience.** Ben is a trained director. The session feels like a mini film shoot, not an awkward photo day.
2. **Two-tier pricing.** Full sessions for premium clients, Portrait Days for budget-conscious. No one is priced out.
3. **Fast delivery.** 3-5 days, not 2-4 weeks.
4. **Personal brand integration.** Ben can advise on how/where to use the photos for maximum impact — LinkedIn optimisation, website placement, social media strategy. No other photographer does this.
5. **Marketing engine.** Power Portraits is marketed by Nunya Bunya's full stack — Mautic, Matomo, Shlink, drip campaigns. Most photographers rely on word-of-mouth only.
