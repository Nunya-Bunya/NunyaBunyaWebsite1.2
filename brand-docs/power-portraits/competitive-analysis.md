# Power Portraits — Competitive Analysis

## Market Overview

Brisbane's headshot/portrait market is fragmented. On one end: cheap mall studios and mobile photographers ($50-$150). On the other: established studio photographers ($500-$1,500). Very few operate in the middle with a strong brand identity and directed experience.

---

## Direct Competitors

### 1. Corporate Headshots Brisbane
- **Pricing:** $350-$600/session
- **Strengths:** Established, good Google ranking, corporate focus
- **Weaknesses:** Generic corporate look, no creative direction, stiff poses
- **How PP wins:** We direct the session like a film set — clients feel confident, not awkward

### 2. Luke Marsden Photography
- **Pricing:** $400-$800/session
- **Strengths:** High-quality work, good portfolio, personal brand
- **Weaknesses:** Expensive, long wait times, limited availability
- **How PP wins:** Portrait Day at $149 captures the price-sensitive segment he misses

### 3. JC Photography Brisbane
- **Pricing:** $200-$450/session
- **Strengths:** Mid-range pricing, good reviews, team photo packages
- **Weaknesses:** Generic style, no distinctive brand, forgettable website
- **How PP wins:** Brand identity. PP looks and feels premium — the website alone communicates quality

### 4. iPhone/Selfie (the real competitor)
- **Who:** Everyone with a phone
- **Pricing:** Free
- **Strengths:** Convenient, instant, no booking required
- **Weaknesses:** Terrible quality, bad lighting, no direction, unprofessional
- **How PP wins:** Before/after comparisons. Show a LinkedIn selfie next to a PP portrait — sells itself

## Opportunities

1. **LinkedIn boom.** Remote work + personal branding trend means more people need professional headshots than ever
2. **Team photo packages.** Companies updating their websites need consistent team headshots — recurring revenue opportunity
3. **Portrait Day model.** Low-cost, high-volume days create accessibility and word-of-mouth
4. **Content for NB portfolio.** Every PP shoot creates social content for both PP and NB social channels

---

## Threats

1. **AI headshot generators** (Remini, Aragon, etc.) — $30 for AI-generated headshots from selfies. Quality is improving fast.
2. **Smartphone cameras improving** — The "good enough" bar keeps rising
3. **Recession sensitivity** — Professional headshots are discretionary spending
4. **No studio overhead risk** — Starting without a studio is flexible but limits perceived legitimacy
