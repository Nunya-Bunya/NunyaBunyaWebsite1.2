# Ben Alek Conner — Business Plan

## Executive Summary

Ben Alek Conner is a personal brand and production company for Ben Conner — director, creative consultant, and digital strategist based in Brisbane. The brand operates across three revenue streams: creative direction (commercial + narrative filmmaking), consulting (AI/marketing systems), and content (YouTube, speaking). It exists both as a business and a creative outlet.

**Location:** Brisbane, QLD, Australia
**Founded:** 2025
**Stage:** Early, building audience and portfolio

---

## Mission

To tell stories that matter and help creative people build things that work.

## Vision

To be recognised as a director and creative strategist who bridges the gap between art and commerce — someone filmmakers and entrepreneurs both want to work with.

---

## The Two Sides

BAC is intentionally dual-purpose:

**Business side:**
- Creative direction for brands (commercials, branded content, music videos)
- Consulting on AI-powered marketing, tech stacks, and automation
- Speaking and workshops on filmmaking, AI, and creative entrepreneurship

**Creative side:**
- Feature film development
- Short films and documentaries
- ORCA Film Awards YouTube show
- Photography projects (street photography, photobooks)

The business side funds the creative side. The creative side builds the reputation that attracts business clients. They're symbiotic.

---

## Target Market

**For creative direction:**
- Brands and agencies needing commercial/branded content directors
- Musicians needing music videos
- Indie producers needing a director for short films
- Brisbane-based, but open to remote/travel projects

**For consulting:**
- Small business owners who want to build AI-powered marketing systems
- Agencies wanting to adopt open-source tools
- Creatives who want to monetise their work but don't know how

**For content/speaking:**
- Aspiring filmmakers and content creators (YouTube audience)
- Business conference organisers wanting a creative/AI speaker
- Podcast hosts covering filmmaking, marketing, or entrepreneurship

## Revenue Model

| Stream | Price Range | Volume Target |
|--------|------------|---------------|
| Creative Direction (commercials) | $2,000-$10,000/project | 2/month |
| Creative Direction (music videos) | $1,000-$5,000/project | 1/month |
| Consulting | $150/hour or $2,000/day | 4 hours/week |
| Speaking/Workshops | $500-$2,000/engagement | 1/month |
| YouTube AdSense | Variable | Passive |
| Affiliate/sponsorships | Variable | As audience grows |

**Revenue targets:**
- Year 1: $3,000/month (consulting + occasional directing gig)
- Year 2: $6,000/month (regular directing work + consulting + YouTube revenue)
- Year 3: $10,000/month (established director + consulting practice + content income)

---

## Competitive Advantage

1. **Unique combination.** Director + marketing consultant + AI builder. Nobody else in Brisbane has this exact overlap.
2. **Nunya Bunya as proof.** The entire NB marketing stack is a living demo of what BAC consulting delivers.
3. **Film credibility.** Actual filmmaking experience (not just "content creation") creates respect in the creative community.
4. **YouTube audience as distribution.** Every video builds authority and attracts both creative and business clients.

## Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| No directing work comes in | Consulting and NB salary provide income floor |
| YouTube doesn't grow | Quality over quantity — 2 strong videos/month, not daily vlogging |
| Spread too thin | BAC is the "creative half" of Ben's work — NB handles the business half |
| Brisbane market too small for film | Target remote/interstate work, use YouTube for national reach |
| Creative work doesn't pay | Business side (consulting) explicitly funds creative side |
