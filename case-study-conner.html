Strategy — Process

How We Did It

1

Discovery & Audit

Analysed Kevin's unique positioning as an insurance company insider turned advocate and identified gaps in communicating complex injury specialties. Discovered strong case results but weak digital presence failing to attract catastrophic injury cases worth $150,000+.

2

Brand Bible (BFK)

Created comprehensive brand strategy positioning Conner Injury Law as Washington's specialist for complex cases like TBI and CRPS. Developed messaging hierarchy emphasising Kevin's $50 million track record and insurance company playbook knowledge.

3

AI Photoshoot

Generated 10 professional images featuring Kevin in Mount Vernon courthouse, consultation settings, and Washington State backdrops. Created authentic visuals showing competence and approachability for traumatic brain injury and workplace accident victims.

4

Website Build

Built 5-page responsive website with dedicated sections for each specialty including Car Accidents, TBI, CRPS, Workplace Injuries, and Catastrophic Cases. Implemented local SEO targeting Mount Vernon and broader Washington State injury searches.

5

Asset Library

Developed 10 branded social media templates highlighting case victories, injury law education, and Washington State legal updates. Created consistent visual system reinforcing expertise in complex personal injury cases across Skagit County.
