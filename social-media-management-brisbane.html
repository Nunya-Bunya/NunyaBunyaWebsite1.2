Why Social Media Matters for Brisbane Businesses

Brisbane has over 2.6 million people in the greater metro area and one of the fastest-growing small business ecosystems in Australia. Your customers are on social media every day — scrolling past competitors, discovering new businesses, and deciding where to spend their money.

The businesses that show up consistently are the ones that win. The ones that post once a month and disappear get forgotten. That is just how algorithms work.

Platforms We Manage

We do not believe every business needs to be on every platform. We help you focus on the channels where your audience actually spends time, so your effort converts into enquiries — not just vanity metrics.

Facebook

Still the largest platform in Australia. Essential for local businesses, community engagement, events, and Facebook Marketplace visibility. Strong for 30+ demographics.

Instagram

Visual storytelling for hospitality, retail, fitness, beauty, and lifestyle brands. Reels, Stories, and carousels drive discovery and engagement for Brisbane audiences.

LinkedIn

The channel for B2B, professional services, consultants, and recruiters. Thought leadership content and networking that generates leads without paid ads.

Twitter / X

Useful for real-time engagement, industry commentary, and brand personality. Works well for media, tech, and businesses with strong opinions worth sharing.

TikTok

The fastest-growing platform in Australia. Short-form video that reaches audiences organically. Particularly strong for trades, food, fitness, and retail in Brisbane.

Google Business Profile

Not a social platform in the traditional sense, but posting regularly to your GBP boosts local SEO and shows Google you are an active, legitimate business.

Results You Can Expect

We are not going to promise you 10,000 followers in 30 days. That is not how social media works for local businesses, and anyone telling you otherwise is lying. Here is what you can realistically expect:

Month 1–2

Consistent posting schedule established. Brand voice locked in. Early engagement growth as your existing audience starts seeing you regularly again. Professional visual identity across all platforms.

Month 3–4

Measurable follower growth. Increased website traffic from social channels. More inbound messages and comments. You start getting recognised as an active, trustworthy business in your space.

Month 5+

Compounding returns. Social proof builds on itself. New customers mention they found you on Instagram or Facebook. Your social presence becomes a genuine lead generation channel, not just a billboard.

Social media is a long game. The businesses that win are the ones that show up consistently for 6+ months. We are built to help you do exactly that.
