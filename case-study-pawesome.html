Strategy — Process

How We Did It

1

Discovery & Audit

We analysed Jake's journey from anxious Kelpie owner to trusted service provider across Brisbane Northside. Our audit revealed the gap between his personal approach and generic pet industry marketing.

2

Brand Bible (BFK)

We crafted positioning around 'the service Jake wished existed' with messaging focused on small groups and genuine communication. The brand bible established warm, trustworthy visual identity distinct from corporate competitors.

3

AI Photoshoot

We created 10 authentic images showing diverse dogs on Brisbane Northside adventures from Paddington parks to Gap hiking trails. Each photo captured the personal, caring approach that sets Pawesome apart from corporate services.

4

Website Build

We built a 5-page conversion website with dedicated service pages for Daily Walking, Adventure Hikes, and Overnight Stays. The site includes waitlist management and showcases coverage across all six Brisbane Northside suburbs.

5

Asset Library

We developed 10 social media templates highlighting real client transformations and service variety. Assets include location-specific content for each suburb and service-focused materials for puppy visits to adventure hikes.
