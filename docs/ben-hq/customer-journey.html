Nunya Bunya — Customer Journey Map

What the customer sees, what happens behind the scenes, and which tool does what.

The Full Journey: Stranger → Lead → Client

STRANGER                    LEAD                         HOT LEAD                    CLIENT
───────── ──────────────── ──────────────────────────── ──────────────────────── ──────────────
Sees ad    Clicks link      Gets emails     Engages      Score > 50    Books call    Signs up
or post    or searches      over 12 days    with content  Alert fires   Proposal     Onboarded

Stage 1: Discovery — "Who is Nunya Bunya?"

What the customer does:

  • Sees a social media post, Google ad, or hears about NB from someone
  • Clicks a link to nunyabunya.com

What they see:

  • The NB website (hosted on Vercel)

What's happening behind the scenes:

Customer clicks link
    │
    ├─→ If link is go.nunyabunya.com/social (or /home, /services, etc.)
    │       │
    │       └─→ SHLINK (Link Tracker) logs the click
    │           Records: timestamp, country, city, referrer, device
    │           Then redirects to the real URL (nunyabunya.com/social-media-special)
    │
    └─→ Customer lands on nunyabunya.com
            │
            └─→ MATOMO (Website Analytics) tracking script fires
                Records: page viewed, time on page, device, location, referrer
                Creates an anonymous visitor profile
                If they visit /services → Matomo Goal #4 fires
                If they visit /pricing → Matomo Goal #5 fires

Tools involved:

ToolWhat it does at this stage
Shlink (Link Tracker)Tracks which links get clicked, from where
Matomo (Website Analytics)Tracks what pages they visit, how long they stay
Mixpost (Social Scheduler)Scheduled the social post they clicked on

Stage 2: Interest — "I want to learn more"

What the customer does:

  • Clicks "Free Strategy Session" or "Free Audit" on the website
  • Fills out the form with their name, email, phone, business name

What they see:

  • The landing page at mautic.nunyabunya.com/free-strategy-session
  • NB-branded page with form
  • After submitting: "Thanks! We'll be in touch within 24 hours."

What's happening behind the scenes:

Customer fills out form and hits submit
    │
    └─→ MAUTIC (Email & Automations) receives the form submission
            │
            ├─→ Creates a new Contact (name, email, phone, company)
            │
            ├─→ Assigns +20 points (form submission scoring rule)
            │   Also +15 points (landing page visit) and +3 (page visit)
            │   Total: ~38 points already
            │
            ├─→ Adds contact to "Nunya Bunya" segment (ID: 6)
            │
            └─→ Adds contact to "NB Welcome Sequence" campaign (ID: 10)
                    │
                    └─→ Campaign immediately sends Email #1:
                        "Welcome — here's what we do differently"
At the same time, Mautic fires webhooks:
    │
    ├─→ N8N workflow "NB - Contact Form" (already existed)
    │       └─→ Sends Slack notification to #nunya-bunya channel
    │
    └─→ N8N workflow "NB - New Lead → Sales Pipeline"
            └─→ Creates a Person in TWENTY CRM (Sales Pipeline)
                with their name and email from the form

Tools involved:

ToolWhat it does at this stage
Mautic (Email & Automations)Captures lead, assigns points, starts drip campaign
n8n (Automations Hub)Routes the event to Slack and CRM
Twenty CRM (Sales Pipeline)Creates the lead as a contact
SlackYou get notified in #nunya-bunya

Stage 3: Nurture — "Drip emails over 12 days"

What the customer sees:

Over the next 12 days, they receive 5 emails (if they don't unsubscribe):

DayEmailSubjectGoal
0Welcome"Welcome — here's what we do differently"Build trust, explain the NB approach
2Value"The 3 biggest mistakes small businesses make with marketing"Educate, create pain awareness
5Proof"How we got a local business 47 leads in 30 days"Social proof, case study
8Offer"What's included in our Starter package"Present the solution
12Urgency"Last chance — free strategy session this week"Create urgency, final CTA

Each email has a CTA button linking to the booking page (go.nunyabunya.com/book → cal.com/nunyabunya).

What's happening behind the scenes:

MAUTIC campaign engine runs every 60 seconds (via mautic-cron container)
    │
    ├─→ Checks: "Has 2 days passed since Email #1?"
    │       Yes → Send Email #2 → Log "email sent" event
    │
    └─→ For each email sent:
            │
            ├─→ If customer OPENS the email:
            │       └─→ Mautic adds +5 points
            │           Matomo tracks (if tracking pixel loads)
            │
            ├─→ If customer CLICKS a link in the email:
            │       └─→ Mautic adds +10 points
            │           Shlink tracks the click (if using go.nunyabunya.com links)
            │           Matomo tracks the page visit
            │
            └─→ If customer does NOTHING:
                    └─→ Next email still sends on schedule
                        (unless they unsubscribe)

Points accumulation example:

Form submitted:         +20 points
Landing page visit:     +15 points
Page visit:              +3 points
                        ─────────
Starting score:          38 points

Opens Email #1:          +5 = 43
Clicks link in Email #2: +10 = 53  ← NOW A HOT LEAD (> 50)
Opens Email #3:          +5 = 58
Visits services page:    +3 = 61

Tools involved:

ToolWhat it does at this stage
Mautic (Email & Automations)Sends scheduled emails, tracks opens/clicks, scores lead
Mautic Cron (background)Runs campaign engine every 60 seconds
Shlink (Link Tracker)Tracks clicks on CTA links in emails
Matomo (Website Analytics)Tracks when they visit the site from email links

Stage 4: Hot Lead Alert — "This one's ready"

What triggers it:

Contact's score crosses 50 points (from email engagement + page visits).

What the customer sees:

Nothing — this is internal.

What's happening behind the scenes:

Mautic detects contact entered "Hot Leads" segment (score > 50)
    │
    ├─→ Fires webhook to N8N "NB - Hot Lead Alert"
    │       │
    │       └─→ Posts to Slack #alerts:
    │           "🔥 Hot Lead Alert
    │            Name: Test Workflow
    │            Email: test-workflow@nunyabunya.com
    │            Score: 60
    │            Company: Test Business
    │            → View in Mautic"
    │
    └─→ You see the alert in Slack and know to follow up personally

Tools involved:

ToolWhat it does at this stage
Mautic (Email & Automations)Detects score threshold, fires webhook
n8n (Automations Hub)Routes alert to Slack
SlackYou get the alert in #alerts

Stage 5: Booking — "Let's talk"

What the customer does:

  • Clicks the booking link in an email or on the website
  • Goes to go.nunyabunya.com/book → redirected to cal.com/nunyabunya
  • Picks a time slot and books a call

What they see:

  • Cal.com booking page with your availability
  • Confirmation email from Cal.com

What's happening behind the scenes:

Customer books on Cal.com
    │
    └─→ N8N workflow "NB - Cal.com Booking Alert" (already existed)
            │
            └─→ Posts to Slack:
                "New booking from [Name] at [Time]"
Meanwhile:
    │
    ├─→ Matomo Goal #3 fires (Strategy Session Booked — URL contains "book")
    │
    └─→ Shlink logs the click on go.nunyabunya.com/book

Tools involved:

ToolWhat it does at this stage
Cal.com (Booking)Handles scheduling, sends confirmation
Shlink (Link Tracker)Tracks the booking link click
Matomo (Website Analytics)Records conversion goal
n8n (Automations Hub)Sends Slack notification

Stage 6: Proposal & Close — "Let's work together"

What happens:

  • You have the strategy call
  • If they want to proceed, you send a proposal
  • Deal moves through the pipeline in Twenty CRM

What's happening behind the scenes:

YOU manually update Twenty CRM (Sales Pipeline):
    │
    ├─→ Move deal: Lead → Qualified → Proposal → Won
    │
    └─→ When deal is marked "Won":
            │
            └─→ (Future n8n workflow opportunity):
                Send welcome email via Mautic
                Add to "Client" segment
                Notify #wins in Slack

Tools involved:

ToolWhat it does at this stage
Twenty CRM (Sales Pipeline)Track deal through pipeline stages
Mautic (Email & Automations)Send welcome/onboarding emails

Stage 7: Ongoing — "Monthly reports & retention"

What the client sees:

  • Monthly report dashboard or PDF
  • Continued email communication
  • Social media content

What's happening behind the scenes:

Every Monday at 9am:
    │
    └─→ N8N "Weekly Marketing Summary" fires
            │
            ├─→ Pulls lead count from Mautic API
            ├─→ Pulls traffic stats from Matomo API
            └─→ Posts summary to Slack #wins

METABASE (Reports):
    │
    └─→ Dashboard pulls live data from ALL databases:
        - Mautic DB → email open rates, click rates, campaign performance
        - Matomo DB → website traffic, goals, conversions
        - Shlink DB → link clicks, geographic data
        - Twenty CRM DB → pipeline value, deals won/lost

Tools involved:

ToolWhat it does at this stage
Metabase (Reports)Pulls from all DBs, creates client dashboards
n8n (Automations Hub)Weekly summary to Slack
Mautic (Email & Automations)Ongoing campaigns, newsletters
Mixpost (Social Scheduler)Scheduled social content
