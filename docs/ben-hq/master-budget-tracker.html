8. QUARTERLY REVIEW CHECKLIST

Financial Review

  • ☐ Compile P&L for each business (full quarter)
  • ☐ Compare actuals to targets — note variances >10%
  • ☐ Review accounts receivable: chase any outstanding >60 days
  • ☐ Review accounts payable: confirm all obligations current
  • ☐ Update 13-week cash flow forecast
  • ☐ Review business bank accounts: confirm reserve levels
  • ☐ Review BAS obligations — confirm quarterly lodgement on track
  • ☐ Tax provision review: sufficient set aside?
  • ☐ Review fixed cost contracts: any coming up for renewal? Renegotiate if possible

Revenue Review

  • ☐ Which businesses exceeded target? Understand why — replicate
  • ☐ Which businesses missed target? Root cause analysis
  • ☐ Pipeline review: deals in progress by business
  • ☐ Pricing review: should any rates be increased?
  • ☐ Client retention: any clients at risk? What's the plan?
  • ☐ New revenue stream opportunities identified?

Cost Review

  • ☐ Identify any cost increases vs. prior quarter
  • ☐ Any subscriptions or contracts unused / underused? Cancel
  • ☐ Team efficiency: are labour costs proportionate to output?
  • ☐ Marketing spend ROI: what's performing, what's not?

Strategy

  • ☐ Set next-quarter revenue targets by business
  • ☐ Update 90-day content calendars
  • ☐ Review and update portfolio goals for the year
  • ☐ Identify top 3 priorities per business for next quarter
  • ☐ Note any risks or opportunities for portfolio

Compliance

  • ☐ All business registrations current (ASIC, ABN, etc.)
  • ☐ Insurance policies reviewed — adequate cover?
  • ☐ Contracts with key clients / suppliers: any up for renewal?
  • ☐ Staff / contractor agreements current?
  • ☐ Privacy and data compliance reviewed?
