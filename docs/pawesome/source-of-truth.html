PAWESOME DOG WALKERS: SOURCE OF TRUTH

Last Updated: 2026-04-19

Business Overview

Pawesome Dog Walkers is a real, revenue-generating dog walking business operated by Ben Conner in inner-north Brisbane. It is the fastest path to cash flow in Ben's business portfolio. Ben is the sole walker. The business launched as a real operation on 2026-04-14.

Website: pawesomedogwalkers.com

Phone: 0484 604 529

Operator: Ben Conner (sole walker)

Service Area

Inner-north Brisbane:

  • New Farm
  • Newstead
  • Teneriffe
  • Fortitude Valley

Services & Pricing

ServicePrice
First Walk (founding client offer)$25
Standard Daily Walk (30 min)$35
Weekly Package (5 walks)$150

Overnight Stay$75/night

Note: The $25 first walk offer is the primary acquisition tool. Standard pricing applies after the first walk.

Target Audience

  • Dog owners in inner-north Brisbane (New Farm, Newstead, Teneriffe, Fortitude Valley)
  • Professionals who work during the day and need midday walks
  • Apartment/townhouse owners whose dogs need outdoor time
  • New dog owners looking for reliable care

Brand Voice

Fun, friendly, trustworthy, energetic. Like talking to your favourite dog-loving neighbour. Posts are written as Ben personally (not as a faceless company). Local trust matters more than corporate polish.

Brand Colors

RoleColor
Primary (green)#2E6B4F
Accent (amber)#F4A742

Typography: Fredoka (headings), Nunito (body)

Key Messages

  1. Reliability: Ben shows up, on time, every time. No excuses.
  2. Communication: Photo updates after every walk. You always know how your dog's day went.
  3. Small groups: Max 4 dogs per walk. Your dog gets real attention, not crowd control.
  4. Local knowledge: Ben knows every park, every off-leash area, every water fountain in inner-north Brisbane.
  5. Genuine care: This is not a side hustle staffed by randoms. Ben walks every dog personally.

Differentiators

  • Solo operator: Ben walks every dog himself. No subcontractors, no strangers.
  • Max 4 dogs per walk: Small groups mean safer, more attentive walks.
  • GPS tracking: Clients can see the route their dog walked.
  • Photo updates: Every walk ends with photos sent to the owner.
  • Fully insured: Professional coverage for peace of mind.
  • Inner-north specialist: Deep knowledge of local parks, paths, and dog-friendly spots.

Content Rules

  • NEVER fabricate testimonials, reviews, or client stories
  • No fake people, quotes, or stats
  • Posts as Ben personally (first person, local trust)
  • No emojis unless they add real value
  • 80% value content (tips, park guides, dog care), 20% promotional (book a walk)
  • Real photos from real walks whenever possible; AI placeholders acceptable for launch but swap to real as they come

Current Status (as of 2026-04-19)

  • Business is LIVE and operating
  • Website is pawesomedogwalkers.com
  • Case study on nunyabunya.com provides credibility
  • Targeting first paid clients via local Facebook groups and word of mouth
  • No content plan exists yet (this is the gap being filled)
  • Instagram and Facebook accounts needed for content execution

Competitive Landscape

Inner-north Brisbane has several dog walking services, most of which are:

  • Large companies with rotating walkers (no consistency)
  • Apps that match random walkers to dogs (Madpaws, Pawshake)
  • Overpriced for what they deliver

Pawesome wins on: personal relationship, consistency (same walker every time), communication (photo updates), and local expertise.

Revenue Model

  • Per-walk and weekly package pricing
  • Recurring weekly clients are the core revenue driver
  • $25 first walk converts to $35/walk or $150/week regulars
  • Target: fill weekday schedule with recurring clients, then raise prices
