Break-Even Calculation

  • Contribution margin per session: $495.45 - $25.00 = $470.45
  • Monthly fixed costs (cash): $1,990
  • Monthly fixed costs (including depreciation): $2,620

Cash break-even: $1,990 / $470.45 = 4.2 sessions/month

P&L break-even (incl. depreciation): $2,620 / $470.45 = 5.6 sessions/month

Power Portraits covers all cash costs at just 5 sessions per month and is profitable on paper (including depreciation) at 6 sessions per month.

Even in Month 1 with 8 sessions, the business is profitable from day one.

Lifetime Value (LTV) Estimate

  • Average first booking value (ex GST): ~$500
  • Rebooking rate (conservative): 25% rebook within 12 months
  • Average rebook value: ~$600 (clients tend to upgrade)
  • Referral rate: 15% of clients refer at least one new client
  • Estimated LTV per client: $700-$850
  • LTV:CAC ratio (at steady state): 15:1 to 25:1 -- excellent unit economics
