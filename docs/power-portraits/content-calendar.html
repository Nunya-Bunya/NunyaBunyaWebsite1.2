Power Portraits -- 4-Week Content Calendar

Brand voice: Literary, unhurried, quietly commanding. Australian English.

Platforms: Instagram (4-5/wk + daily Stories + 2 Reels/wk) | LinkedIn (3/wk) | Google Business Profile (1/wk) | TikTok (2-3/wk)

Content Pillars:

  1. The Work -- Portfolio images, full-bleed portraits
  2. Behind the Lens -- Process, setup, lighting, editing BTS
  3. The Creative -- Client spotlights, tagging their work
  4. Perspective -- Thought pieces on image, perception, personal branding
  5. Invitation -- Session availability, seasonal offers, referral reminders

Monthly Themes (Rotating)

Use one theme per month. These shape the visual aesthetic and caption tone for the entire month without changing the pillar structure.

  1. Faces of Brisbane Creatives -- Spotlight local actors, musicians, designers, founders. Every portrait tells a Brisbane story. Heavy on Pillar 3.
  2. The Light Series -- Entire month explores directional light as subject matter. Dramatic shadows, rim light, window light. Heavy on Pillars 1 and 2.
  3. Transformation Month -- Before/after energy. Not cheesy makeover content -- the shift in how someone carries themselves once they see a portrait that actually looks like them. Heavy on Pillars 3 and 4.
  4. Behind the Curtain -- Pull the process apart. Show the studio, the gear, the decisions, the edits. Demystify the craft. Heavy on Pillar 2.

Daily Stories Plan (Instagram)

Post 1-3 Stories per day. These are low-effort, high-presence content. Shot on phone unless otherwise noted.

DayStory Content
MonMorning studio setup -- "Opening up for the week." Show the lights coming on, coffee in hand.
TueBTS from a session or edit -- a single behind-the-scenes frame or screen grab from editing.
WedClient portrait reveal -- share the final image with a "delivered today" caption. Tag the client.
ThuA thought or observation about photography, image, or creative work. Text on Parchment background.
FriAvailability check-in -- "A few sessions open next week" with a booking link sticker.
SatPortfolio reshare -- repost a grid image to Stories with a short reflection on the shoot.
SunPersonal or behind-the-curtain -- something from outside the studio. A walk, a book, a film. Show the person behind the lens.

Stories formatting:

  • Use Parchment (#F5F0E8) background for text-only Stories
  • Use Ink (#0F0F0F) background for portrait reshares
  • Cormorant Garamond for headlines, Inter for body (use closest available in Instagram's editor)
  • Keep text minimal. One sentence. Two at most.

Weekly Batching Guide

All content creation happens in two focused blocks. Do not scatter it across the week.

Sunday Afternoon -- Plan + Write (90 minutes)

  1. Review the week ahead (10 min) -- Open this calendar, identify which posts need new images vs. existing portfolio shots.
  2. Write all captions (50 min) -- Draft Monday through Saturday captions in a single document. LinkedIn posts first (they're longest), then Instagram, then TikTok scripts.
  3. Pull visuals (20 min) -- Select portfolio images for The Work and The Creative posts. Flag any gaps that need a BTS shoot or graphic.
  4. Schedule everything possible (10 min) -- Load completed posts into Mixpost or scheduling tool. Leave Reels and TikToks as drafts until filmed.

Monday Morning -- Shoot + Edit (2 hours, or fold into client session days)

  1. Shoot BTS and Reels (45 min) -- Film all video content for the week in one block. Lighting setups, studio walkthroughs, talking heads, kit bag videos. Batch them back to back.
  2. Edit video (45 min) -- Cut all Reels and TikTok clips. Export in both Instagram and TikTok formats.
  3. Create graphics (15 min) -- Any Parchment/Ink text graphics for Invitation posts or Stories.
  4. Final schedule (15 min) -- Upload remaining content to scheduler. Set publish times.

Daily (5 minutes)

  • Post 1-2 Stories from your phone. These are spontaneous -- don't overthink them.
  • Reply to comments and DMs within 4 hours during business hours.
  • Check tags and reshare any client posts that mention Power Portraits.

Monthly (First Sunday of the month, 30 minutes)

  • Choose the month's theme from the rotating list above.
  • Review last month's analytics: which posts got saves, which got shares, which fell flat.
  • Adjust the coming month's calendar based on what worked. Move more energy toward high-performing pillars.
  • Identify 2-3 clients to feature in The Creative pillar and reach out for permission.

Notes

  • No hashtag strategy included here. Maintain a separate hashtag bank and rotate sets. Avoid more than 15 per Instagram post.
  • Tagging is mandatory for all Creative pillar posts. Always tag the client, their business, and their industry hashtags.
  • Repurposing: Every Instagram Reel becomes a TikTok. Every LinkedIn post seeds an Instagram caption (shorter). Every client spotlight becomes a Google Business update the following week.
  • Photography for content: Dedicate 10 minutes at the end of every paid session to shoot 3-5 BTS frames on your phone. This is your content pipeline. No BTS footage means no Behind the Lens posts.
  • Review cadence: Revisit this calendar monthly. It is a template, not a mandate. If a pillar stops performing, replace it. If a platform stops converting, scale it back.
