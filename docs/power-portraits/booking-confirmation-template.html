What Happens Next

There are three touchpoints between now and your session, each designed to make the day feel effortless:

  1. 7 days before your session — You will receive a short questionnaire about your goals, wardrobe ideas, and the vision you have for your portraits. It takes about five minutes and helps me tailor the session to you.
  1. 48 hours before your session — Your Session Prep Guide will land in your inbox. It covers everything: what to wear, how to prepare your skin and hair, what to bring, and what to leave to us.
  1. Day of — Arrive on time with your wardrobe options. That is the only job you have. I will handle the rest.

Need to Reschedule?

Life is unpredictable. We understand. Here is how rescheduling works:

  • 48+ hours before your session — Free reschedule, subject to availability.
  • 24–48 hours before — A $75 rescheduling fee applies.
  • Less than 24 hours before — The deposit is forfeited.

If something comes up, reach out as early as you can and we will find a solution.

A Quick Note Before We Meet

The best portrait sessions happen when the person in front of the camera feels unhurried. That is exactly the atmosphere we create — no rush, no pressure, no assembly line. Just considered, deliberate work that results in images you will actually want to use.
