Last Updated: 2026-04-09

Identity

  • What: Portrait photography studio for creative professionals in South East Queensland
  • Founder: Ben Alek Conner
  • ABN: 48 890 965 530
  • Domain: powerportraitsphotography.com
  • Mission: To give creative professionals portraits that carry the weight of their ambition — images so precise, so deliberate, that they become inseparable from the person they depict.
  • Brand Promise: Every portrait we deliver is a declaration, not a photograph.
  • Location: Location-based studio model (no fixed studio yet). Shoots across Brisbane CBD, South Bank, Howard Smith Wharves, New Farm, Fortitude Valley, Botanic Gardens, etc. On-site for corporate clients.
  • Fixed Studio Trigger: Evaluate lease when consistently 20+ sessions/month for 3 months. Target areas: Fortitude Valley, Milton. Budget: $2,000-$2,500/month.
  • Revenue Status: Pre-launch (April 2026)

Brand

  • Positioning: Upper-end portrait studio for creative professionals — more intentional than generalists, more accessible than commercial studios that only work with agencies
  • Voice: Literary, unhurried, quietly commanding. Beat poet who traded the coffeehouse for a darkroom. Elegant sentences, interesting word choice.
  • Voice Rules: No "no fluff," "no BS," "real talk," "level up," "game-changer." No more than one exclamation mark per page. Em dashes and semicolons welcome.
  • Visual Reference: Sela Shiloni — monochromatic colour blocking, editorial portraiture, minimal design
  • Colours:
  • Ink #0F0F0F (primary/backgrounds)
  • Parchment #F5F0E8 (light backgrounds/cards)
  • Antique Brass #B08D57 (accent/CTAs/highlights)
  • Graphite #4A4A4A (body text)
  • Ash #9A9A9A (captions/metadata)
  • Bone #FAFAF7 (page backgrounds)
  • Typography: Cormorant Garamond (display/headlines) + Inter (body) + JetBrains Mono (pricing/data)
  • Logo: Wordmark "POWER PORTRAITS" in Cormorant Garamond SemiBold, all caps, 0.15em tracking. Antique Brass rule. "PHOTOGRAPHY" in Inter Medium below.

Target Market

Primary: Creative Professionals

Working actors, musicians, solo entrepreneurs, freelance creatives, and independent artists in South East Queensland who need portraits that match the seriousness of their ambition.

  • Building careers, not hobbies
  • Visually literate — they know the difference between a good photo and the right photo
  • Active on platforms where image matters (casting sites, Spotify, LinkedIn, Instagram, personal sites)
  • Willing to invest in quality because they understand ROI on perception
  • Avg spend: $350-$650

Secondary: Corporate Teams

Small-to-medium businesses in Brisbane/Gold Coast who want cohesive, elevated team photography.

  • Avg spend: $2,500-$5,000+

Trigger Events: New role, agency representation, album/EP release, website refresh, rebrand, speaking engagement, career pivot

Brisbane Context: 200,000+ CBD workers, growing creative/startup scene, 2032 Olympics influx

Operations

Client Journey

  1. Inquiry — Website/Instagram/LinkedIn/phone. Response within 4 business hours.
  2. Consultation Call — 15 min (optional, recommended for Portrait/Editorial)
  3. Booking — Package selection, invoice, 50% deposit confirms session
  4. Pre-Session Questionnaire — Automated email 7 days before (brand goals, wardrobe, style preferences)
  5. Session Prep Guide — Sent with booking confirmation + 48 hours before session
  6. Session Day — Ben arrives 20 min early. Welcome/rapport, Power Coaching (Editorial), continuous direction
  7. Editing & Delivery — Cull, Lightroom preset, Photoshop retouching, Pixieset gallery (password-protected, 90-day expiry)
  8. Follow-Up — 7-day check-in (review request, referral offer). 6-month re-engagement.

Retouching Standards

  • Remove blemishes/temporary imperfections, reduce (not eliminate) fine lines, maintain skin texture
  • Never over-smooth, no heavy liquify, no artificial teeth whitening beyond natural range
  • Consistent warm-neutral colour grade across all images in a gallery

Delivery Specs

  • High-res JPEG (300 DPI print + 72 DPI web-optimized)
  • Minimum 3000px on short edge
  • Multiple aspect ratios: 1:1 (LinkedIn), 4:5 (Instagram), 2:3 (portrait), 16:9 (banner/hero)
  • File naming: CLIENTLASTNAMEFIRSTNAMESESSIONDATE_001.jpg

Photography Style

  • Monochromatic colour blocking (Sela Shiloni influence)
  • Directional, sculpted light — Rembrandt, split, butterfly patterns
  • Minimal retouching — skin texture preserved
  • Negative space — compositions breathe
  • Eye contact or deliberate aversion — every gaze intentional

Financials

Revenue Targets

  • Year 1: $90,000-$123,000 (conservative-to-on-track). Target: 150-200 sessions.
  • Year 2: $175,000-$220,000
  • Year 3: $280,000-$320,000

Blended Average Revenue

  • Individual session: $530 (blended across Session/Portrait/Editorial at new pricing)
  • Corporate engagement average: $3,500

Break-Even

  • Monthly fixed costs: ~$1,990
  • Variable cost per session: ~$25
  • Break-even: 4 sessions/month
  • At 20 sessions/month: ~$8,410 monthly profit after fixed costs

Pricing Strategy

  • GST-inclusive pricing. GST itemized on invoices.
  • Annual price review each January.
  • Pricing deliberately upper-end to signal quality and attract serious creatives

Key Decisions & Clarifications

  • Power Portraits = prestigious portrait studio (commercial business). Not a side project.
  • 2 photobooks are separate personal creative work (not PP). Ben's personal art projects.
  • Location-based model first. Fixed studio only when revenue justifies it (20+ sessions/month sustained).
  • Self-hosted tools preferred — Cal.com, Twenty CRM, Mautic, Matomo. No paid SaaS for booking/CRM/email.
  • GST: Register from commencement (projecting above $75K threshold).
  • Sole trader structure (not company). Net profit taxed as personal income.
  • Cancellation Policy: 48+ hrs = free reschedule. 24-48 hrs = $75 fee. <24 hrs = deposit forfeited. No-show = full fee forfeited.
  • Gallery expiry: 90 days. Archive retrieval fee: $50.
  • Referral program: 10% discount for referred bookings. 10% commission or reciprocal promotion for active referral partners.
