Power Portraits — Client Intake Form

Sent 7 days before your session

We want to get this right. These questions help us understand your world so your session feels less like a photo shoot and more like a conversation. There are no wrong answers — just be yourself. That's the whole point.

About You

  1. Full name
  1. What do you do?

(e.g., actor, musician, freelance designer, business owner)

  1. Tell us about your work in a few sentences.

What are you known for? What are you building?

  1. Where will these portraits live? (Tick all that apply)
  • ☐ Website
  • ☐ LinkedIn
  • ☐ Instagram
  • ☐ Casting profiles
  • ☐ Spotify / Apple Music
  • ☐ Press kit
  • ☐ Business cards
  • ☐ Other: _

Your Vision

  1. How do you want people to feel when they see your portrait?

For example: "They're serious about their craft," "They're approachable," "They're someone I want to work with."

  1. Are there any portraits or photographers whose work speaks to you?

Share links or names if you have them — even a screenshot from Instagram works.

  1. Is there a mood or energy you're going for?

Some starting points: bold and editorial, warm and natural, dark and moody, clean and minimal — or something else entirely.

  1. Any colours you'd like incorporated into the shoot?

Think wardrobe, backgrounds, general palette.

Wardrobe & Styling

  1. How many looks/outfits are you planning?

This depends on your package — Session includes 1-2, Portrait includes 3, Editorial is unlimited.

  1. Describe the outfits you're considering.

Or send through photos if that's easier — a mirror selfie works perfectly.

  1. Will you need hair and makeup?

We can arrange a partnered MUA from $150. Just let us know and we'll sort it.

  1. Any accessories, props, or instruments you'd like to bring?

Practical Details

  1. Do you have a preferred location in Brisbane?

If not, we'll suggest options based on your vibe.

  1. Any physical considerations we should know about?

For example: "I have a good side," "I'm more comfortable sitting," "I wear glasses and get glare." Anything that helps us make you feel at ease.

  1. Anything you absolutely do NOT want in your photos?

For example: "No teeth showing," "Nothing too corporate," "No plain white backgrounds." We'd rather know now than guess on the day.

Final Thoughts

  1. What's the occasion for this shoot?

New role, portfolio refresh, album release, rebrand, just time for new photos — whatever it is, it helps us understand the moment you're in.

  1. Is there anything else you'd like us to know before your session?

Your answers stay between us. We use them to prepare your session — lighting, locations, direction — so that the day itself feels effortless.
