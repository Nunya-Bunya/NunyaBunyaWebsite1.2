POWER PORTRAITS — BUSINESS FOUNDATION DOCUMENT

Your Image. Your Power.

Confidential Business Document — Ben Alek Conner, Founder

1. MISSION & VISION

Mission Statement

Power Portraits exists to transform how professionals show up in the world. We create premium headshots and portraits that capture not just a face, but a presence — the confidence, authority, and authenticity that makes careers accelerate and brands unforgettable.

Vision Statement

To become Brisbane's most sought-after headshot studio for professionals who understand that their image is their most powerful business asset — and to build a studio brand recognized for consistently delivering photographs that change how clients see themselves and how the world sees them.

Core Values

  1. Excellence Without Compromise — Every image delivered meets a standard we'd be proud to display on our own wall.
  2. Client Transformation — We measure success not just in pixels, but in the confidence clients feel when they see their photos.
  3. Authenticity — We capture who you actually are at your best, not a stiff corporate cliche.
  4. Relationship-First Business — Clients are collaborators, not transactions.
  5. Continuous Craft — We invest constantly in technique, equipment, and aesthetic refinement.

2. BRAND POSITIONING & STORY

The Gap We Fill

Brisbane's photography market is split between two extremes: budget operators running factory-style headshots (poorly lit, generic backgrounds, minimal retouching) and high-end fashion/commercial photographers who charge $2,000+ for a personal brand session most professionals can't justify.

Power Portraits occupies the premium-but-accessible middle ground — delivering work that rivals the top-tier photographers, at pricing that makes sense for ambitious professionals ready to invest in their image.

Brand Story

Ben Alek Conner founded Power Portraits after recognizing a consistent pattern: talented professionals were being held back not by their skills or credentials, but by how they presented themselves online. A weak LinkedIn photo signals weakness. A powerful, professional portrait signals authority — before you've said a word.

The "Power Portrait" concept was born from the understanding that a great headshot isn't documentation — it's a statement. It says: I take myself seriously. You should too.

Positioning Statement

For Brisbane professionals who are serious about their career and brand, Power Portraits delivers premium headshots and portraits that communicate confidence, authority, and authenticity — because your image is your first impression, and first impressions set the trajectory of every opportunity that follows.

Competitive Differentiators

  • Power Coaching Element: The Executive Package includes 30 minutes of pre-session confidence and posture coaching — competitors don't offer this
  • Location Variety: We shoot in Brisbane's most compelling professional backdrops (CBD architecture, Southbank, South Bank Parklands, waterfront) — not just a studio backdrop
  • Brand Consultation: Pre-session questionnaire ensures every shoot is tailored to the client's specific professional positioning
  • Same-Day Delivery Option: Available on Corporate packages — no competitor in Brisbane currently offers this as standard
  • Premium Retouching: Natural, skin-perfecting retouching that looks polished but never plastic
  • Gallery Experience: Clients receive a curated, beautifully presented online gallery — not a Dropbox link

3. TARGET MARKET ANALYSIS

Primary Markets

3A. Brisbane Corporate Professionals

  • Who: Senior managers, department heads, C-suite executives at Brisbane's major employers (mining, resources, finance, law, consulting, property development)
  • Age Range: 32–58
  • Pain Point: Outdated or low-quality LinkedIn photos that undermine their professional stature
  • Trigger Events: Promotion, new role, career change, speaking engagement, media appearance, company rebrand
  • Average Spend: $450–$800 (Professional or Executive package)
  • Where They Are: LinkedIn, professional associations (Queensland Law Society, AICD, CPA Australia, Brisbane Chamber of Commerce)

3B. Entrepreneurs & Business Owners

  • Who: Founders, coaches, consultants, real estate agents, financial advisors, personal brand builders
  • Age Range: 28–50
  • Pain Point: Their brand image doesn't match the quality of their actual service
  • Trigger Events: Business launch, rebranding, website refresh, media pitch, speaking circuit entry
  • Average Spend: $450–$800
  • Where They Are: LinkedIn, Instagram, local networking events (BNI, Chambers of Commerce, co-working spaces)

3C. Actors & Creatives

  • Who: Professional actors, models, presenters, performers needing current headshots for agencies
  • Age Range: 18–45
  • Pain Point: Need industry-standard headshots that open doors with agencies and casting directors
  • Average Spend: $250–$450 (Essential or Professional)
  • Where They Are: Queensland Theatre Company, La Boite Theatre, Brisbane acting schools, Talent Agency contacts

3D. Corporate Teams

  • Who: HR managers and marketing directors at Brisbane companies needing consistent team headshots
  • Size: 5–100+ employees
  • Pain Point: Inconsistent headshots across teams make the "About Us" page look unprofessional; onboarding takes months to align new hires
  • Average Spend: $2,500–$10,000+ depending on team size
  • Where They Are: LinkedIn, Chamber events, referrals from existing individual clients

3E. LinkedIn-Focused Professionals

  • Who: Job seekers, career changers, mid-career professionals building their personal brand online
  • Age Range: 25–45
  • Pain Point: Know their current photo is costing them opportunities but haven't invested in a proper update
  • Trigger Event: Starting job search, platform algorithm awareness, visible peers with great headshots
  • Average Spend: $250–$450

Brisbane Market Context

  • Brisbane's CBD employment base is approximately 200,000+ workers
  • Queensland's resources and energy sector creates a constant pipeline of high-earning professionals with image needs
  • The 2032 Olympics infrastructure boom is bringing an influx of professionals, consultants, and media personalities
  • Significant growth in executive coaching, personal branding, and thought leadership culture in Brisbane since 2022

5. PRICING STRATEGY & RATIONALE

Pricing Philosophy

Power Portraits is positioned as a premium service — not the cheapest option, never the most expensive. Pricing is set to:

  1. Reflect genuine value and quality
  2. Attract serious, committed clients who respect the craft
  3. Support sustainable business growth and equipment investment
  4. Create clear package tiers that guide clients toward higher-value options

Price Anchoring

The Executive Package at $800 serves as the anchor — when clients see the full range, the Professional Package at $450 appears to be exceptional value (and it is). The Essential at $250 captures the entry-level market without undercutting brand perception.

GST Treatment

All prices are quoted GST-inclusive. GST (10%) is itemized separately on invoices for business clients claiming the input tax credit.

Deposit Policy

  • 50% deposit required at booking (non-refundable within 48 hours of session)
  • Balance due on gallery delivery
  • Corporate packages: 50% deposit on signing, 50% on delivery

Price Review Schedule

  • Annual price review each January
  • Year 2 planned increase: 10–15% across all packages as brand reputation builds
  • Corporate pricing reviewed per project

8. BOOKING PROCESS OVERVIEW

Step 1: Inquiry

Client submits inquiry via website contact form, Instagram DM, LinkedIn message, or phone. Response target: within 4 business hours.

Step 2: Consultation Call (Optional but Recommended)

15-minute call to understand the client's goals, industry, how they want to be perceived, and which package suits them. This call also serves as a rapport-builder — clients who consult before booking have higher satisfaction rates.

Step 3: Package Selection & Booking

Client selects package. Invoice sent via Xero with 50% deposit link. Session is confirmed only upon receipt of deposit.

Step 4: Pre-Session Questionnaire

Automated email sends the Pre-Session Questionnaire (what they do, brand positioning goals, wardrobe preferences, any specific requirements). Completed 48–72 hours before session.

Step 5: Booking Confirmation & Session Prep Guide

Full booking confirmation email sent with:

  • Date, time, and meeting point
  • Session Prep Guide (wardrobe, hair, skincare, mental prep)
  • Location details and parking instructions
  • Cancellation/rescheduling policy reminder

Step 6: Session Day

  • Ben arrives 20 minutes early to scout/set up
  • Client welcomed, brief rapport conversation before shooting begins
  • Power Coaching (Executive package only) before first shot
  • Consistent direction throughout — clients are guided, never left to figure it out

Step 7: Gallery Delivery

  • Images culled and retouched within agreed timeframe
  • Gallery published via Pixieset with password protection
  • Gallery delivery email sent with download instructions and usage guidelines

Step 8: Post-Session Follow-Up

  • Follow-up email 7 days after gallery delivery
  • Review request (Google Business + Trustpilot)
  • Referral encouragement (10% discount for referred bookings)
  • 6-month check-in for repeat booking opportunity
