Power Portraits — Service Packages

Photography that honours the person in front of the lens.

The Session — $350 (inc. GST)

Some portraits need to arrive quickly and land cleanly. The Session is built for precisely that — a focused hour where we capture a decisive set of images for a specific purpose. No meandering, no filler. You come in knowing what you need; you leave knowing you have it.

Ideal for actors refreshing their headshots, musicians updating press imagery, or solopreneurs stepping into something new.

Inclusions:

  • 1-hour session
  • 10 retouched images (high-res + web-optimised)
  • 2 locations
  • 1 round of retouching feedback
  • Delivery: 5–7 business days

The Portrait — $650 (inc. GST)

This is the anchor. Two hours, three locations, three wardrobe changes — enough room to explore the different registers of who you are on camera. A 15-minute style consultation before the session ensures we arrive with intention, not improvisation. The Portrait is for creatives building a visual identity that stretches across websites, social platforms, press kits, and casting profiles. It is the package most of our clients book, and for good reason: it gives you range without excess.

Inclusions:

  • 2-hour session
  • 25 retouched images (high-res + web-optimised)
  • 3 locations
  • 3 wardrobe changes
  • 15-minute style consultation (pre-session)
  • 2 rounds of retouching feedback
  • Delivery: 5–7 business days

The Editorial — $950 (inc. GST)

The definitive portrait experience. Three unhurried hours, unlimited locations, unlimited wardrobe changes — and before the camera comes out, a 30-minute Power Coaching session on confidence, posture, and presence. We also begin with a brand strategy call so that every image serves a deliberate purpose. The result is editorial-quality imagery that could sit comfortably in a magazine spread. This package is for established creatives who treat their image with the same discipline they bring to their craft.

Inclusions:

  • 3-hour session
  • 50 retouched images (high-res + web-optimised)
  • Unlimited locations and wardrobe changes
  • 30-minute Power Coaching session (confidence, posture, presence)
  • 30-minute brand strategy call (pre-session)
  • Unlimited retouching feedback
  • Priority scheduling
  • Delivery: 3–5 business days

Corporate — From $2,500 (inc. GST)

Most corporate headshots look like they were taken under fluorescent light by someone who had fifteen other things to do that morning. Ours don't. We come to your office, set up a proper studio environment, and photograph each team member with the same care we'd give an individual client — consistent styling, consistent backdrop, individually retouched images. The whole thing runs quietly and efficiently so your team barely notices the disruption.

Inclusions:

  • On-site at your office or location
  • 15–20 minutes per person
  • 3–5 retouched images per team member
  • Consistent styling and backdrop across all team members
  • Individual password-protected galleries
  • Delivery: 48–72 hours (preview option available)

What Every Package Includes

Regardless of which package you choose, the following comes standard:

  • Pre-session questionnaire — brand goals, wardrobe guidance, style preferences
  • Session Prep Guide — so you arrive confident and camera-ready
  • Professional direction throughout the entire session
  • Colour-graded, retouched images delivered via Pixieset gallery
  • High-resolution (300 DPI, print-ready) + web-optimised (72 DPI) versions of every image
  • Multiple aspect ratios: 1:1, 4:5, 2:3, 16:9
  • 90-day gallery access (archive retrieval after expiry: $50)
