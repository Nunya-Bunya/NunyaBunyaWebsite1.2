Case Study: Brisbane Zinia Massage

At a Glance

Client: Brisbane Zinia Massage

Industry: Massage Therapy & Wellness

Location: Brisbane CBD, Queensland

Services Provided: Complete brand development, website design & build, SEO setup, content strategy, AI brand photography, analytics implementation

Timeline: 3 weeks from discovery to launch

The Client

Brisbane Zinia Massage is a boutique wellness practice located in the heart of Brisbane's CBD. Led by Mary, an experienced massage therapist specialising in remedial massage, deep tissue massage, and traditional Chinese massage, the practice focuses on helping clients recover from stress, sports fatigue, and the physical demands of modern life.

Operating from Suite 2 at 117 Queen Street, Mary offers personalised treatments designed to relieve muscle tension, improve circulation, and restore balance. Her unique offering includes traditional clinic services as well as premium mobile massage services for 4-star and 5-star hotels within 2km of the CBD—available 24 hours a day.

The Challenge

When Brisbane Zinia Massage first approached Nunya Bunya, their online presence wasn't doing justice to Mary's expertise or the quality of her services. The practice faced several critical challenges:

No Professional Brand Identity: Without a cohesive brand strategy, potential clients couldn't understand what made Brisbane Zinia Massage different from dozens of other massage therapists in Brisbane.

Confusing Website Experience: The existing website lacked clear navigation, professional imagery, and compelling service descriptions. Visitors couldn't easily understand the range of services offered or how to book appointments.

Missing SEO Foundation: The site had no proper SEO structure, meaning potential clients searching for "remedial massage Brisbane CBD" or "mobile massage Brisbane" simply weren't finding Mary's services.

Poor Mobile Experience: With many clients searching for massage services on their phones, the non-responsive design was turning away bookings before they even began.

Unclear Value Proposition: The unique combination of traditional Chinese massage expertise, convenient CBD location, and premium hotel outcall services wasn't being communicated effectively.

The Solution

Nunya Bunya developed a comprehensive digital transformation strategy that positioned Brisbane Zinia Massage as the premium choice for therapeutic massage in Brisbane's CBD.

Brand Bible (BFK): We created a complete brand strategy document establishing Brisbane Zinia Massage's warm, nurturing, and professional tone. This included messaging frameworks, brand personality, and positioning against competitors.

Website Design & Build: A complete redesign focusing on user experience, clear service presentation, and conversion optimisation. The new site makes it easy for clients to understand services and book appointments.

SEO Setup: Comprehensive Schema.org markup, optimised meta tags, and keyword targeting for terms like "remedial massage Brisbane," "deep tissue massage CBD," and "mobile massage Brisbane."

Content Strategy: Development of content pillars around pain relief, wellness education, and recovery techniques, with an editorial calendar for ongoing content creation.

AI Brand Photography: Professional imagery showcasing Mary's techniques, the serene treatment environment, and the unique mobile service offering.

Analytics Setup: Matomo tracking and Meta Pixel implementation to measure website performance and support future digital marketing campaigns.

The Process

Discovery & Audit (Week 1): We conducted comprehensive research into Brisbane's massage therapy market, analysed competitor positioning, and identified key opportunities for differentiation. The audit revealed significant gaps in local SEO and mobile user experience.

Brand Development (Week 1-2): Creation of the Brand Bible established Brisbane Zinia Massage's positioning as the go-to practice for therapeutic massage that combines traditional Chinese medicine principles with modern remedial techniques.

Website Design & Build (Week 2-3): Built on a responsive framework with clear service hierarchies, intuitive booking flows, and mobile-first design principles. Special attention was paid to showcasing the unique hotel outcall service.

Content & SEO (Week 3): Implementation of targeted keyword strategies, creation of service-specific landing pages, and development of educational content that positions Mary as a trusted wellness expert.

Launch & Handover (Week 3): Comprehensive testing across devices, analytics setup, and training Mary on content management and booking system administration.

Before & After

The transformation was immediately apparent. The original website featured cluttered layouts, unclear navigation, and amateur photography that failed to convey the professional, therapeutic nature of Mary's practice.

The new Brisbane Zinia Massage website presents a clean, calming aesthetic that immediately communicates professionalism and expertise. Service pages now clearly explain each treatment type, duration options, and pricing—removing confusion and building confidence for potential clients.

The mobile experience went from practically unusable to seamless, with thumb-friendly navigation and one-touch booking capabilities. The unique hotel outcall service now has dedicated prominence, appealing to Brisbane's significant business traveller market.

Most importantly, the site now tells Mary's story effectively—establishing trust through her qualifications in traditional Chinese massage and positioning the practice as a haven for recovery and wellness in the busy CBD.

The Website

Every design decision serves Brisbane Zinia Massage's dual goals: educating potential clients about therapeutic massage benefits and making bookings as frictionless as possible.

Calming Colour Palette: Warm, neutral tones create an immediate sense of tranquillity and professionalism, reflecting the nurturing experience clients can expect.

Service-Focused Architecture: Each massage type has dedicated space explaining techniques, benefits, and ideal client scenarios. This educational approach builds trust and helps clients choose the right treatment.

Clear Pricing Structure: Transparent pricing by duration removes barriers to booking and positions the services as accessible yet professional.

Mobile-First Booking: The booking system works seamlessly on mobile devices, recognising that many clients search for massage services while experiencing pain or stress.

Hotel Outcall Prominence: The unique mobile service gets featured placement, appealing to Brisbane's business district clientele and differentiating from clinic-only competitors.

With a solid digital foundation in place, Brisbane Zinia Massage is positioned for strategic growth. Key opportunities include:

  • Google Ads campaigns targeting specific conditions like "back pain relief Brisbane" and "sports massage CBD"
  • Content marketing through wellness blog posts and recovery guides to build authority
