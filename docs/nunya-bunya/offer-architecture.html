Nunya Bunya -- Offer Architecture

Purpose: Define the current Nunya Bunya offer ladder in one practical place.

Status: Working canonical draft created on 2026-04-13.

This document resolves the current offer drift across the workspace:

  • Old model: Starter, Growth, Scale, Enterprise
  • Current model: Bedrock one-time packages + gemstone retainers

For client-facing use, prefer the current model.

Treat the old tier names as historical/internal references unless deliberately revived.

1. Offer Strategy

Nunya Bunya should not lead with "AI marketing."

It should lead with:

We build the marketing system your business should already have.

The strongest offer logic is:

  1. build the foundation
  2. prove the system works
  3. then manage and improve it monthly

That makes the ladder:

Free / Low-ticket diagnostic
        ->
Bedrock setup package
        ->
Monthly retainer
        ->
Premium growth partnership

3. Current Primary Offer

Bedrock

Bedrock should be the main near-term sales anchor.

Why:

  • easier to sell before strong case studies exist
  • concrete deliverables
  • faster proof cycle
  • natural path into retainer
  • fits the current "first paying client" phase

Suggested positioning:

Bedrock is a complete marketing foundation for Brisbane businesses that need their website, funnel, email, booking, analytics, and lead capture fixed without hiring a full agency team.

4. Bedrock Packages

Bedrock Lite

Price: $2,000 + GST

Best for: Early-stage or very small businesses that need the basics done properly.

Client gets:

  • simple website or high-converting landing page
  • lead magnet or simple opt-in asset
  • email capture setup
  • basic thank-you page
  • simple analytics/tracking
  • handoff notes

Do not include:

  • ad campaign setup
  • full CRM automation
  • full content calendar
  • deep brand strategy

Sales angle:

The fastest way to stop looking unfinished online.

Bedrock

Price: $3,500 + GST

Best for: Local service businesses that need a real marketing system, not random posts and a half-finished website.

Client gets:

  • website or refreshed website structure, usually 5-8 pages
  • landing page for one core offer or service
  • lead magnet PDF
  • email list setup
  • 5-email welcome or nurture sequence
  • booking system setup
  • Slack or email notifications for leads/bookings
  • basic CRM setup
  • analytics and tracking
  • ad copy and creative direction for launch campaign
  • handoff guide

Review rule:

All visible deliverables require review before going live.

Sales angle:

Website, landing page, email, booking, tracking, and campaign assets built as one connected system.

Bedrock Pro

Price: $8,000-$10,000 + GST

Best for: Businesses that need the full business/brand/marketing foundation, not just a website and funnel.

Client gets everything in Bedrock, plus:

  • brand strategy document
  • style guide
  • customer personas
  • messaging framework
  • content strategy
  • 90-day content calendar
  • 90 days of generated draft content
  • Mixpost scheduling setup for approved content
  • SEO keyword strategy
  • 4 blog posts
  • competitor analysis
  • printed or PDF Business Bible

Sales angle:

A complete business and marketing foundation built from one deep intake.

5. Retainers

The retainer structure is strategically useful, but the scope needs to stay simple enough to sell and deliver.

NB Onyx

Price: $3,500/mo + GST

Best for: Businesses that have a foundation and need consistent marketing management.

Suggested scope:

  • monthly strategy review
  • content calendar
  • weekly social content drafts
  • basic email/newsletter support
  • reputation/review monitoring
  • analytics dashboard
  • monthly report
  • light landing page or funnel updates
  • ongoing optimization recommendations

Not included by default:

  • full ad spend management
  • weekly video production
  • podcast production
  • major website rebuilds

Positioning:

Keep the machine running and improving.

NB Tanzanite

Price: $5,000/mo + GST

Best for: Businesses ready to actively grow through campaigns, content, and optimization.

Suggested scope:

  • everything in Onyx
  • paid ad campaign management
  • campaign landing page iterations
  • expanded content output
  • SEO content plan
  • blog/article drafts
  • conversion tracking and reporting
  • monthly campaign testing plan
  • priority strategy support

Positioning:

Turn the foundation into a growth engine.

NB Black Opal

Price: $12,000/mo + GST

Best for: Established businesses with budget, ambition, and enough operational capacity to handle growth.

Suggested scope:

  • everything in Tanzanite
  • dedicated strategist
  • video content system
  • podcast or YouTube channel support
  • premium campaign production
  • advanced reporting
  • deeper competitor and market intelligence
  • weekly review cadence
  • broader creative direction
  • custom automation and funnel builds

Positioning:

Full-stack growth partnership for businesses that want the whole machine.

6. Conversion Path

Best near-term sales path:

  1. Free audit or Business Foundation Kit
  2. Bedrock or Bedrock Lite
  3. Results documentation
  4. Retainer offer within 30 days
  5. Month 1 retainer discount if they convert

Existing rule to preserve:

  • Bedrock buyers who convert to retainer within 30 days get Month 1 at 50% off

7. What To Sell First

For first external clients

Sell:

  • Bedrock at $3,500
  • or a strategic trade/cash hybrid only if it creates proof

Avoid selling:

  • complex retainers before proof
  • too many custom add-ons
  • vague "AI marketing" services

Why

The agency needs a clear win, a clear case study, and a repeatable delivery pattern.

Bedrock is the cleanest path to that.

8. Offer Guardrails

Always say

  • We build connected systems.
  • You own your data and assets.
  • AI creates leverage, but a human reviews before anything goes live.
  • The point is not more content. The point is better customer flow.

Avoid saying

  • "No fluff"
  • "Revolutionary"
  • "Guaranteed ROI"
  • "Fully automated agency"
  • "Set and forget"

Use carefully

  • AI-powered
  • automation
  • open-source stack
  • low-cost infrastructure

These are advantages, but they should support the business outcome rather than become the whole pitch.

For the next 30-60 days, Nunya Bunya should focus on:

That gives the agency proof, cash, and a cleaner operational rhythm.
