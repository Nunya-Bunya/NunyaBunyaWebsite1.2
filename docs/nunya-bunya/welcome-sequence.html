Email 2: Your First 100 Website Visitors

Subject: How to get your first 100 website visitors (without paying for ads)

Preview Text: This 20-minute strategy works every time.

Body:

<p>Hey {{contact.firstname|default('there')}},</p>

<p>Most business owners launch a website and then... wait. Crickets. Tumbleweeds. Maybe their mum visits.</p>

<p>Here's a quick win you can execute today to start driving real traffic:</p>

<h3>The "Answer 10" Method</h3>

<ol>
  <li><strong>Open Google</strong> and type your main service + "near me" or "how to"</li>
  <li><strong>Look at "People Also Ask"</strong> — write down 10 questions</li>
  <li><strong>Create one blog post or social post answering each question</strong> — keep them short (300-500 words)</li>
  <li><strong>Post them over the next 2 weeks</strong> — one every day or two</li>
  <li><strong>Share each post</strong> in 2-3 relevant Facebook groups or on LinkedIn</li>
</ol>

<p>That's it. No ad spend. No fancy tools. Just answering the questions your future customers are already asking.</p>

<p>We've seen businesses go from zero to 100+ monthly visitors in under 30 days using this exact approach.</p>

<p><strong>Bonus tip:</strong> Use the free version of Ubersuggest or AnswerThePublic (both in your $0 Marketing Stack guide) to find even more questions to answer.</p>

<p>Tomorrow I've got one more thing for you — and it might be the most valuable email in this sequence.</p>

<p>Keep building,<br>
<strong>The Nunya Bunya Team</strong></p>
