Featured Image Prompts — March 2026

Run these through Gemini Image Gen, Midjourney, or DALL-E to create featured images for each blog post.

Week 1: AI Marketing for Law Firms

Blog title: Why Law Firms Need AI-Powered Marketing in 2026

Prompt:

A modern, clean office scene showing a confident attorney reviewing analytics on a large screen while an abstract AI visualization (subtle glowing neural network pattern) overlays the data. Warm lighting, professional atmosphere. Shot from a slight angle. No text, no logos. Color palette: deep navy, warm gold accents, white. Professional photography style, shallow depth of field.

Alternate prompt (more abstract):

Abstract digital artwork representing the intersection of law and artificial intelligence. A minimalist golden scale of justice transforming into flowing data streams and neural pathways. Dark navy background with warm gold and white accent colors. Clean, sophisticated, modern. No text.

Week 2: SEO for Personal Injury Lawyers

Blog title: 5 SEO Mistakes Personal Injury Lawyers Make

Prompt:

A personal injury attorney's desk from above, showing a laptop with a Google search results page (blurred/generic), a notepad with SEO notes, and a coffee cup. Clean, organized workspace. Natural daylight from a window. Professional flat-lay photography style. Color palette: warm neutrals, touches of blue from the screen. No faces, no text overlay.

Alternate prompt:

Split-screen concept showing two law firm websites side by side — one cluttered and outdated on the left (slightly muted/grey), one modern and clean on the right (vibrant and clear). Abstract representation, not real websites. Professional digital illustration style. Clean lines, modern design.

Week 3: Google Reviews for Law Firms

Blog title: How to Turn Google Reviews Into Your Law Firm's Best Marketing Tool

Prompt:

A warm, genuine moment of a client shaking hands with their attorney in a modern office lobby, seen from behind the client's shoulder. The attorney is smiling naturally. Soft, professional lighting. In the background, a subtle hint of a 5-star rating (very subtle, not the focus). Color palette: warm tones, professional. Photorealistic style.

Alternate prompt:

A smartphone screen showing a glowing 5-star review (generic/blurred text) floating above a professional desk surface. The phone casts a warm golden glow on the desk. Dark, moody background with shallow depth of field. Product photography style. No identifiable brand elements.

Week 4: Content Marketing for Law Firms

Blog title: The Content Marketing Playbook Every Law Firm Needs in 2026

Prompt:

An open playbook/notebook on a modern desk with a content calendar visible (hand-drawn/sketched style), surrounded by a laptop, tablet showing blog analytics, and a cup of coffee. Clean, aspirational workspace. Overhead flat-lay shot. Natural lighting. Color palette: white, navy, gold accents. Professional lifestyle photography.

Alternate prompt:

Abstract illustration of content types flowing from a central hub — blog posts, social media icons, video thumbnails, and email newsletters radiating outward in an organized, strategic pattern. Modern minimalist style with clean lines. Dark navy background, gold and white elements. No text, no real brand logos.

Social Media Image Prompts

General brand imagery (use across social posts):

  1. Authority shot: Professional headshot setup — clean backdrop, confident pose, warm lighting. Use for LinkedIn thought leadership posts.
  1. Behind the scenes: A creative workspace with multiple screens showing dashboards, analytics, and content drafts. Moody lighting with screen glow. Use for Instagram/Facebook.
  1. Results visual: A clean, modern bar chart or line graph showing upward growth trend. Abstract/stylized, not a real screenshot. Gold and navy colors. Use for case study or results posts.
  1. Community engagement: Diverse group of professionals in a modern co-working space having a collaborative discussion. Natural, candid feel. Use for Facebook community posts.
