The Two Tracks

TRACK A: "I'm Starting Fresh"

For people at the idea/planning stage or pre-revenue. They don't have customers yet, might not have a name yet, haven't validated anything. Questions are more aspirational and planning-oriented.

TRACK B: "I'm Already in Business"

For people already operating — they have (or had) customers, have revenue (or had revenue), have tried marketing. Questions pull real data, real metrics, real stories.

SHARED QUESTIONS

Both tracks share the majority of questions. The difference is:

  • Track A skips: current metrics, existing customer stories, testimonials, what's worked/failed
  • Track B skips: some planning/aspirational questions that don't apply
  • Track B adds: metrics, results, social proof, current marketing audit

Design Rules

  1. Never more than 3 textareas in a row. After every 2-3 textareas, insert a radio, checkbox, picker, or visual element.
  2. Micro-celebration after every section. Brief animated card showing what deliverables just got unlocked by their answers.
  3. Auto-save + "Continue Later" link. Saves to Supabase on every step change. Email link to resume. Essential for 90+ questions.
  4. Emotional arc: easy → personal → creative → analytical → visionary. The survey should feel like a journey from "who are you" to "where are you going."
  5. Section names should inspire, not bore. No "Operations" or "Sales & Money."
