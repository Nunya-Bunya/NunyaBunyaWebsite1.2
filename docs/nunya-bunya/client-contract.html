RECITALS

A. Nunya Bunya provides digital marketing services to businesses in Australia.

B. The Client wishes to engage Nunya Bunya to provide digital marketing services on the terms set out in this Agreement.

C. Both parties agree to be bound by the terms and conditions of this Agreement.

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

1.1 Service Tiers

Nunya Bunya offers digital marketing services across four (4) service tiers. The Client's selected tier is indicated in the Service Schedule (Schedule A) attached to this Agreement.

Starter Package — $1,500/month (+ GST)

Designed for businesses new to digital marketing who need a strong foundation. Includes:

  • Local SEO optimisation (Google Business Profile management, citation building, on-page SEO for up to 10 pages)
  • Social media management — 2 platforms (content creation, scheduling, community management — up to 12 posts per month per platform)
  • Monthly performance report
  • Dedicated account manager (email support, 1 x 30-minute strategy call per month)
  • Website health monitoring and basic technical SEO

Growth Package — $2,800/month (+ GST)

For businesses with an existing digital presence ready to accelerate growth. Includes everything in Starter, plus:

  • Expanded SEO (up to 25 pages, link building, competitor analysis, monthly keyword reporting)
  • Social media management — 3 platforms (up to 18 posts per month per platform)
  • Content creation: 4 x blog posts per month (up to 800 words each)
  • Basic paid advertising management (Google Ads or Meta Ads) — up to $2,000 ad spend managed (ad spend paid by client separately)
  • Bi-weekly strategy calls
  • Monthly reporting dashboard

Scale Package — $4,500/month (+ GST)

For growth-focused businesses ready to invest in multi-channel digital marketing. Includes everything in Growth, plus:

  • Full SEO campaign (unlimited pages, quarterly technical audit, schema markup, content strategy)
  • Social media management — 4 platforms (up to 24 posts per month per platform)
  • Content creation: 8 x blog posts or content pieces per month
  • Full paid advertising management (Google Ads + Meta Ads) — up to $5,000 ad spend managed (ad spend paid by client separately)
  • Email marketing: 4 x campaigns per month, list management and segmentation
  • Landing page creation (up to 2 per month)
  • Weekly strategy calls
  • Advanced monthly reporting with competitor benchmarking

Enterprise Package — $6,500/month (+ GST)

For established businesses requiring comprehensive, integrated digital marketing. Includes everything in Scale, plus:

  • Unlimited SEO pages and full technical SEO governance
  • Social media management — all relevant platforms, custom content calendar
  • Content creation: 12+ pieces per month (blogs, video scripts, infographics)
  • Full paid advertising management (Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads) — ad spend negotiated separately
  • CRM integration and marketing automation setup
  • Dedicated senior strategist + dedicated account manager
  • Monthly in-person or video board-level reporting
  • Priority support (4-hour response time during business hours)
  • Quarterly business review and digital strategy sessions

1.2 Service Customisation

Any services outside the scope of the Client's selected tier, as agreed in writing between both parties, will be subject to a separate Statement of Work (SOW) and additional fees.

1.3 Client Obligations

The Client agrees to:

(a) Provide timely access to all accounts, assets, and materials required for Nunya Bunya to perform the services (including but not limited to website logins, Google Ads accounts, Meta Business Manager, Google Analytics, social media accounts);

(b) Assign a designated point of contact who has authority to provide approvals on behalf of the Client;

(c) Respond to requests for approvals, content, or information within three (3) business days. Delays caused by the Client's failure to respond within this timeframe shall not constitute a breach by Nunya Bunya, and timelines will be adjusted accordingly;

(d) Provide accurate, complete, and up-to-date information about the Client's business, products, services, and target market;

(e) Notify Nunya Bunya immediately of any material changes to the Client's business, products, pricing, or services that may affect the digital marketing strategy.

1.4 Subcontracting

Nunya Bunya may engage subcontractors or contractors to assist in the delivery of services. Nunya Bunya remains responsible for the quality and delivery of all services, regardless of whether subcontractors are used.

2. FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

2.1 Monthly Retainer

The Client agrees to pay Nunya Bunya the monthly retainer fee for the selected service tier as set out in Schedule A, plus Goods and Services Tax (GST) at the rate applicable under Australian law (currently 10%).

2.2 Payment Due Date

Monthly retainer invoices will be issued on the 25th of each month for services to be provided in the following month. Payment is due on the 1st of each month.

2.3 Grace Period

A grace period of seven (7) calendar days from the due date is provided. If payment is not received within this grace period, Nunya Bunya reserves the right to:

(a) Suspend all services immediately without further notice;

(b) Apply a late payment fee of 1.5% per month on the outstanding balance (calculated from the original due date);

(c) Terminate this Agreement pursuant to clause 10.

2.4 Payment Method

Payment is to be made by direct bank transfer (EFT) to Nunya Bunya's nominated bank account as set out in each invoice, or by credit card via the payment link provided. Credit card payments may incur a processing surcharge of up to 1.5%.

2.5 Ad Spend

All advertising spend (Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads, or any other paid media) is separate from and in addition to the monthly retainer fee. The Client is responsible for funding their own advertising accounts directly. Nunya Bunya will manage these accounts on the Client's behalf but does not pay for advertising spend on behalf of the Client.

2.6 Setup Fees

A one-time setup fee may apply to new clients as outlined in Schedule A. This fee covers initial account auditing, strategy development, account configuration, and onboarding.

2.7 Price Reviews

Nunya Bunya may review and adjust its pricing with a minimum of 60 days' written notice to the Client. The Client may terminate this Agreement in accordance with clause 9.2 if they do not agree to the new pricing.

2.8 Disputed Invoices

If the Client disputes any invoice in good faith, they must notify Nunya Bunya in writing within seven (7) days of receipt of the invoice, setting out the basis of the dispute. Any undisputed amounts must be paid by the due date.

3. TERM AND COMMENCEMENT

3.1 Initial Term

This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for an initial term of three (3) months ("Initial Term"), after which it continues on a monthly rolling basis unless terminated in accordance with clause 9.

3.2 No Lock-In After Initial Term

After the Initial Term, either party may terminate this Agreement in accordance with the 30-day notice provisions in clause 9.2.

4. CANCELLATION POLICY

4.1 Notice Requirement

After the Initial Term, either party may terminate this Agreement by providing thirty (30) calendar days' written notice to the other party. Notice must be provided in writing (email to the relevant contact address constitutes written notice for these purposes).

4.2 Services During Notice Period

Services will continue to be delivered at full capacity during the 30-day notice period. The Client is liable to pay for all services delivered during this period.

4.3 Early Termination During Initial Term

If the Client terminates during the Initial Term, the Client is liable to pay the retainer fee for the remainder of the Initial Term, or the equivalent of one (1) month's retainer (whichever is greater), as a cancellation fee. This reflects Nunya Bunya's genuine pre-estimate of loss for early termination.

4.4 Immediate Termination

Either party may terminate immediately in accordance with the provisions of clause 10.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

5.1 Client Owns Content Created for Client

All content, creative assets, copy, graphics, photographs, videos, and other materials created by Nunya Bunya specifically for the Client under this Agreement ("Client Content") shall, upon full payment of all fees owing, become the property of the Client.

5.2 Pre-Existing IP

Nunya Bunya retains ownership of all intellectual property, methodologies, frameworks, processes, templates, and tools that were developed by Nunya Bunya prior to or independently of this Agreement ("Nunya Bunya IP"). The use of Nunya Bunya IP in delivering services to the Client does not transfer ownership of that IP to the Client.

5.3 Third-Party Materials

Where third-party materials (such as stock images, licensed fonts, or third-party software) are incorporated into Client Content, the relevant third-party licence terms apply. Nunya Bunya will advise the Client of any such licences and their limitations.

5.4 Portfolio Rights

Nunya Bunya reserves the right to display and reference the Client's project in Nunya Bunya's portfolio, case studies, website, and marketing materials, unless the Client provides written notice that they do not wish this work to be included in Nunya Bunya's portfolio. Such notice may be provided at any time during or after the Agreement.

5.5 Client Warranties

The Client warrants that all materials, logos, images, copy, and other content provided to Nunya Bunya for use in delivering the services do not infringe the intellectual property rights of any third party. The Client indemnifies Nunya Bunya against any claims arising from such infringement.

6. PERFORMANCE EXPECTATIONS

6.1 Good Faith Performance

Nunya Bunya will deliver all services with professional care and skill, in accordance with industry best practices, and in good faith pursuit of the Client's marketing objectives.

6.2 No Guarantee of Results

The Client acknowledges that digital marketing results are influenced by factors outside Nunya Bunya's control, including but not limited to: search engine algorithm changes, competitor activity, market conditions, website technical issues, and the quality of the Client's product or service.

Accordingly, Nunya Bunya does not guarantee:

  • Specific search engine rankings or positions for any keywords
  • Specific numbers of leads, enquiries, or sales
  • Specific return on ad spend (ROAS) figures
  • Specific increases in website traffic, followers, or engagement

6.3 Targets and Projections

Any targets, projections, or estimates provided by Nunya Bunya in proposals, presentations, or strategy documents are good-faith estimates based on available data and industry benchmarks. They are not contractual guarantees.

6.4 Reporting

Nunya Bunya will provide monthly performance reports that accurately reflect actual results. Nunya Bunya will not manipulate, misrepresent, or cherry-pick data in client reports.

6.5 Strategy Changes

Nunya Bunya reserves the right to adjust strategies, tactics, and approaches in response to performance data, algorithm changes, or market conditions. Material strategy changes will be communicated to the Client before implementation.

7. ADVERTISING ACCOUNTS AND ACCESS

7.1 Client Owns All Ad Accounts

All advertising accounts (Google Ads, Meta Business Manager, LinkedIn Campaign Manager, or any other platform) used in the delivery of services remain the property of the Client. Nunya Bunya operates as an authorised user/manager of the Client's accounts.

7.2 Account Setup

Where the Client does not have an existing advertising account, Nunya Bunya will assist the Client in establishing accounts in the Client's name and under the Client's billing information. Nunya Bunya will not operate advertising accounts in Nunya Bunya's own name on behalf of clients.

7.3 Access Management

The Client will grant Nunya Bunya the access levels required to manage the agreed services. Nunya Bunya will not use access granted by the Client for any purpose other than delivering services under this Agreement.

7.4 Access on Termination

Upon termination of this Agreement, Nunya Bunya will promptly revoke its own access to all Client accounts and provide the Client with all account credentials, historical data, and relevant assets within five (5) business days of the termination date.

8. CONFIDENTIALITY

8.1 Definition

"Confidential Information" means any non-public information relating to either party's business, operations, financial information, clients, strategy, pricing, or proprietary methods, that is disclosed by one party (the "Disclosing Party") to the other party (the "Receiving Party") in connection with this Agreement, whether disclosed in writing, verbally, or by any other means.

8.2 Obligations

The Receiving Party agrees to:

(a) Keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential;

(b) Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without the Disclosing Party's prior written consent, except to employees, contractors, or advisers who need to know it for the purpose of this Agreement and who are bound by equivalent confidentiality obligations;

(c) Use Confidential Information only for the purpose of fulfilling its obligations under this Agreement;

(d) Notify the Disclosing Party promptly if it becomes aware of any unauthorised disclosure of Confidential Information.

8.3 Exceptions

Confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that:

(a) Is or becomes publicly available through no breach of this Agreement;

(b) Was known to the Receiving Party before disclosure;

(c) Is independently developed by the Receiving Party without reference to the Confidential Information;

(d) Is required to be disclosed by law, court order, or regulatory authority (in which case the Receiving Party will give the Disclosing Party as much notice as possible before disclosure).

8.4 Survival

Confidentiality obligations survive termination of this Agreement for a period of two (2) years.

9. SOCIAL MEDIA ACCESS AND SECURITY

9.1 Secure Access Protocol

The Client agrees to provide Nunya Bunya with access to social media accounts and digital platforms using the following protocols:

(a) Where possible, access will be granted through the platform's official business access or role management system (e.g., Meta Business Manager, Google Analytics User Management) rather than sharing login credentials directly;

(b) Where direct credential sharing is unavoidable, the Client will create a dedicated login for Nunya Bunya with appropriate permissions and a unique, secure password;

(c) The Client should not share passwords via unencrypted email. Nunya Bunya recommends using a password manager or secure sharing tool.

9.2 Two-Factor Authentication

The Client acknowledges that Nunya Bunya cannot be held responsible for account breaches, lockouts, or security incidents that arise from the Client's own security practices, including the Client's two-factor authentication arrangements.

9.3 Access Revocation

Either party may request immediate revocation of access to any account at any time. Nunya Bunya will comply with access revocation requests within one (1) business day.

9.4 Data Security

Nunya Bunya will take reasonable steps to maintain the security of Client account credentials and will not share Client login information with unauthorised parties. In the event of a suspected security breach, Nunya Bunya will notify the Client immediately.

10. TERMINATION FOR CAUSE

10.1 Immediate Termination by Nunya Bunya

Nunya Bunya may terminate this Agreement immediately, without liability, if:

(a) The Client fails to pay any amounts due under this Agreement within the grace period specified in clause 2.3, and fails to remedy the non-payment within a further five (5) business days after written notice;

(b) The Client requests Nunya Bunya to produce, publish, or distribute content that is unlawful, misleading, deceptive, defamatory, discriminatory, or in breach of Australian Consumer Law or any other applicable legislation;

(c) The Client engages in conduct that, in Nunya Bunya's reasonable opinion, poses a reputational risk to Nunya Bunya;

(d) The Client provides materially false or misleading information to Nunya Bunya;

(e) The Client becomes insolvent, enters voluntary administration, or a liquidator or receiver is appointed.

10.2 Immediate Termination by Client

The Client may terminate this Agreement immediately if:

(a) Nunya Bunya materially breaches this Agreement and fails to remedy the breach within fourteen (14) days of written notice specifying the breach;

(b) Nunya Bunya becomes insolvent, enters voluntary administration, or ceases to carry on business.

10.3 Consequences of Termination

Upon termination:

(a) All amounts owing to Nunya Bunya up to the date of termination become immediately due and payable;

(b) Nunya Bunya will cease all work and return Client assets in accordance with clause 7.4;

(c) Each party will return or destroy the other's Confidential Information on request;

(d) The Client's ownership of all fully paid-for Client Content is confirmed.

11. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

11.1 Indirect Loss

To the extent permitted by law, Nunya Bunya's liability to the Client for any indirect, consequential, special, incidental, or punitive loss or damage (including but not limited to loss of profits, loss of revenue, loss of business, loss of data, or reputational harm) is excluded.

11.2 Cap on Liability

To the extent permitted by law, Nunya Bunya's total aggregate liability to the Client under or in connection with this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by the Client to Nunya Bunya in the three (3) months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim.

11.3 Australian Consumer Law

Nothing in this Agreement limits or excludes any consumer guarantee, right, or remedy that cannot be excluded under the Australian Consumer Law (Schedule 2 to the Competition and Consumer Act 2010 (Cth)).

12. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

12.1 Negotiation

If a dispute arises out of or in connection with this Agreement, the parties agree to first attempt to resolve the dispute through good-faith negotiation between senior representatives of each party. A party wishing to invoke this clause must provide written notice to the other party specifying the nature of the dispute.

12.2 Mediation

If the dispute is not resolved within fourteen (14) days of the written notice (or such longer period as the parties agree in writing), either party may refer the dispute to mediation administered by the Queensland Law Society or a mutually agreed independent mediator. The cost of mediation will be shared equally between the parties.

12.3 Litigation

If mediation fails to resolve the dispute within twenty-eight (28) days of the mediation commencing (or such longer period as the mediator recommends), either party may commence proceedings in the courts of Queensland, Australia.

12.4 Continuing Obligations

Neither party is relieved of its obligations under this Agreement while a dispute is being resolved under this clause.

13. GST

13.1 Application of GST

All fees and charges under this Agreement are stated exclusive of GST. GST will be added to all taxable supplies at the rate of 10% as required by A New Tax System (Goods and Services Tax) Act 1999 (Cth).

13.2 Tax Invoices

Nunya Bunya will issue valid tax invoices to the Client for all taxable supplies. The Client must not make payment until a valid tax invoice has been received.

13.3 GST Registration

Nunya Bunya is registered (or will be registered) for GST. The Client warrants that any ABN provided is current and correct.

14. GENERAL PROVISIONS

14.1 Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement is governed by the laws of Queensland, Australia. Both parties submit to the non-exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Queensland.

14.2 Entire Agreement

This Agreement, together with all Schedules attached, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to its subject matter and supersedes all prior agreements, representations, and understandings.

14.3 Amendments

This Agreement may only be amended by written agreement signed by both parties.

14.4 Waiver

A failure or delay by either party to exercise a right under this Agreement does not constitute a waiver of that right.

14.5 Severability

If any provision of this Agreement is found to be invalid, unenforceable, or illegal, that provision will be severed from the Agreement, and the remaining provisions will continue in full force.

14.6 Assignment

Neither party may assign or transfer its rights or obligations under this Agreement without the prior written consent of the other party (not to be unreasonably withheld). Nunya Bunya may assign this Agreement to a related body corporate without the Client's consent.

14.17 Force Majeure

Neither party is liable for delay or failure to perform obligations caused by events beyond their reasonable control (including natural disasters, pandemics, government actions, or internet infrastructure failures), provided the affected party notifies the other as soon as practicable and takes reasonable steps to resume performance.

14.8 Relationship of Parties

The parties are independent contractors. Nothing in this Agreement creates an employment, agency, joint venture, or partnership relationship.

14.9 Notices

Notices under this Agreement must be in writing and sent by email to the addresses set out at the beginning of this Agreement. Notices are deemed received on the next business day after sending.

15. SIGNATURES

By signing this Agreement, both parties confirm they have read, understood, and agree to be bound by its terms.
