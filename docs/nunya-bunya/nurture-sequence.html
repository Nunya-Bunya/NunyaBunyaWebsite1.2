Email 8 — Day 54

Subject: 3 things you can do this week to get more leads (no budget needed)

Preview: Takes about an hour total.

Hi {contactfield=firstname|there},

You don't always need to spend money to get more leads. Here are three things you can do this week — for free — that actually move the needle:

1. Update your Google Business Profile (15 min)

Add 3 new photos, update your business description, and respond to any reviews. Google rewards active profiles with higher local rankings. Most of your competitors haven't touched theirs in months.

2. Add a clear CTA to your homepage (10 min)

If someone lands on your website right now, is it obvious what they should do next? Add a button above the fold: "Book a Free Consultation" or "Get a Quote" — something specific. Generic "Contact Us" doesn't convert.

3. Post one piece of helpful content on LinkedIn or Facebook (30 min)

Not a sales pitch. Write about a common question your customers ask, or a mistake you see people making in your industry. Give the answer away for free. This builds trust faster than any ad.

Do those three things and you're already ahead of 80% of small businesses.

Want us to audit the rest? That's what we do.
