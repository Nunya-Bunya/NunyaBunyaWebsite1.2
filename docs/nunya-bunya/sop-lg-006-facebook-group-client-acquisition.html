SOP-LG-006 — Facebook Group Client Acquisition

Category: Lead Generation

Owner: Ben Conner

Created: 2026-03-23

Status: Active — Testing Phase

Purpose

Acquire marketing clients organically by providing free, genuine marketing advice in Facebook Groups where small business owners gather. No paid ads, no cold outreach — just value-first positioning that attracts inbound leads.

The Strategy

Phase 1 — Establish Presence (Week 1-2)

Goal: Be seen as a helpful expert, not a salesperson.

  1. Join 5-10 Facebook Groups where your target clients hang out
  2. Post pure value content — no links, no downloads, no CTAs to your website
  3. Answer other people's questions in the comments
  4. Post 1x/day in 2-3 different groups (rotate)
  5. Engage on other posts (comment, react) so your name becomes familiar

Rules:

  • NO links in posts during this phase
  • NO pitching, even softly
  • End posts with "Happy to answer any questions" — that's it
  • Read group rules before posting (some ban marketing agency owners)
  • Don't copy-paste identical posts across groups — tweak the opening line

Phase 2 — Introduce Lead Magnet (Week 3-4)

Goal: Start collecting emails from interested business owners.

  1. Create a lead magnet PDF (see below)
  2. Post valuable content as before, but end with:
"I put together a free [checklist/guide/template] on this — drop a comment if you want it and I'll DM it to you"
  1. When people comment, DM them: friendly message + link to download (hosted on your site with email capture)
  2. Their email goes into Mautic → tagged as "fb-group-lead"
  3. Nurture via email sequence

Why comments, not links:

  • Group admins don't flag comment-based engagement
  • It creates visible social proof (20 people commenting "yes please!")
  • You build a DM relationship which converts better than cold traffic

Phase 3 — Convert (Week 5+)

Goal: Turn engaged followers into discovery calls.

  1. People who engaged with your content will start DMing you with questions
  2. Answer genuinely, then: "Want me to take a proper look at your setup? Happy to do a free 15-min audit"
  3. On the call: identify 2-3 quick wins, then pitch your package
  4. Send proposal (use 90-day roadmap template)
