CONTENT CREATION WORKFLOW

YouTube Batch Day (every other Saturday)

  1. Film YouTube video (30-60 min of filming)
  2. Film 3 shorts/reels back to back (15 min each)
  3. Take any photos needed for the week

YouTube Edit Day (every other Sunday)

  1. Edit YouTube video
  2. Edit 3 shorts
  3. Upload everything
  4. Schedule the week's text posts in Buffer

Off-Week Saturday/Sunday

  1. Film and edit shorts only
  2. Batch schedule posts for the week
  3. Use extra time for business work, creative projects, or rest

Daily (15-30 min)

  1. Post the day's scheduled content (or let Buffer do it)
  2. Comment on 5 LinkedIn posts
  3. Check engagement on yesterday's posts
  4. Respond to any DMs or comments
