4. ORCA Film Awards

Business: Film awards body + film news/review publication + YouTube show

Target Market: Film enthusiasts, cinephiles, critics, and people who care about underseen/indie/international cinema

What ORCA is NOT: A film festival. Does NOT accept submissions from filmmakers. Members nominate and vote on existing films.

Differentiator: An alternative awards voice championing underseen, indie, and international films that the Oscars overlook. Operates through film criticism, essays, and a growing body of voting members.

Trajectory: Voting body → Film news/review publication → Press credentials + festival coverage → Full awards ceremony

8. Cross-Business Synergies

These are keyword clusters where multiple businesses can support each other.

Nunya Bunya + Power Portraits

  • "personal branding package brisbane" -- Nunya Bunya markets, Power Portraits delivers the photography component
  • "business brand photoshoot brisbane" -- cross-promote between both sites
  • Content synergy: Nunya Bunya blog posts about personal branding link to Power Portraits for photo services

Nunya Bunya + Law Firms (M&C, Conner Injury Law, MBCS)

  • Nunya Bunya uses the law firms as case studies for "law firm marketing brisbane"
  • Keyword target: "law firm SEO brisbane", "legal marketing agency brisbane"
  • Content: "How We Grew a Brisbane Law Firm's Leads by X% with AI Marketing"

Ben Alek Conner + Nunya Bunya

  • YouTube content about AI marketing directly feeds Nunya Bunya authority
  • Keyword target: "AI marketing agency owner", "marketing entrepreneur australia"
  • Content: Ben creates behind-the-scenes content about building Nunya Bunya

ORCA Film Awards + Ben Alek Conner

  • Ben's personal brand as filmmaker drives awareness to ORCA
  • Keyword targets: "filmmaker brisbane", "independent film australia"
  • Content: Ben's filmmaking journey content links to ORCA submissions

All Law Firms -- Content Coordination

To avoid keyword cannibalization between the three firms:

  • Merkel & Conner: Own general PI terms + car accidents + workers comp
  • Conner Injury Law: Own niche injury types (motorcycle, rideshare, catastrophic)
  • MBCS: Own non-PI practice areas (family, property, employment, wills)
  • Each firm targets different suburbs/regions where possible
