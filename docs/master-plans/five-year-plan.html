Strategic Principles

  1. Nunya Bunya funds everything. Every other venture gets de-risked by agency revenue. Never starve the agency to feed a passion project.
  1. Content is the connective tissue. YouTube, podcast, social — everything you do becomes content. The bar opening, the film set, the portrait sessions, the agency wins. Document everything.
  1. Each business feeds the others. Nunya Bunya markets all the businesses. Power Portraits shoots headshots for Nunya clients. The YouTube channel promotes ORCA. The bar hosts ORCA screenings. Conner Productions creates Nunya Bunya case study videos. Nunya Bunya clients give you directing reps for your reel. Build the flywheel.
  1. Nunya Bunya is your directing launchpad. Every branded video you produce for a Nunya client is a reel piece. You're not waiting for permission to direct — you're already doing it. The agency gives you budgets, clients, and creative briefs that most aspiring directors would kill for. Use it.
  1. People before scale. You can't do this alone. Year 1 is about proving you can deliver. Year 2+ is about hiring people who are better than you at specific things.
  1. Protect your health. None of this happens if you burn out. ADHD management, fitness, and mental health aren't side quests — they're prerequisites.

How to Use This Plan

  • Quarterly: Review the current year section. Are you on track? What needs to shift?
  • Monthly: Update GOALS.md with specific monthly targets pulled from this plan.
  • Weekly: Your ACTIONS files should trace back to items in this plan.
  • When overwhelmed: Come back to the Strategic Principles. Nunya Bunya funds everything. Content connects everything. You don't have to do it all today.
