Sales Funnel Build -- All Businesses

🚀 Master Plans MASTER PLANS

Sales Funnel Build -- All Businesses

Created: 2026-03-28

Goal: 2 ads, 2 offers, 2 lead magnets, 2 landing pages, 2 email drip sequences per business. All connected to Slack notifications, all links working, clear pipeline to purchase or book a call.

Tech Stack:

  • Landing pages: HTML hosted on each business's site
  • Email drips: Mautic (self-hosted)
  • Booking: Cal.com
  • CRM: Twenty (self-hosted)
  • Slack notifications: NunyaBunya workspace webhooks
  • Link tracking: Shlink (self-hosted)
  • Analytics: Matomo (self-hosted)

Pipeline Flow (Same for Every Business)

Ad (Facebook/Google/LinkedIn)
  --> Landing Page (offer + lead magnet)
    --> Form submission triggers:
        1. Mautic adds contact + starts drip sequence
        2. Slack notification (#channel for that business)
        3. Twenty CRM creates lead
    --> Email Drip (5-7 emails over 14 days)
        --> CTA: Book a call (Cal.com) or Purchase
          --> Slack notification on booking/purchase
