METHODOLOGY

These projections use the conservative targets from the Source of Truth, not the business plan's more aggressive numbers. The business plan projects $78K-$148K in Year 1; these projections use $40K-$60K. The intent is to set targets Ben is likely to overshoot rather than chase numbers that create pressure and distortion.

All figures are in AUD.

KEY ASSUMPTIONS

  1. YouTube growth follows a typical business-niche trajectory (slow start, compounding after 10K subs).
  2. Course revenue depends on email list size — projections assume 2K subs Year 1, 8K Year 2, 20K Year 3.
  3. Coaching rates start low and increase as brand authority grows.
  4. Brand sponsorships do not begin until audience exceeds 5K YouTube subscribers.
  5. Film revenue is highly variable and treated as upside, not baseline.
  6. All team costs are contractor-based (no employee overhead, superannuation, or payroll tax).
  7. Ben's personal living expenses are covered separately and not included here.
  8. No external funding or debt is planned. Losses in Year 1 are self-funded.
