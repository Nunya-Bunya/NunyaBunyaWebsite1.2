YOUR BRAND STORY (IN BRIEF)

The Elevator Pitch:

"I'm Ben Alek Conner. I build multiple profitable businesses—agencies, law firms, digital products—while also making feature films and documenting the journey. My goal is to show other entrepreneurs that it's possible to be multi-passionate and successful, while maintaining health and sanity. I share the real numbers, the failures, and the systems that work."

The Long Form (2 min):

"Three years ago, I was burned out, overweight, and smoking. I decided to change everything. I started documenting my journey—building businesses, losing weight, creating films, managing ADHD—all while trying to build authentic systems that actually work.

Today, I run multiple profitable businesses (Nunya Bunya is at $35K MRR, Conner Law Group spans three firms, Power Portraits does $150K annually). I've also written and am producing my first feature film.

But more than the wins, I want to share the messy reality: the failures, the mental health challenges, the systems that took months to build, the pivot points that changed everything.

My mission is simple: Show other entrepreneurs that you can build multiple businesses, create meaningful art, transform your health, and maintain your sanity—if you build the right systems and stay honest about the journey."
