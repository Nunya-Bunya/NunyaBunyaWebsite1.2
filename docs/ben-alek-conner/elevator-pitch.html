Ben Alek Conner — Elevator Pitches

Version 1.0 | April 2026

10-SECOND PITCH

"I'm Ben Alek Conner — I build businesses, make films, and document the whole thing publicly so other entrepreneurs can see how it actually works."

30-SECOND PITCH

"I'm Ben Alek Conner, a Brisbane-based entrepreneur running multiple businesses — a digital marketing agency, a law firm, a portrait studio — while producing my first feature film. I document the entire journey on YouTube with real numbers, real failures, and real systems. My goal is to show other multi-passionate people that you can pursue all of it without pretending it's easy. No guru nonsense — just what's actually working and what isn't."

60-SECOND PITCH

"I'm Ben Alek Conner. I run a digital marketing agency, a personal injury law firm, and a portrait studio in Brisbane. I'm also producing my first feature film and training for a marathon. Three years ago I was burned out, overweight, and smoking. I decided to rebuild everything — my health, my businesses, my creative ambitions — and I started documenting the process publicly.

Now I share the full journey on YouTube, a weekly newsletter, and soon a podcast. I show the revenue numbers, the systems, the ADHD management strategies, the weight loss — all of it. Not the highlight reel. The actual process.

I do this because I spent years looking for someone who was building multiple things at once and being honest about what that looks like. I couldn't find them, so I became that person. Through courses, coaching, and content, I help other multi-passionate entrepreneurs build systems that let them pursue everything they care about — without the burnout and without the fake guru energy."

USAGE NOTES

  • Networking events / introductions: Use the 10-second version. Let curiosity do the work.
  • Podcast guest appearances / panel introductions: Use the 30-second version. Gives enough context for the host to riff.
  • Speaking engagement bios / investor meetings / brand deal pitches: Use the 60-second version. Establishes credibility and differentiator.
  • Adapt freely. These are starting points, not scripts to memorise verbatim. The key beats to hit every time: multi-passionate, Australian, building in public, real numbers, no hype.
