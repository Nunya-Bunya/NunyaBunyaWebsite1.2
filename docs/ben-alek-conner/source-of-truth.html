Ben Alek Conner — Source of Truth

Last Updated: 2026-04-05

Identity

  • What it is: Personal brand and media business built around YouTube, podcast, courses, speaking, and film — separate from (but complementary to) Nunya Bunya and other portfolio businesses.
  • Mission: Show entrepreneurs that you can build multiple businesses, create meaningful art, transform your health, and maintain sanity — by sharing the journey transparently with real numbers.
  • Vision: Become the leading Australian voice in the "build in public" entrepreneurship space. Conservative target: $350-600K annual revenue by Year 3.
  • Location: Brisbane, Australia.
  • Revenue status: Pre-revenue (Foundation Year — 2026). YouTube channel not yet monetised.

Brand

  • Positioning: "Building multiple profitable businesses while creating films and sharing the journey."
  • Elevator pitch: Multi-passionate Australian entrepreneur documenting the real process of running agencies, making feature films, managing ADHD, and transforming his health — all in public.
  • Tone of voice: Honest, systems-oriented, action-focused, growth-minded. Australian sensibility — unpretentious, dry humour, no hype. Shares struggles alongside wins. No guru energy.
  • Brand pillars: Builder, Transparent, Multi-Passionate, Systemiser, Growth-Focused.
  • Brand colours: Dark/black base, gold accent (#C9A84C). Montserrat headings, Inter body text.

Key Decisions & Clarifications

  • Ben Alek Conner IS: A personal brand and media business (YouTube + podcast + courses + speaking + film). It generates its own revenue independently.
  • Ben Alek Conner IS NOT: A side project or hobby channel. Not just a marketing vehicle for Nunya Bunya.
  • Conner Productions (name TBD) is a separate entity — Ben's personal production company for features, documentaries, game shows, podcast, and ORCA Film Awards show. BAC the brand documents the journey; Conner Productions makes the creative works.
  • YouTube cadence: 2 long-form videos per month (NOT 1/week or 2/week as some documents suggest). This was a deliberate decision to prioritise quality over volume.
  • No paid SaaS for core ops — self-hosted stack (Mautic, Twenty CRM, Matomo, Metabase) per Nunya Bunya's tech philosophy. Course platform is the exception (Kajabi/Teachable).
  • Ethical sponsorship rule: Only promote products/services actually used. No misleading claims. Transparent about paid partnerships.
  • Nunya Bunya cross-promotion: BAC content drives leads to the agency, but this is tracked as agency revenue, not personal brand revenue.
