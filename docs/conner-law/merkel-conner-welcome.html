Subject: How we helped a family get back on their feet

Preview Text: After a rear-end collision left her unable to work, Sarah didn't know where to turn.

Body:

<p>Dear {{contact.firstname|default('Friend')}},</p>

<p>We'd like to share a story that shows why we do what we do.</p>

<p>A client — we'll call her Sarah — was rear-ended at a stoplight on her way home from work. The other driver was texting. Sarah suffered a herniated disc and severe whiplash that kept her out of work for months.</p>

<p>The insurance company offered her a settlement that barely covered her medical bills. Nothing for lost wages. Nothing for pain and suffering. Nothing for the months of physical therapy ahead.</p>

<p><strong>Sarah came to Merkel & Conner.</strong></p>

<p>We investigated the accident, gathered evidence, worked with her medical team, and built a case that reflected the true impact on her life. The result: a settlement that covered all of her medical expenses, lost income, and compensated her for what she'd been through.</p>

<p>Sarah's story isn't unique. Insurance companies count on people not knowing their rights. That's exactly why we're here — to level the playing field.</p>

<p><strong>Key takeaway:</strong> Never accept an insurance company's first offer without consulting an attorney. That first offer is almost always far less than what you're entitled to.</p>

<p>If you or someone you love has been injured, we're always available for a free, confidential conversation about your options.</p>

<strong>The Merkel & Conner Team</strong></p>
