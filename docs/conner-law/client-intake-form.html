SOLICITOR NOTE — LIMITATION PERIODS: Under the Limitation of Actions Act 1974 (Qld), the general limitation period for personal injury claims is three (3) years from the date the cause of action arose (generally the date of injury). However, WorkCover Queensland claims and MAIC / CTP claims have different and in some cases shorter limitation periods. The Workers' Compensation and Rehabilitation Act 2003 (Qld) imposes strict notice and claim timeframes. Confirm the applicable limitation period for each matter immediately at intake and record the expiry date prominently on the file.
