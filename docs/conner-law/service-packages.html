Legal Services Pricing and Offerings

SERVICE MODEL

Conner Law Group offers three pricing/service models:

  1. Hourly Billing - Traditional rate-per-hour
  2. Flat Fee - Fixed price for specific services
  3. Contingency - Client pays only if we win/settle

FAQ - LEGAL SERVICES

Q: How much will my case cost?

A: Depends on your case type. Personal injury = mostly contingency. Business law = hourly or flat fee. We'll discuss all options in your consultation.

Q: Will you guarantee a win?

A: No attorney can guarantee outcomes, but we honestly assess case strength and won't take cases we don't believe in.

Q: How long will my case take?

A: Personal injury typically 3-6 months. Business law varies (contracts can be weeks, litigation can be years). We'll give honest timelines.

Q: What if I can't afford a lawyer?

A: If your case has good value, we'll take it on contingency (you pay nothing upfront). Contact us for free consultation.

Q: Can I call or text with questions?

A: Yes. Retainer clients have priority access. Other clients reach out via email or scheduled calls.
