Conner Law Group -- Source of Truth

Last Updated: 2026-04-05

Identity

  • What it is: A FUTURE rebrand that will unify two existing PI law firms (both owned by Kevin Conner) under one brand, plus an estate planning firm and a legal tech SaaS product
  • Current reality: Two separate PI firms already operate — Conner Injury Law (WA) and Merkel & Conner (OR). "Conner Law Group" is the planned umbrella rebrand. Ben is preparing all branding/marketing so it's ready to implement when Kevin is ready
  • Mission: Get as much money from insurance companies as humanly possible for injured people. We know all their tricks and we live to make them pay
  • Vision: The most aggressive, client-first PI brand in the Pacific Northwest — one unified identity instead of two separate firms doing the same work
  • Brand ethos: We fucking hate insurance companies. They are scum. Every dollar we extract from them is a win. This is the core of our marketing, our culture, and our identity. We are on the side of the injured, always
  • Owner: Kevin Conner (both firms). Ben builds the brand/marketing/tech
  • Location: Washington State (Conner Injury Law) and Oregon (Merkel & Conner)
  • Rebrand rationale: One brand with the same anti-insurance energy is better than doing twice the marketing work for two separate brands that do the same thing
  • Revenue status: Pre-revenue for new marketing/rebrand. Existing firms are operational

Brand

  • Positioning: Ruthless against insurers, compassionate with clients. We fight harder and dirtier than anyone because we genuinely hate what insurance companies do to injured people
  • Tagline candidates: "Justice Through Excellence" / "Legal Excellence. Client Results."
  • Tone of voice: Aggressive toward insurers, warm and empathetic with clients. Direct, no-bullshit, confident. Plain English over legal jargon. Marketing channels the rage people feel when insurance companies lowball them
  • Primary colours: Law Black #1A1A1A, Justice Blue #1E4D7B, Accent Gold #B8860B, White #FFFFFF
  • Secondary colours: Confident Green #2DBB4E, Alert Red #C7254E, Light Gray #F5F5F5
  • Typography: Headlines: Georgia (serif). Body: Open Sans (sans-serif)

Sub-Entities

1. Conner Injury Law (Washington State) -- Personal Injury

  • Also known as: Kevin Conner Law
  • What: Plaintiff-side personal injury firm. Existing, operational firm
  • Practice areas: Motor vehicle accidents, workplace injuries, public liability, medical negligence, disability and income protection claims
  • Fee model: No win, no fee (contingency). 25% standard, 25-33% complex/high-value
  • Target market: Adults injured in Washington State. Referred by doctors, physios, orthopaedic surgeons, rehab providers
  • Revenue target: $360K (FY26) -> $720K (FY27) -> $1.2M (FY28)
  • Key metric: 60-100 active files, avg settlement $80K-$180K
  • Site: 01-Conner Law Group/Site/ (HTML/CSS, deployed on Vercel)

2. Merkel & Conner (Oregon) -- Personal Injury

  • What: Plaintiff-side personal injury firm. Existing, operational firm. Same owner (Kevin Conner), same work, different state
  • Practice areas: Same as Conner Injury Law — motor vehicle accidents, workplace injuries, public liability, medical negligence
  • Fee model: No win, no fee (contingency). 25% standard, 25-33% complex/high-value
  • Target market: Adults injured in Oregon
  • Revenue target: $240K (FY26) -> $480K (FY27) -> $780K (FY28)
  • Key metric: 30 active clients by end FY26, 80+ by FY28

3. MBCS -- Estate Planning Law Firm

  • What: Friendly estate planning law firm. Owned by Conner Law Group but COMPLETELY different brand energy — warm, approachable, no anti-insurance teeth
  • Full name: McGinty, Conner, Belcher, Smith (per brand guidelines)
  • Marketing tone: Friendly, helpful, trust-focused. The opposite of CLG's aggressive PI energy. Keep branding and marketing SEPARATE from the PI firms
  • Revenue target: $180K (FY26) -> $360K (FY27) -> $600K (FY28)
  • Key metric: 10 external firm clients by end FY26, 25+ by FY28
  • Assets: 01-Conner Law Group/4. MBCS/ (Brand Bible, Elevator Pitch, Launch Content, Roadmaps, Social Media)

4. KevLaw AI -- Legal Technology SaaS

  • What: AI-powered document automation platform for law firms. Built internally, now being productised for external sale
  • Core features: Document automation (demand letters, retainers, correspondence), client intake automation, AI legal research, compliance tracker, precedent library management
  • Fee model: SaaS subscription -- Starter $99/mo (1 user), Professional $299/mo (5 users), Growth Firm $499/mo (15 users), Enterprise POA. 15% annual discount. 14-day free trial
  • Target market: Small-to-mid law firms (1-50 lawyers) nationally (US), especially PI/workers comp with high doc volume
  • Revenue target: $30K (FY26) -> $240K (FY27) -> $800K (FY28)
  • Key metric: 50 subscribing firms by end FY27, 200+ by FY28
  • Existing app: 01-Conner Law Group/1. KevLaw AI/App/demand-letter-system/ (Node/SQLite)
  • Dashboard: 01-Conner Law Group/1. KevLaw AI/kevlaw-ai-dashboard/ (React/TypeScript)
  • Demand drafts: 01-Conner Law Group/1. KevLaw AI/Demand Drafts 1/ and Demands/

  • Both existing firms are PI. Conner Injury Law (WA) and Merkel & Conner (OR) both do personal injury, same owner, same anti-insurance mission. The old docs describing Merkel & Conner as "commercial/business law" are WRONG
  • MBCS = estate planning law firm. Friendly, warm, approachable. Completely separate brand energy from the PI firms. Do NOT apply CLG's aggressive anti-insurance tone to MBCS marketing
  • Kevin Conner owns both PI firms. Ben does not own the law firms — Ben builds the brand, marketing, and tech (KevLaw AI)
  • KevLaw AI was built internally first for Kevin's PI practice, then being productised for external sale. It is NOT a third-party tool -- it is proprietary
  • KevLaw AI is also a potential standalone SaaS business (noted in workspace memory as having potential beyond internal use)
  • Both PI firms are plaintiff-side only -- do not represent insurers or defendants. We are the enemy of every insurance company
  • No-win, no-fee model for all PI work (contingency fee agreements)
  • Pacific Northwest strategy -- deep local court relationships and practitioner networks in WA/OR before further expansion
  • Existing demand-letter-system app lives at 01-Conner Law Group/1. KevLaw AI/App/demand-letter-system/ (Node.js + SQLite)
  • Website (Conner Injury Law) is live on Vercel: 01-Conner Law Group/Site/ (static HTML/CSS)
