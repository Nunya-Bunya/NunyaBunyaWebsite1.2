ELEVATOR PITCHES

Conner Law Group — All Entities

Version 2.0 | March 2026

These pitches are living documents. Test them in real conversations, refine what lands, and update here. The best pitch is the one that sounds natural when spoken — practise until it does. Every version below is intended to be used immediately, without modification.

PART ONE: CONNER LAW GROUP (UMBRELLA BRAND)

10-Second Version

"Conner Law Group is a Brisbane legal services group — we run three specialist law firms covering personal injury, business law, and medical-legal support, plus KevLaw AI, our legal technology product for other law firms."

30-Second Version

"Conner Law Group is a Brisbane-based legal services group built differently from the typical law firm. We have three specialist legal practices — Conner Injury Law for personal injury claims, Merkel & Conner for commercial and business law, and MBCS for medical-legal consulting. And we've built KevLaw AI — an AI-powered document automation platform that we use internally and now license to other law firms nationally. We're not trying to be the biggest firm in Queensland. We're building the most integrated, most efficient, and most client-focused legal group in the country."

60-Second Version

"Conner Law Group is a Brisbane-based legal services umbrella comprising four distinct businesses. On the legal services side, Conner Injury Law handles plaintiff-side personal injury work — motor vehicle accidents, workplace injuries, public liability claims — all on a no-win, no-fee basis. Merkel & Conner is our commercial and business law practice, serving Queensland SMEs and founders with contracts, corporate structuring, employment law, and commercial disputes. And MBCS is our medical-legal consulting and support service — we coordinate expert reports, independent medical examinations, and file preparation for law firms that need that capability but don't have it in-house.

What makes the group genuinely unique is KevLaw AI. We built a proprietary legal technology platform — document automation, client intake, compliance tracking, precedent management — originally for our own use. It's cut our internal document drafting time by over seventy percent. And now we're licensing it to other law firms nationally on a SaaS subscription model.

So we're running a profitable law practice while simultaneously building a scalable technology business. Multi-million dollar revenue target, Brisbane-based, and growing fast."

PART TWO: CONNER INJURY LAW (Personal Injury)

10-Second Version

"Conner Injury Law is a Brisbane personal injury firm. Car accidents, workplace injuries, public liability claims — no win, no fee, no jargon."

30-Second Version

"Conner Injury Law is a Brisbane personal injury law firm built for people who've never hired a lawyer before. We handle motor vehicle accident claims, WorkCover injuries, and public liability claims — slip and falls, injuries in public places — all on a no-win, no-fee basis. We communicate in plain English, we keep our clients informed at every step, and we don't disappear once the file is opened. Most of our clients come to us not knowing what their claim is worth. They leave with real money in their pocket."

60-Second Version

We handle motor vehicle accident claims under Queensland's CTP scheme, workplace injury and WorkCover claims, and public liability matters — injuries in shopping centres, car parks, restaurants, and public spaces. All of our personal injury work is handled on a no-win, no-fee basis, which means there is genuinely no financial risk to calling us and having a conversation about what happened to you.

What separates us from the big national firms is attention. Our clients get a direct line to the solicitor on their matter, regular updates they can actually understand, and a team that is genuinely invested in their outcome — not just moving files through a system.

If you've been injured in Queensland in the past three years, you likely have a claim worth pursuing. The only thing it costs you to find out is a phone call."

If you run a business in Queensland and you don't have a trusted legal partner, that's a risk you're probably not thinking about until something goes wrong. We'd like to change that."

10-Second Version

"KevLaw AI is a document automation platform for law firms — retainers, demand letters, client correspondence, first draft in under sixty seconds."

30-Second Version

"KevLaw AI is an AI-powered legal document automation tool built by lawyers, for law firms. You input the key details of a matter and it produces a professional, jurisdiction-compliant first draft — retainer agreements, letters of demand, settlement correspondence, client update letters — in under a minute. We built it for our own personal injury practice at Conner Law Group and it cut our document drafting time by over seventy percent. We're now making it available to other firms on a monthly subscription starting at ninety-nine dollars. If your team spends more than a few hours a week on standard document drafting, it pays for itself in the first week."

60-Second Version

"KevLaw AI is a legal document automation platform — and the pitch is simple. A huge percentage of what a solicitor or paralegal does each week involves drafting documents that follow predictable formats. Retainer agreements. Letters of demand. Settlement offers. Client update letters. Notices of claim. These aren't complex — they're high-volume, detail-critical, and time-consuming. Every firm I've spoken to agrees this is one of the biggest drains on fee-earner capacity.

KevLaw AI solves that. You enter the key variables — client name, matter type, incident details, claim value, key facts — and the platform generates a professional first draft in under sixty seconds. Your solicitor reviews, edits if needed, and sends. What used to be a forty-five minute task becomes five minutes.

We built it originally for Conner Law Group's Queensland personal injury practice. After running it internally for several months, the efficiency gain is significant enough that we knew we had to build it for the broader market.

We're currently in a select beta with Queensland law firms, with pricing starting at ninety-nine dollars a month for sole practitioners. If you're a practice principal and this sounds like a problem worth solving, I'd like to get you into the beta."

USAGE NOTES & DELIVERY PRINCIPLES

When to Use Each Version

  • 10-second: At events when someone asks "what do you do?" — opens the conversation, prompts a follow-up question
  • 30-second: Networking events, formal introductions, podcast intros, referral partner meetings
  • 60-second: Investor conversations, conference presentations, formal pitches, business development meetings with high-value targets

Delivery Principles

  • Speak conversationally — do not read word for word from any of these scripts
  • Pause after a strong line; let it land before continuing
  • End with an invitation, not a sales push — open a door, do not close a deal
  • Adapt language to your audience: use "motor vehicle accident" with lawyers; "car accident" with everyday clients; "personal injury practice" with referrers; "document automation" with tech-aware audiences
  • Make eye contact; slow down on specifics (numbers, names, stats)

Opening Dialogue Prompts (After the Pitch)

  • "Do you work with a lot of small businesses that might not have strong legal support in place?"
  • "Do you know many people who've been through a personal injury claim?"
  • "How much time does your team currently spend on document drafting?"
  • "Have you had any experience with personal injury work yourself?"

Words to Avoid

  • "Lawyer" (use "solicitor" in professional contexts)
  • "Lawsuit" (use "claim" or "proceedings")
  • "Win big" or "massive payout" (use "maximum entitlement" or "full compensation")
  • "Cutting-edge" / "revolutionary" / "game-changing" — show, don't tell
