CONTENT GOVERNANCE & WORKFLOW

Approval Process

  1. Marketing Manager drafts all post copy and identifies imagery — minimum 5 business days before scheduled publish date
  2. All posts shared to Principal (Ben Alek Conner) for approval — minimum 48 hours before scheduled publish
  3. Any post referencing specific legal rights, claim types, legislation, or legal processes to be reviewed by the responsible solicitor before publication
  4. Client testimonials and anonymised outcomes reviewed for compliance with Queensland Law Society Solicitors' Advertising Guidelines before publication
  5. All published posts saved to the shared marketing drive with date, platform, engagement metrics recorded monthly

Legal Compliance Standards

  • All testimonials and client stories are anonymised in full — no identifying details, no specific settlement figures without legal approval
  • All posts containing legal information include: "This information is general in nature and does not constitute legal advice. Please contact us for advice specific to your circumstances."
  • No guarantees of outcomes in any post or graphic
  • KevLaw AI posts include: "KevLaw AI generates first-draft documents for review by a qualified legal practitioner. Outputs do not constitute legal advice."
  • All advertising complies with the Queensland Law Society Solicitors' Advertising Guidelines

Image & Video Standards

  • All images from the approved Conner Law Group brand asset library, licensed stock (Unsplash/Getty/Pexels — license verified), or original brand photography
  • No image used without confirmed license or original creation
  • Video: minimum iPhone 13 quality, 1080p; fully captioned via Kapwing or Rev; exported at correct platform dimensions
  • All post graphics designed in Canva (Conner Law Group shared workspace) using brand templates
