ORCA Film Awards — Welcome Email Sequence

3-email welcome sequence for new subscribers who downloaded the "50 Underseen Films" guide.
Platform: Mautic | Delay: Email 1 = immediate, Email 2 = Day 3, Email 3 = Day 6

Email 1: Your Film Guide Is Here

Subject: 50 films you probably haven't seen (but absolutely should)

Preview Text: Your curated guide to the most underseen cinema is ready. Welcome to ORCA.

Body:

<p>Hey {{contact.firstname|default('there')}},</p>

<p>Welcome to the ORCA Film Awards community — a growing collective of cinephiles who believe the best films don't always get the spotlight they deserve.</p>

<p>Here's your <strong>50 Underseen Films You Need to Watch</strong> guide — hand-curated by the ORCA team:</p>

<p style="text-align: center; margin: 30px 0;">
  <a href="{{lead_magnet_url}}" style="background-color: #0D1B2A; color: #D4AF37; padding: 14px 28px; text-decoration: none; border-radius: 6px; font-weight: bold;">Download Your Film Guide</a>
</p>

<p>A bit about us — the <strong>ORCA Film Awards</strong> exists to champion the films that the Oscars overlook. We're not a festival. We don't accept submissions. Instead, we nominate and celebrate underseen, foreign, niche, and independent films through film criticism and community voting.</p>

<p>What makes ORCA different:</p>

<ul>
  <li>We spotlight films the mainstream ignores — foreign gems, indie breakthroughs, genre films that got snubbed</li>
  <li>Our awards are decided by a volunteer community of real cinephiles, not industry insiders</li>
  <li>We publish film essays and criticism that go deeper than a star rating</li>
</ul>

<p>Over the next week, we'll share some of our favourite discoveries and tell you how you can get involved.</p>

<p>Glad you're here,<br>
<strong>The ORCA Film Awards Team</strong></p>

Email 2: Why These Films Matter

Subject: The films nobody talks about (but everyone should)

Preview Text: Why we started ORCA — and the films that inspired it all.

Body:

<p>Hey {{contact.firstname|default('there')}},</p>

<p>Every year, thousands of incredible films get made. A handful get Oscar nominations. The rest? They disappear.</p>

<p>That's why ORCA exists.</p>

<p>Here are a few discoveries that remind us why this matters:</p>

<h3>The Foreign Film That Changed Everything</h3>
<p>A small Korean drama with no stars and no marketing budget. Critics who saw it called it one of the year's best. But without a major distributor, it never reached mainstream audiences. ORCA nominated it. Our community watched it. And dozens of people told us it became their favourite film of the year.</p>

<h3>The Documentary Nobody Covered</h3>
<p>An independent documentary about a forgotten community, made on a shoestring budget. No streaming platform picked it up. No major publication reviewed it. ORCA's volunteer critics wrote a 2,000-word essay about why it mattered. The filmmaker told us it was the first real recognition they'd received.</p>

<h3>The Genre Film That Got Snubbed</h3>
<p>A sci-fi thriller with stunning practical effects and a script that made you think. Awards season ignored it because "genre films don't win awards." We disagreed. Our voters nominated it across three categories.</p>

<p><strong>That's what ORCA is about.</strong> Finding the films that deserve to be seen and making sure people see them.</p>

<p style="text-align: center; margin: 30px 0;">
  <a href="https://orcafilmawards.com" style="background-color: #0D1B2A; color: #D4AF37; padding: 14px 28px; text-decoration: none; border-radius: 6px; font-weight: bold;">Explore ORCA Film Awards</a>
</p>

<p>In our next email, we'll tell you how you can help decide which films get recognized.</p>

<p>— <strong>The ORCA Team</strong></p>
