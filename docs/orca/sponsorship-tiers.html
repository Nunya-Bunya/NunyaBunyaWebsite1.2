ORCA Film Awards — Sponsorship & Partnership Opportunities

2026 Awards Season

A Platform for Independent Film. A Stage for Your Brand.

Why Partner With ORCA Film Awards?

ORCA Film Awards is where the independent film community gathers. We are Brisbane's premier independent film awards organization — a growing cultural institution that celebrates craft, creativity, and the storytellers who refuse to wait for permission.

Our audience is engaged, discerning, and passionate. They are filmmakers, directors, cinematographers, producers, screenwriters, and film lovers — creative professionals and passionate audiences who respond to brands that stand for something.

When you partner with ORCA Film Awards, you don't just put your logo on an event. You become part of a story about the power of independent vision. You align your brand with artistic excellence, community, and Australian cultural life.

Our Audience

  • Independent filmmakers: emerging to established, 18–45
  • Film students and industry newcomers
  • Film enthusiasts, arts audiences, and cinema lovers
  • Industry professionals: agents, distributors, post-production, equipment
  • Cultural community: arts advocates, media, journalists
  • General public: arts event attendees in Brisbane and beyond

The Five Sponsorship Tiers

TIER 1 — PRESENTING SPONSOR

$15,000 AUD

"The ORCA Film Awards, presented by [Your Brand]"

The Presenting Sponsor owns the ORCA Film Awards experience. Your name is synonymous with the event. Every communication, every piece of collateral, every ceremony moment carries your brand alongside ours.

This is the most exclusive partnership ORCA offers — only one Presenting Sponsor is available per awards season.

Benefits:

Naming Rights & Branding

  • Official title: "ORCA Film Awards, Presented by [Brand]" — used in all event communications, press releases, social media, and on-night ceremony
  • Logo placed on the ORCA website hero section throughout the season (October–October)
  • Brand name and logo featured on all event marketing materials: posters, digital ads, email newsletters, social graphics
  • Logo on all printed materials: awards program, invitations, media credentials, certificates
  • Dedicated sponsor page on the ORCA website with brand description, logo, and link
  • Presenting Sponsor logo on stage backdrop / step-and-repeat banner

Awards Night

  • Exclusive VIP table of 10 at awards night (premium positioned, named)
  • Presenting Sponsor "moment" — brand featured in the opening of the ceremony
  • Brand representative invited to deliver opening remarks or present an award category
  • Branded naming rights to one awards category of your choice (e.g., "The [Brand] Award for Best Feature Film")
  • Pre-ceremony VIP reception (if applicable) branded with your identity

Social Media

  • Minimum 12 dedicated social media mentions across Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn throughout the season
  • Featured in all major announcement posts (submissions open, nominees, winners)
  • Co-branded social content created and shared (2 custom pieces per quarter)
  • Tagged in all event-day social content

Content & Media

  • Presenting Sponsor acknowledgement in all press releases (4 per season minimum)
  • Brand featured in post-event highlights reel / video content
  • Option to contribute a sponsored blog post or filmmaker spotlight to ORCA's content platform
  • Access to event photography for your own brand use (with ORCA credit)
  • Presenting Sponsor mention in any podcast or interview content produced during the season

Screening Series

  • Brand acknowledgement at all 4 quarterly screening series events
  • Logo on screening series event programs and digital assets

Additional

  • Personal briefing with ORCA founder before season begins to align on brand integration
  • End-of-season impact report with event statistics, reach metrics, and media coverage summary
  • Right of first refusal for 2027 Presenting Sponsorship

TIER 2 — GOLD SPONSOR

$7,500 AUD

Major Event Partner

The Gold Sponsor is a central partner of ORCA Film Awards — prominently featured across the season and deeply integrated into the awards night experience.

Up to 3 Gold Sponsors available per season.

Benefits:

Branding

  • Logo on ORCA website sponsor page throughout the season
  • Logo on all major event marketing materials: posters, digital banners, email newsletters
  • Logo on awards night printed program and stage backdrop (secondary placement)
  • Gold Sponsor acknowledgement in the opening of all ORCA newsletters

Awards Night

  • Reserved table of 6 at the awards night
  • Gold Sponsor representative invited to present one award category
  • Verbal acknowledgement at ceremony (opening and closing)
  • Branded acknowledgement in the awards night program (half-page)

Social Media

  • Minimum 8 dedicated social media mentions across the season
  • Featured in at least 2 major announcement posts
  • Logo on all awards night social content

Content & Media

  • Gold Sponsor acknowledgement in 2 press releases per season
  • Featured in post-event highlights reel (logo inclusion)
  • Access to event photography for brand use (with ORCA credit)

Screening Series

  • Logo on 2 quarterly screening series event programs

Additional

  • End-of-season impact report
  • Right of first refusal for following year at Gold level

TIER 3 — SILVER SPONSOR

$3,500 AUD

Supporting Partner

The Silver Sponsor is a recognized and valued supporter of ORCA Film Awards, with meaningful visibility across the season and at the awards night.

Up to 5 Silver Sponsors available per season.

Benefits:

Branding

  • Logo on ORCA website sponsor page
  • Logo on awards night printed program
  • Logo on event marketing digital materials (sponsor acknowledgement panel)
  • Email newsletter sponsor acknowledgement (footer level)

Awards Night

  • 4 general admission tickets to the awards night
  • Verbal acknowledgement at ceremony
  • Logo on stage backdrop (third-tier placement)

Social Media

  • Minimum 4 dedicated social media mentions across the season
  • Logo included in awards night social announcement posts

Content & Media

  • Silver Sponsor acknowledgement in 1 press release (awards night announcement)
  • Logo in post-event highlights content

Additional

  • End-of-season impact summary
  • Certificate of Partnership for internal use

TIER 4 — BRONZE SPONSOR

$1,500 AUD

Community Partner

Bronze Sponsors are proud community supporters of ORCA Film Awards. Their investment helps make the event possible and earns meaningful brand presence within the ORCA community.

Up to 8 Bronze Sponsors available per season.

Benefits:

Branding

  • Logo on ORCA website sponsor page (Bronze category)
  • Logo on awards night printed program (community sponsors section)
  • Acknowledgement in email newsletter sponsor footer

Awards Night

  • 2 general admission tickets to the awards night
  • Verbal group acknowledgement at ceremony

Social Media

  • Minimum 2 social media acknowledgements across the season (group sponsor recognition posts)

Additional

  • Certificate of Partnership
  • Mutual social media follow and support from ORCA accounts

TIER 5 — MEDIA PARTNER

In-Kind Trade

Press & Media Coverage Partner

ORCA Film Awards welcomes partnerships with media organizations whose audience aligns with ours. Media Partners provide editorial coverage or advertising space in exchange for event access, branding presence, and ORCA co-promotion.

Suitable for: arts publications, entertainment media, film industry magazines, local/national newspapers, podcasts, radio, and online platforms.

What ORCA Offers Media Partners:

  • Logo on awards night program and website (Media Partners section)
  • Media credentials for up to 3 journalists/photographers at the awards night
  • Exclusive interview access to ORCA founders and key guests
  • First access to nominee announcements and winner reveals (embargo basis)
  • Co-promotion of media partner's coverage across ORCA social channels
  • ORCA editorial content available for syndication (filmmaker interviews, etc.)
  • Verbal acknowledgement at awards night ceremony

What We Ask of Media Partners:

  • Minimum 2 editorial pieces covering ORCA Film Awards per season (event preview + event review/winner announcement)
  • Social media posts announcing ORCA news to their audience (minimum 3 per season)
  • Listing of ORCA Film Awards in relevant event calendars and listings
  • Agreed advertising space value: minimum $2,500 equivalent in-kind

FILM SCHOOL PARTNERSHIP PROGRAM

Nurturing the next generation of independent filmmakers

ORCA Film Awards has a deep commitment to emerging filmmakers. The Film School Partnership Program formalizes our relationship with Australia's leading film education institutions.

Partnership Structure

ORCA offers Film School Partners the following in exchange for institutional endorsement and student promotion:

What ORCA Provides to Film School Partners:

  • Dedicated "Film School Partner" listing on the ORCA website (logo + link)
  • Inclusion in ORCA's communications to filmmakers as a recommended educational institution
  • 40% submission fee discount for all verified enrolled students
  • Reserved student tickets to awards night at concession pricing
  • ORCA representative available for a guest lecture or panel appearance per year (subject to availability)
  • Student filmmaker spotlight content on ORCA platform (1 student spotlight per semester)
  • Nominations category specifically for Student Film with dedicated recognition

What ORCA Asks of Film School Partners:

  • Feature ORCA Film Awards in student communications as a recommended submission destination
  • Include ORCA submission information in relevant course syllabuses or student resources
  • Share ORCA content on institutional social media (minimum 2 posts per season)
  • Provide a faculty judge for the ORCA judging panel (at least one faculty member per season)
  • Mutual logo display (ORCA on film school website; Film School on ORCA website)

Annual Value Exchange: Estimated $3,000–$6,000 in mutual promotional value per partnership.

Current Film School Outreach Targets:

  • AFTRS (Australian Film, Television and Radio School)
  • QUT Creative Industries Faculty
  • Griffith Film School
  • VCA (Victorian College of the Arts) — Film & Television
  • SAE Brisbane
  • Bond University Film & Television

INDUSTRY PARTNER PROGRAM

For businesses that serve the film industry

The ORCA Industry Partner Program is designed for companies whose products and services directly serve the filmmaking community — camera and equipment rental houses, post-production facilities, sound studios, distribution platforms, colour grading services, and software providers.

Program Benefits

Industry Partners receive:

  • Logo and listing on ORCA website "Industry Partners" section with company description and link
  • Opportunity to provide exclusive discounts or offers to ORCA filmmakers and nominees (promoted via ORCA newsletter and social)
  • Presence at quarterly screening series events (table/display, subject to venue)
  • Social media mention (minimum 2 per season)
  • Access to ORCA filmmaker database for targeted promotional communications (opt-in only, strictly one-time use per agreed campaign)
  • Option to provide sponsor prizes for specific award categories (e.g., "Winner of Best Cinematography receives a $500 equipment rental voucher from [Partner]")

What We Ask:

  • Provision of valuable offer/discount to ORCA's filmmaker community
  • Minimum in-kind value of $1,500 (product, services, or vouchers)
  • Co-promotion of the partnership on your own channels

Custom Sponsorship Packages

No two brands are identical in their goals. If none of the standard tiers perfectly match your organization's objectives, ORCA is happy to develop a custom sponsorship package.

Custom packages may include:

  • Category naming rights for a specific award
  • Exclusive activation space at the awards night
  • Custom content production (e.g., a sponsored filmmaker interview series)
  • Branded screening events (your brand presents a curated screening)
  • Online screener platform sponsorship

Please contact us to discuss how we can create a partnership that delivers precisely what your brand needs.

Sponsorship Agreement & Payment

  • A formal Sponsorship Agreement is provided upon commitment, outlining all deliverables, payment terms, and brand usage rights
  • Payment is accepted in full or 50/50 split (50% upon signing, 50% four weeks prior to awards night)
  • ORCA issues a tax invoice upon agreement signing
  • All fees are in AUD and inclusive of GST where applicable
