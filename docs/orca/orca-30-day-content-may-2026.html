PLATFORM: Instagram

TYPE: Film Content - Film Criticism Matters

COPY: A SCORE IS NOT A REVIEW. 87% on Rotten Tomatoes tells you nothing about why a film matters. It doesn't tell you how the cinematography makes you feel claustrophobic on purpose. It doesn't explain why the director chose silence over a score in the final scene. It doesn't argue that this performance redefines what we expect from an actor. Numbers flatten art into consumer recommendations. Film criticism expands art into conversation. ORCA members don't rate films. They write about them. They argue for them. They make the case that a film you've never heard of might change the way you see the world. That's the difference. #ORCAFilmAwards #FilmCriticism #RottenTomatoes #CinemaIsArt #IndieFilm #FilmWriting
