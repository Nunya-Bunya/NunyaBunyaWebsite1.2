ORCA Film Awards 2026 — Event Planning Master Checklist

Annual Ceremony — Brisbane, Queensland

Target Capacity: 200–500 Guests

Format: Red Carpet Arrival | Screening Program | Awards Ceremony | After-Party

This is the master operational document for producing the ORCA Film Awards annual ceremony. Work through each phase sequentially. Assign a team member to each task block. Check items off as they are completed.

PHASE 1 — 6 Months Out

Venue

  • ☐ Confirm target event date and lock into the annual awards calendar
  • ☐ Define venue brief: minimum capacity 200, target 300–500; main stage with large-format projection screen; separate foyer or pre-function space for red carpet; catering facilities on-site or adjacent; accessible entrances and parking
  • ☐ Research and shortlist Brisbane venues — candidates: QPAC Concert Hall, Brisbane City Hall, The Fortitude Music Hall, Emporium Hotel Grand Ballroom, Howard Smith Wharves Function Centre
  • ☐ Conduct site visits at shortlisted venues
  • ☐ Negotiate venue hire agreement including: bump-in and bump-out access windows, exclusivity period, parking arrangements, noise curfew, insurance requirements, cancellation policy
  • ☐ Confirm venue AV infrastructure capabilities or plan for full external AV bring-in
  • ☐ Obtain venue's preferred/approved vendor lists and any supplier restrictions

Budget

  • ☐ Draft master event budget covering: venue hire, AV production, stage design, catering, trophies and plaques, print and signage, photographer, videographer, marketing, staffing, contingency (minimum 10%)
  • ☐ Get budget approved by ORCA leadership
  • ☐ Set up event expenditure tracking spreadsheet

Sponsors

  • ☐ Identify Presenting / Title Sponsor targets — approach with naming rights offer
  • ☐ Send formal Sponsor Proposal document to all Platinum and Gold tier prospects
  • ☐ Begin formal sponsor contracting for committed sponsors
  • ☐ Define sponsor benefits and deliverables schedule (logo placement, tickets, social mentions, program placement)

Judging and Submissions

  • ☐ Confirm submissions platform is live and accepting entries
  • ☐ Lock in judging panel — obtain confirmed commitments and signed conflict of interest declarations from all judges
  • ☐ Set judging timeline: screener access date, scoring deadline, deliberation date
  • ☐ Confirm deliberation date and ensure all judges are available

Key Suppliers — Initial Outreach

  • ☐ Begin search for award trophy and plaque manufacturer — brief on ORCA design aesthetic (dark, gold, cinematic)
  • ☐ Identify MC candidates — required style: cinematic, authoritative, warm, comfortable with an industry crowd
  • ☐ Identify red carpet photographer and videographer candidates
  • ☐ Identify AV production companies if venue requires external supplier — request quotes from minimum 3

PHASE 2 — 3 Months Out

Venue and Production

  • ☐ Finalise venue layout: stage configuration, seating plan, red carpet entry position, media wall placement, catering service flow
  • ☐ Confirm and execute AV production company contract
  • ☐ Brief AV team on ceremony structure: number of award segments, sizzle reel count, opening package, screen graphics, live streaming requirements
  • ☐ Brief production designer on stage set and ORCA brand aesthetic
  • ☐ Order ORCA award trophies and plaques — allow minimum 8 weeks for production; confirm engraving specifications
  • ☐ Commission media wall / step-and-repeat backdrop design and order fabrication
  • ☐ Confirm all branded signage requirements and begin design

MC and Presenters

  • ☐ Confirm MC — execute agreement, provide ceremony brief and tone reference
  • ☐ Begin identifying and inviting award presenters (film industry figures, sponsor representatives, notable past nominees or alumni)
  • ☐ Draft presenter confirmation process and briefing package

Nominees and Filmmaker Invitations

  • ☐ Set nominees announcement date aligned to judging timeline
  • ☐ Draft filmmaker invitation email: RSVP form, ticket details, event logistics
  • ☐ Confirm complimentary ticket allocation per nominated filmmaker

Catering

  • ☐ Brief and shortlist catering companies with event details
  • ☐ Confirm catering format: pre-show drinks reception, seated dinner or cocktail reception during/after ceremony, after-party food and beverage service
  • ☐ Discuss menu options, dietary accommodation process, and service staff numbers
  • ☐ Request catering proposals; shortlist and select caterer

Sponsors — Ongoing

  • ☐ Execute all sponsor contracts and collect initial payments (if staged structure)
  • ☐ Collect all sponsor logos in required formats: SVG, EPS, high-res PNG (white version and dark version)
  • ☐ Confirm sponsor ticket allocations and table assignments
  • ☐ Plan all sponsor logo placements: media wall, event program, digital assets, stage graphics
  • ☐ Brief design team on all sponsor deliverable timelines

Photography and Videography

  • ☐ Confirm red carpet and event photographer — execute agreement
  • ☐ Confirm event videographer for ceremony highlights reel — execute agreement
  • ☐ Brief both on shot list: red carpet arrivals, sponsor signage, awards moments, crowd atmosphere, presenter and winner portraits, trophy close-ups
  • ☐ Confirm live streaming plans — if yes, confirm platform (YouTube Live, ORCA website embed) and brief AV team on technical requirements

Marketing and Communications

  • ☐ Confirm event date on ORCA website with save-the-date announcement
  • ☐ Draft nominees announcement press release template
  • ☐ Build media invite list: film journalists, entertainment press, Brisbane arts media, lifestyle media, industry publications
  • ☐ Plan social media countdown campaign to run from nominees announcement through ceremony

PHASE 3 — 1 Month Out

Venue and Production

  • ☐ Confirm final floor plan and seating arrangement with venue
  • ☐ Confirm all stage set elements are in production and on schedule
  • ☐ Confirm bump-in schedule with venue, AV company, and all other suppliers
  • ☐ Confirm media wall fabrication is complete and delivery is scheduled
  • ☐ Confirm all trophies and plaques have been received — inspect each for quality, accuracy of engraving, and condition
  • ☐ Prepare winner envelopes — label by category, seal, store securely with Head of Judging or designated secure custodian

Program and Script

  • ☐ Draft full ceremony run-of-show (minute-by-minute schedule from doors open to close)
  • ☐ Write full MC script: welcome remarks, award category introductions, transitions, closing
  • ☐ Write presenter briefing scripts for each award category
  • ☐ Confirm order of award presentations — build to headline categories
  • ☐ Produce nominees sizzle reel for each award category (film clips, titles, music)
  • ☐ Produce opening ceremony brand film / sizzle reel
  • ☐ Produce all AV lower-thirds graphics, category titles, stinger animations
  • ☐ Draft acceptance speech guidance for winners (30–60 second target)

Nominees and Tickets

  • ☐ Send nominated filmmaker invitations with RSVP deadline, event logistics, and access information
  • ☐ Open general public ticket sales if applicable
  • ☐ Send sponsor ticket allocations and confirm RSVPs
  • ☐ Manage VIP and media ticket list separately

Catering

  • ☐ Confirm catering contract including projected guest count and per-head rate
  • ☐ Send dietary requirement survey to all confirmed guests via RSVP form
  • ☐ Confirm beverage package and service staff numbers

Red Carpet

  • ☐ Confirm red carpet hire or purchase and scheduled delivery date
  • ☐ Confirm red carpet hosting arrangement (MC, dedicated host, or ORCA representative)
  • ☐ Brief photographer and videographer on red carpet logistics and shot priorities
  • ☐ Order crowd barriers or stanchions as required

Live Streaming

  • ☐ Confirm streaming platform and full technical setup requirements with AV team
  • ☐ Conduct streaming test at venue — confirm internet bandwidth is adequate
  • ☐ Plan social media promotion for livestream link

Media and Press

  • ☐ Send formal media invitations to shortlisted journalists and accredited press
  • ☐ Prepare and distribute nominees announcement press release
  • ☐ Set up media accreditation process for event night
  • ☐ Confirm any embargoed communications with key media contacts

Social Media

  • ☐ Plan nominees announcement social campaign — individual nominee posts, category reveal series
  • ☐ Launch countdown content series across all ORCA platforms
  • ☐ Prepare event-night social media content calendar: pre-written captions, graphic templates, story formats
  • ☐ Assign and brief social media coverage responsibilities for event night

PHASE 4 — 2 Weeks Out

Production and Logistics

  • ☐ Confirm final guest count with venue and caterer
  • ☐ Finalise seating chart — provide to venue and catering team
  • ☐ Confirm all AV elements are production-complete: sizzle reels, lower thirds, stingers, award category title cards
  • ☐ Conduct full technical rehearsal with AV team at venue — run every video element, test all sound levels, confirm camera feeds and streaming output
  • ☐ Confirm all trophies and plaques are on-site and correct
  • ☐ Confirm winner envelopes are sealed, labelled, and stored securely
  • ☐ Confirm all presenter commitments — chase any outstanding confirmations

Staffing

  • ☐ Assign all event night roles: door and guest list, red carpet management, front of house, backstage crew, social media coverage, AV operation, catering liaison, green room coordinator
  • ☐ Brief all staff and volunteers on roles, full run-of-show, venue layout, and emergency procedures
  • ☐ Conduct full MC briefing — review final script together

Communications

  • ☐ Send "week-of" reminder email to nominees with event logistics (arrival time, parking, dress code, green room access)
  • ☐ Send sponsor event reminder with on-night activation details and logistics
  • ☐ Schedule week-of promotional social content across all ORCA channels
  • ☐ Distribute media advisory to all accredited press with event details and red carpet access time

Assets and Equipment

  • ☐ Confirm all branded print assets are produced: event programs, table cards, sponsor signage, directional signage
  • ☐ Confirm event photography shot list is finalised and distributed to photographer
  • ☐ Charge all event-night technology: cameras, streaming hardware, team communication devices

PHASE 5 — 1 Week Out

Final Confirmations

  • ☐ Confirm final RSVP count — communicate to venue and caterer
  • ☐ Confirm full venue bump-in schedule — share with all suppliers
  • ☐ Final MC briefing — review complete final script together
  • ☐ Final confirmation with all presenters — distribute presenter packs (script, winner envelope instructions, timing guidance)
  • ☐ Confirm photographer and videographer call times and briefing is complete
  • ☐ Confirm AV final technical rehearsal date and attendance
  • ☐ Confirm red carpet hosting arrangement is locked

Print and Materials

  • ☐ Print final ceremony programs
  • ☐ Print table seating cards
  • ☐ Print all signage: directional, sponsor boards, table numbers, red carpet barriers
  • ☐ Prepare and seal all winner envelopes — store securely

Social Media and Press

  • ☐ Schedule all pre-event social media posts through to ceremony day
  • ☐ Send final media advisory with red carpet time and access details to all accredited press
  • ☐ Pre-stage all event-night live social content (stories, posts, reels)

Logistics

  • ☐ Confirm all supplier delivery times and access arrangements with venue
  • ☐ Prepare event night emergency contact sheet — distribute to all key staff
  • ☐ Confirm after-party venue or area and service arrangements

PHASE 6 — Day Before

Bump-In and Setup

  • ☐ Oversee full venue bump-in — supervise all supplier deliveries and setup
  • ☐ Stage and dress red carpet area: carpet, media wall, crowd barriers, directional lighting
  • ☐ AV rig, screens, and audio setup with AV production company — confirm all cabling complete
  • ☐ Full technical rehearsal — run every video element, test sound at performance levels, confirm all camera feeds and stream output
  • ☐ Set up catering areas — confirm service flow and staff positions with catering team
  • ☐ Position all trophies and winner envelopes in secure backstage area in ceremony order
  • ☐ Set up guest registration desk — prepare guest list, name badges, programs
  • ☐ Dress the stage: ORCA branding, podium, award presentation table, floral or staging elements
  • ☐ Walk the full venue floor — confirm signage, seating setup, table dressing, directional signage, sponsor placements

Final Briefings

  • ☐ Full crew briefing — walk through the evening schedule minute by minute with all team leads
  • ☐ Brief door and registration staff on guest list procedures, VIP handling, and media access
  • ☐ Confirm backstage crew positions and responsibilities for each award segment
  • ☐ Ensure all staff have the event-night emergency contact list

Communications

  • ☐ Post "tomorrow night" teaser content across all ORCA social channels
  • ☐ Send "see you tomorrow" reminder email to all nominees and confirmed guests
  • ☐ Confirm photographer and videographer start times

PHASE 7 — Day of Event

Pre-Show (Morning to Afternoon)

  • ☐ Final venue walkthrough — confirm all setup is correct, nothing missing
  • ☐ Confirm catering is on schedule — check in with catering team lead
  • ☐ AV team conducting final system checks
  • ☐ Distribute winner envelopes to backstage area in presentation order
  • ☐ Brief front-of-house team on door procedures, VIP table locations, and guest list management
  • ☐ Confirm social media manager has all content staged and is ready for live coverage

Red Carpet (1–1.5 Hours Before Ceremony)

  • ☐ Open red carpet — confirm all media are in position behind barriers
  • ☐ Red carpet host or MC is present and briefed on key guests to watch for
  • ☐ Photographer in position — actively shooting all arrivals
  • ☐ Videographer capturing all arrivals
  • ☐ Social media team posting live content from red carpet
  • ☐ ORCA team member managing red carpet traffic and directing notable guests to media position

Ceremony

  • ☐ Doors open for general guests — front of house managing seating and programs
  • ☐ MC is backstage with final script in hand
  • ☐ AV team completing final sound check
  • ☐ All presenters confirmed backstage with their winner envelopes in category order
  • ☐ Live stream goes live 5 minutes before ceremony commences (if applicable)
  • ☐ Ceremony commences on schedule
  • ☐ Award segments run per run-of-show — AV team cuing each reel on signal from Event Director
  • ☐ Photographer capturing all award presentation moments
  • ☐ Videographer capturing all award moments and acceptance speeches
  • ☐ Social media team posting winners live as announced using pre-staged templates
  • ☐ Event Director monitoring pace and signalling MC if adjustments are needed
  • ☐ AV crew ready to troubleshoot any technical issues immediately

After-Party

  • ☐ Transition guests to after-party area or reception space immediately following ceremony
  • ☐ Confirm bar and catering service is operational
  • ☐ Continue social media coverage: winner celebrations, candid moments, atmosphere
  • ☐ Collect all unclaimed trophies — set aside for postal dispatch

PHASE 8 — Post-Event

Immediate (Day After)

  • ☐ Thank-you email to all nominees and attendees
  • ☐ Thank-you email to all sponsors (personalised per tier)
  • ☐ Thank-you message to MC, all presenters, judges, and venue team
  • ☐ Post winners announcement across ORCA website, all social channels, and email list
  • ☐ Share event photography highlights on social media — begin with 3–5 hero images

Within 1 Week

  • ☐ Dispatch all uncollected trophies and plaques by registered post to winners
  • ☐ Receive and review full event photography — select hero images for archive, press, and ongoing content
  • ☐ Receive event highlights video footage — begin edit for ceremony highlights reel
  • ☐ Send sponsor post-event report: final attendance numbers, social reach, media coverage summary, photos featuring sponsor branding
  • ☐ Collect and archive all media coverage: articles, clips, social mentions

Within 2 Weeks

  • ☐ Publish ceremony highlights reel across all ORCA channels (YouTube, Instagram, website)
  • ☐ Issue post-event press release with full winner list and event highlights to all media contacts
  • ☐ Conduct internal event debrief: what worked, what to improve, action items for 2027
  • ☐ Update ORCA website with complete 2026 winner records and ceremony photography gallery
  • ☐ Archive all event documentation: contracts, vendor contacts, final budget, run-of-show, guest list

Financial Wrap

  • ☐ Reconcile all event expenditure against budget
  • ☐ Process all outstanding supplier invoices
  • ☐ Issue any outstanding invoices to sponsors with remaining balances
  • ☐ Prepare financial event summary report
  • ☐ Begin 2027 planning notes document based on debrief findings
