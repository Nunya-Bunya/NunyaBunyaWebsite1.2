ORCA Film Awards — Implementation Checklist

IMPORTANT: ORCA is an awards body and voting collective, NOT a film festival. No submissions, no entry fees, no screening events. Free membership only.

Phase 1: Foundation (Current)

Voting Body

  • ☑ Launch website (orcafilmawards.com)
  • ☑ Define 20 award categories
  • ☑ Run 2024 and 2025 voting cycles
  • ☐ Recruit voting members (target: 1,000 by 2030)
  • ☐ Build member onboarding flow on website

Film Criticism & Essays

  • ☐ Publish first articles on orcafilmawards.com
  • ☐ Recruit volunteer writers/critics
  • ☐ Establish editorial calendar for film essays

Content & Promotion

  • ☑ Create social media accounts
  • ☑ Develop brand assets (logo, style guide, brand bible)
  • ☑ Create lead magnet: "25 Films the Oscars Missed"
  • ☐ Deploy lead magnet (PDF + landing page + Mautic form)
  • ☐ Promote on Film Twitter, Reddit r/TrueFilm, newsletter
  • ☑ Build 30-day content calendar
  • ☐ Execute content calendar

Infrastructure

  • ☑ Set up Mautic email sequences
  • ☑ Set up Matomo analytics
  • ☐ Deploy tracking code on ORCA site

Phase 2: Film Publication (Next)

Build Credibility

  • ☐ Publish regular film reviews and criticism
  • ☐ Cover major festivals (Gold Coast, Venice, Cannes, etc.) through written criticism
  • ☐ Build a body of work that establishes ORCA as a serious voice

Grow Membership

  • ☐ Active recruitment campaigns
  • ☐ Get members writing and contributing
  • ☐ Community engagement (discussions, recommendations)

Phase 3: Press & Recognition (Future)

  • ☐ Apply for press credentials at major film festivals
  • ☐ Establish ORCA as a recognized film news/review publication
  • ☐ Build relationships with film industry

Phase 4: Full Awards Ceremony (Long-term)

  • ☐ Plan and execute a proper awards ceremony
  • ☐ Build toward rivaling established awards bodies
  • ☐ Maintain free membership model

Success Metrics

  • Voting members recruited
  • Articles/essays published
  • Engagement on film criticism content
  • Press credential applications accepted
  • Website traffic and email list growth
