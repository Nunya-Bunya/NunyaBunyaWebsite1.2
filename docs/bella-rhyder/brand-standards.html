1. LOGO USAGE

Logo Variants

VariantBackground UseFile Format
Primary (Navy)White or light backgroundsSVG, PNG
Reversed (White)Midnight navy, dark photographySVG, PNG
Gold (Warm Gold)Navy or black backgrounds onlySVG, PNG
Monochrome (Black)Single-color print, embossingSVG, EPS
Monochrome (White)Dark print, screenprint, embroiderySVG, EPS

Logo Lockups

  • Primary lockup: Wordmark + tagline "Move Boldly. Live Freely." stacked below
  • Secondary lockup: Wordmark alone (when tagline reads too small)
  • Icon mark: BR monogram — used for favicon, embossing, small-scale use only
  • Horizontal lockup: Wordmark + icon side-by-side (email headers, packaging tape)

Clear Space Rule

Maintain clear space equal to the height of the capital "B" in the logo on all four sides. Nothing — no text, images, borders, or other logos — enters this zone.

Minimum Size

  • Print: 25mm wide minimum (primary lockup)
  • Digital: 120px wide minimum (primary lockup)
  • Icon mark: No smaller than 16px x 16px (favicon), 12mm in print

Logo Don'ts

  • Do NOT stretch or distort the logo
  • Do NOT rotate the logo
  • Do NOT apply drop shadows or effects
  • Do NOT change the logo colors to anything outside the approved palette
  • Do NOT place the primary (navy) logo on a dark background
  • Do NOT place the reversed (white) logo on a white background
  • Do NOT recreate the logo in another typeface
  • Do NOT add outlines or strokes around the logo
  • Do NOT place on busy, cluttered photography without a treatment (overlay, color block, or frosted background)

2. COLOR SYSTEM

Primary Palette

Midnight Navy

  • Hex: #1A1A2E
  • RGB: R26 G26 B46
  • CMYK: C93 M90 Y55 K65
  • Pantone: 2766 C
  • Usage: 60% — primary brand color. Backgrounds, packaging, primary text, apparel tags.

Warm Gold

  • Hex: #E8B86D
  • RGB: R232 G184 B109
  • CMYK: C6 M27 Y62 K0
  • Pantone: 7508 C
  • Usage: 10% — premium accent. Headlines over dark backgrounds, logo on navy, decorative details, swing tags.

White

  • Hex: #FFFFFF
  • RGB: R255 G255 B255
  • CMYK: C0 M0 Y0 K0
  • Pantone: N/A
  • Usage: 25% — breathing space, text on dark backgrounds, product photography backgrounds, clean UI.

Accent / Supporting Palette

Coral / Terracotta

  • Hex: #D4654A
  • RGB: R212 G101 B74
  • CMYK: C9 M69 Y72 K1
  • Pantone: 7526 C
  • Usage: 5% — seasonal accent, sale/promotion highlights, CTA buttons in specific campaign contexts. Use sparingly.

Sand

  • Hex: #F2E8D9
  • RGB: R242 G232 B217
  • CMYK: C2 M6 Y13 K0
  • Pantone: 9183 C
  • Usage: Supporting neutral — flat lay surfaces, email backgrounds, light-mode UI secondary.

Soft Blush

  • Hex: #F0D9D2
  • RGB: R240 G217 B210
  • CMYK: C3 M14 Y13 K0
  • Pantone: 9203 C
  • Usage: Campaign and seasonal use only.

Dark Charcoal

  • Hex: #2C2C3E
  • RGB: R44 G44 B62
  • CMYK: C85 M80 Y50 K50
  • Pantone: 2768 C
  • Usage: Button hover states, secondary dark tone, email body text.

Color Usage Ratios

  • 60% Midnight Navy — dominant tone, establishes premium/luxury feel
  • 25% White — breathing room, clean energy
  • 10% Warm Gold — premium accent, never overwhelming
  • 5% Coral + Sand + Blush combined — campaign and seasonal use only

Color Combination Rules

Approved pairings:

  • Midnight Navy + White (high legibility, primary)
  • Midnight Navy + Warm Gold (premium, branded)
  • White + Midnight Navy text (web body copy)
  • White + Coral (campaign-specific only)
  • Sand + Midnight Navy text (email background)
  • White + Warm Gold on photography (overlaid text treatments)

Never pair:

  • Warm Gold + Coral (too warm, low contrast)
  • Coral on Navy (insufficient contrast, fails accessibility)
  • Warm Gold on White (insufficient contrast for body text — use for display only, large sizes)

Accessibility

  • All body text must meet WCAG 2.1 AA contrast ratio (4.5:1 minimum)
  • Navy on White: PASS (contrast 15.2:1)
  • White on Navy: PASS (contrast 15.2:1)
  • White on Coral: PASS at large sizes only — use for display, not body copy

3. TYPOGRAPHY

Font Stack

Display:  Playfair Display — Bold 700, Bold Italic 700
Body:     DM Sans — Regular 400, Medium 500, Bold 700
Accent:   Cormorant Garamond — Italic 400

Google Fonts import (web):

@import url('https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Playfair+Display:ital,wght@0,700;1,700&family=DM+Sans:wght@400;500;700&family=Cormorant+Garamond:ital@1&display=swap');

Font Details

Display / Headlines — Playfair Display

  • Source: Google Fonts (free)
  • Weights in use: 700 Bold, 700 Bold Italic
  • Use cases: Campaign headlines, hero text, editorial pull quotes, seasonal campaign titles
  • Letter spacing: -0.02em (tight, refined)
  • Line height: 1.15
  • Character: Elegant, feminine strength. Balances the active energy of the brand with its aspirational quality.

Body / UI — DM Sans

  • Source: Google Fonts (free)
  • Weights in use: 400 Regular, 500 Medium, 700 Bold
  • Use cases: Product descriptions, navigation, captions, buttons, email copy, subheadings
  • Letter spacing: 0 (body), +0.05em (all-caps labels)
  • Line height: 1.6 (body), 1.2 (headings)
  • Character: Clean, modern, highly legible at small sizes.

Accent / Refined Details — Cormorant Garamond

  • Source: Google Fonts (free)
  • Weights in use: 400 Italic only
  • Use cases: Tagline treatments, swing tag copy, pull quotes, packaging sub-text
  • Use very sparingly — this is the brand's whisper, not its voice
  • Character: Refined, subtle elegance.

6. ICON SYSTEM

Icon Style

  • Style: Outlined, minimal, single-stroke
  • Stroke weight: 1.5px
  • Corner radius: 1.5px (slightly softened, not harsh)
  • Size grid: Designed on 24x24px canvas
  • Display sizes: 16px, 20px, 24px, 32px

Icon Color Rules

  • All icons render in #1A1A2E (Navy) on light backgrounds
  • All icons render in #FFFFFF (White) on dark backgrounds
  • Gold icon treatment (#E8B86D) for premium / featured UI elements only (wishlist star, loyalty badge)
  • No multi-color icons in UI — single colour per icon always

Icon Set Recommendation

Base: Phosphor Icons (MIT License) — clean, minimal, outline style that matches brand aesthetic.

Alternative: Feather Icons or custom SVG set.

Social Media Icons

  • Use official brand icon variants, not generic placeholders
  • Render in White on Navy backgrounds; Navy on White/Sand backgrounds
  • Do not use colored social icons (no Instagram gradient, no Facebook blue) — always on-brand tones

7. EMAIL TEMPLATE STANDARDS

Email Dimensions

  • Max width: 600px
  • Background: #F2E8D9 (Sand) or #FFFFFF (White)
  • Inner content background: #FFFFFF
  • Mobile-responsive: Yes — single column below 480px

Email Header

  • Height: 80px
  • Background: #1A1A2E (Midnight Navy)
  • Logo: Reversed (white) variant, centered or left-aligned
  • Max logo width: 160px

  • Body: #FFFFFF
  • Feature section: #F2E8D9 (Sand) — alternate section blocks
  • Footer: #1A1A2E (Navy) — white text, gold social icons

Email Footer Requirements

  • Logo (white reversed)
  • Physical address (Brisbane, QLD, Australia)
  • Unsubscribe link (legally required in Australia)
  • Social media icons
  • "You're receiving this because..." reminder text
  • Footer font: DM Sans Regular 12px, #FFFFFF or #B0B0B8

Email Spacing

  • Top/bottom padding: 40px on all major sections
  • Left/right padding: 40px (desktop), 20px (mobile)
  • Button: Centered, 180px minimum width, 16px padding top/bottom

8. PACKAGING DESIGN STANDARDS

Poly Mailer Bags

  • Background: Midnight Navy (#1A1A2E)
  • Logo: White reversed variant — centered
  • Size: Standard Australia Post size range (DL, B4, B3)
  • Interior: Optional subtle pattern (small BR monogram repeat in dark navy on navy — tone-on-tone)
  • No branding bleed to inside — keep interior clean for unboxing experience

Swing Tags

  • Material: 400gsm uncoated card (tactile, premium feel)
  • Front: Bella Rhyder logo (navy ink on cream/sand stock, OR white logo on navy stock)
  • Back: Care instructions (DM Sans Regular 8pt) + product code
  • Tagline below logo: Cormorant Garamond Italic 10pt — "Move Boldly. Live Freely."
  • Hole: 4mm punch, top center, with natural cotton string
  • Dimensions: 55mm x 90mm

Labels (Garment Labels)

  • Woven main label: Midnight Navy ground, white woven logo + BR monogram
  • Size label: Midnight Navy heat-transfer or woven — white text, DM Sans Bold
  • Care label: Printed, white with navy text — standard Australian care symbol set
  • Country of origin: Required on all garments (Australian Consumer Law)

Tissue Paper

  • Color: White tissue paper with Warm Gold (#E8B86D) or Midnight Navy foil-printed pattern
  • Pattern option A: BR monogram scattered (allover, subtle)
  • Pattern option B: Botanical / wave motif (Queensland-inspired)
  • Minimum sheet: 50cm x 70cm per fold

Stickers / Seals

  • Round sticker seal: Midnight Navy, gold foil BR monogram, 40mm diameter
  • "Thank You" card: Sand (#F2E8D9) card stock, navy print — Playfair Display 20pt headline, DM Sans 12pt body
  • Text: "Thank you for choosing Bella Rhyder. Move Boldly. Live Freely."

Gift Box (Premium / Bundle Orders)

  • Rigid lid-and-base box
  • Exterior: Midnight Navy with embossed or foil-stamped logo
  • Interior: Gold tissue lining OR gold satin ribbon
  • Size variants: Small (accessories), Medium (single garment), Large (full outfit bundle)
