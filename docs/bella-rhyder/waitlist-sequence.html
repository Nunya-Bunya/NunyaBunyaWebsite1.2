Bella Rhyder -- Pre-Launch Waitlist Nurture Sequence

3 emails. Triggered when someone joins the waitlist.

Voice: warm, authentic, aspirational but grounded. Independent creative brand, NOT corporate athleisure.

No fake testimonials. No specific pricing. No promises we can't keep.

Email 1 -- Welcome (Day 0)

Subject: You're on the list. Here's why that matters.

Preview text: A brand born from a dream and the Queensland coastline.

Hey {contactfield=firstname},

Thank you for joining the Bella Rhyder waitlist. Genuinely -- it means a lot.

We want to tell you what this brand actually is, because it's not just another activewear label.

Bella Rhyder started as a dream. A real one -- the kind that keeps you up at night sketching ideas, pulling fabric swatches, and imagining what it would feel like to build something truly yours. It was born on the Queensland coast, from early morning runs along the esplanade and Sunday swims before the crowds roll in.

The idea is simple: activewear and lifestyle pieces designed for women who don't draw a hard line between their workout and their life. The morning coffee run. The beach session. The gym class. The weekend hike. Movement is woven into everything -- and your clothes should keep up.

We're building this brand with intention, not speed. Every piece is being designed to move with you, hold up wash after wash, and look as good at brunch as it does in the gym.

You'll be among the first to see the collection when it's ready. We'll keep you in the loop as things take shape.

Welcome aboard.

-- The Bella Rhyder Team

Move Boldly. Live Freely.

Email 2 -- Sneak Peek (Day 7)

Subject: A first look at what we're building

Preview text: Three collections. One philosophy. Here's what's coming.

Hey {contactfield=firstname},

It's been a week since you joined the waitlist, and we wanted to share what we've been working on.

Bella Rhyder is shaping up around three core areas:

Performance Activewear

Leggings, sports bras, shorts, and crops built for real movement. We're talking squat-proof, moisture-wicking, four-way stretch fabrics that actually perform -- not just on the first wear, but after dozens of washes. These are pieces designed to work as hard as you do.

Coastal Lifestyle

Swimwear and cover-ups that blur the line between the beach and the gym. If your morning starts with a swim and ends with a coffee run, you shouldn't need three outfit changes in between. We're designing for that reality.

Accessories

Bags, hats, and lifestyle pieces that tie a whole look together. Functional, considered, and designed with the same care as everything else in the collection.

Every product starts with one question: Does this make her move more freely? If the answer is yes, it earns its place.

We're not rushing anything. Quality takes time, and we'd rather get it right than get it fast.

More soon.

-- The Bella Rhyder Team

Email 3 -- Still Building (Day 21)

Subject: An honest update from the studio

Preview text: We're still here. Still building. And we'd love your input.

Hey {contactfield=firstname},

Quick check-in from behind the scenes.

We're still in the thick of it -- refining fabrics, testing fits, finalising colourways, and making sure every piece lives up to the standard we've set for ourselves. Some days that means celebrating a perfect sample. Other days it means going back to the drawing board. Both are part of the process.

Here's what we believe: a brand worth wearing is a brand worth waiting for. We're not cutting corners on fabric, fit, or finish. And we're not launching until we're proud of every single piece.

We'd love to hear from you while we work. What matters most to you in activewear?

  • Performance and durability above everything?
  • Style that crosses over from gym to life?
  • Pieces designed for the Queensland heat and coastline?
  • Something else entirely?

Hit reply and tell us. Seriously -- we read every response, and your input shapes what we build.

Thank you for sticking with us. The waitlist isn't just a list to us -- it's the beginning of a community. And you're part of it from the start.

We'll be in touch when there's something real to show you.

-- The Bella Rhyder Team

Move Boldly. Live Freely.
