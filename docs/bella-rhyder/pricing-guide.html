💰 OUR PRICING PHILOSOPHY

Bella Rhyder believes in transparent, value-based pricing that reflects our commitment to quality, sustainability, and authentic Queensland lifestyle. We position ourselves as a premium brand that offers exceptional value through durable, versatile products that last beyond a single season.

Our Promise: No hidden fees, no surprise charges, no nickel-and-diming. What you see is what you pay.

🎯 PRICING STRATEGY

Value-Based Positioning

  • Premium Quality: Positioned above fast fashion ($30-70 AUD) but below luxury brands ($200+ AUD)
  • Target Price Range: $50-150 AUD for most products
  • Perceived Value: 2-3x the quality of budget competitors
  • Brand Equity: Australian-made quality with Queensland authenticity

Pricing Framework

  • Cost Plus Model: Base price = (COGS × 2.5) + 20% margin
  • Psychological Pricing: $89 instead of $90, $99 instead of $100
  • Tiered Pricing: Base, Premium, and Limited Edition variants
  • Bundle Pricing: 10-15% discount for co-ord sets and bundles

💳 E-COMMERCE PRICING & FEES

Base Pricing (Website)

  • All prices include GST
  • Free standard shipping on orders over $100 AUD
  • Standard shipping: $9.95 AUD (under $100)
  • Express shipping: $14.95 AUD
  • International shipping: Calculated at checkout

Payment Methods

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express (2.9% + 30¢ fee)
  • PayPal: 2.9% + 30¢ fee
  • Afterpay: 4% fee (interest-free payments)
  • Apple Pay/Google Pay: Same as card fees

Currency & International

  • Primary Currency: Australian Dollars (AUD)
  • International Conversion: Automatic currency conversion at checkout
  • No currency surcharges

Pricing Elasticity

❓ FREQUENTLY ASKED PRICING QUESTIONS

Q: Why are your prices higher than fast fashion brands?

A: We use premium, sustainable fabrics and ethical manufacturing. Our pieces are designed to last 2-3 years vs. 6 months for fast fashion.

Q: Do you offer student or senior discounts?

A: Yes! 10% off for students (valid ID required) and seniors 65+.

Q: What's your price match policy?

A: We match prices from authorized Australian retailers. Contact customer service with proof of lower price.

Q: Can I negotiate prices for bulk orders?

A: Yes, for orders over $500. Contact wholesale@bellaryder.com for custom quotes.
