Platform 1: Instagram (Primary)

Role

Instagram is the flagship platform for Bella Rhyder. It is the brand's primary storefront, community hub, customer service touchpoint, and aspiration engine. A strong, cohesive Instagram presence is the primary driver of brand credibility, purchase intent, and customer trust.

Grid Aesthetic

  • Warm, consistent colour temperature across all posts — every image edited to the same Lightroom preset
  • Content rhythm (loose, not rigid): Lifestyle → Product → Lifestyle → Quote/Campaign → Lifestyle
  • Background tones: Sandy, warm white, coastal — never cold or grey
  • Vertical product shots (4:5) and square lifestyle shots (1:1) — mix for visual rhythm
  • No harsh studio lighting in the grid — all natural light or warm-toned outdoor settings
  • Brand colours (#1A1A2E navy and #E8B86D gold) referenced in packaging, props, and design overlays

Feed Posts

  • Frequency: 5 per week (Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday)
  • Format mix per week: Static images (3), Carousel (1), Reel (1)
  • Caption length: 80–200 words for primary posts; under 50 words for product posts
  • Hashtags: 10–15 per post (in caption or first comment)

Hashtag Strategy:

  • Branded: #BellaRhyder #MoveBoldlyLiveFree #BellaRhyderWoman
  • Community: #AussieActivewear #QueenslandFitness #ActiveWomen #BeachFitness #AussieWomen
  • Product niche: #Activewear #Swimwear #SportsStyle #LeggingsOfInstagram #SportsBra #GymStyle
  • Local/lifestyle: #BrisbaneStyle #SunshineCoast #GoldCoastLife #QLDFitness #CoastalLiving #BrisbaneWomen
  • Seasonal/theme: Rotate based on monthly theme (see Monthly Calendar below)

Reels Strategy

  • Frequency: Minimum 1 Reel per week, target 2
  • Length targets:
  • 7–15 seconds: Product showcase, colourway reveal, quick lifestyle moment
  • 15–30 seconds: Morning routine, GRWM workout, beach day content
  • 30–60 seconds: Community spotlight, brand story moments, seasonal campaign
  • Hook rule: First 2 seconds must stop the scroll — start mid-action, not at the beginning of a pose
  • Audio: Trending audio when contextually appropriate; original ambient audio for premium brand content
  • Text overlay: Use on all Reels in case audio is off — DM Sans Bold, white text
  • CTA: End screen with logo on navy, CTA text ("Shop the look — link in bio")

Platform 2: TikTok (Secondary)

Role

TikTok is Bella Rhyder's discovery engine. The algorithm allows a brand with zero followers to reach millions of targeted viewers through a single high-performing video. Content should feel notably more raw, real, and native than Instagram — never a polished repurpose.

Content Philosophy

  • Native first — content that belongs on TikTok, not Instagram reformatted
  • Personality-led — a real human presence on camera dramatically increases performance
  • Trend-aware — respond to trending sounds and formats within 24–48 hours of peak
  • Authentic over polished — real environments, handheld footage, natural cuts all outperform over-produced content

Posting Cadence

  • Frequency: 3 videos per week (minimum)
  • Best posting times (AEST): 7–9 AM, 12–2 PM, 6–9 PM
  • Consistency beats perfection — a real video posted on schedule beats a perfect video posted late

TikTok SEO

  • Include target keywords in caption and spoken audio (TikTok transcribes speech for search)
  • Target keywords: "activewear australia," "sports bra review," "gym outfit australia," "activewear haul," "brisbane lifestyle," "aussie activewear," "leggings review," "beach outfit"
  • Use 3–5 relevant hashtags per post (TikTok is less hashtag-dependent than Instagram)
  • Branded TikTok hashtags: #BellaRhyder #MoveBoldlyLiveFree

Platform 3: Pinterest (Tertiary)

Role

Pinterest is the long-tail discovery platform. A well-optimised pin can drive traffic for 3–5 years. The audience is overwhelmingly female (76%) and actively searches for inspiration before purchase decisions. Pinterest functions as a visual search engine for lifestyle and fashion — ideal for Bella Rhyder's aesthetic.

Board Structure

  • Bella Rhyder Activewear — product shots, outfit styling
  • Beach Life Queensland — coastal lifestyle and location inspiration
  • Workout Style & Fitness — gym and training aesthetic
  • Summer Ready — swimwear and beach collections
  • Active Women Lifestyle — movement, fitness, community imagery
  • Behind the Brand — BTS, values content, brand story

Pinterest Content Guidelines

  • Repurpose all Instagram images optimised for vertical format (1000×1500px)
  • Add text overlay to all pins: brand name + category keyword (e.g., "Bella Rhyder | High-Waist Leggings")
  • Link every pin directly to the relevant Shopify product page or collection
  • Enable Product Rich Pins so price and availability display automatically
  • Schedule via Tailwind (5–10 pins per week, schedulable in advance)

Content Pillars (5)

All Bella Rhyder social content maps to one of five pillars. Content calendars are planned with the correct split to ensure variety and strategic balance.

Pillar 1: PRODUCT SHOWCASE (30%)

What we make. Why it matters. How it moves.

  • Product reveals and new arrivals
  • Colourway showcases and comparison posts
  • Fabric detail and feature callouts
  • Styling posts (how to wear it three ways)
  • Flat lays and product photography
  • "Why our leggings are different" comparison content

Specific post ideas:

  • "3 ways to wear the [Legging Name]" carousel
  • Fabric close-up Reel: "This is why they don't fall down mid-run"
  • Before/after product layout: packaging reveal to styled outfit
  • "What fits in the [Bag Name]" — pack with me content
  • Colourway drop: "New colours just dropped — which is yours?"

Goal: Drive purchase intent and product awareness

Pillar 2: LIFESTYLE & MOVEMENT (25%)

The world she lives in. The world we're built for.

  • Beach, outdoor, and Queensland location content
  • Morning routines and active day-in-the-life
  • Post-workout moments (coffee, stretch, sauna)
  • Travel and weekend content featuring BR pieces
  • Seasonal content aligned to monthly themes

Specific post ideas:

  • "Sunday morning in Brisbane" aesthetic reel — beach walk, coffee, movement
  • "This is what our pieces were made for" — surf-to-street outfit transition
  • Golden hour workout session on the beach — lifestyle editorial
  • "Morning routine" TikTok — wake up, workout, get ready, coffee
  • Weekend hike content: "Where movement takes us"

Goal: Build aspirational brand identity and emotional connection

Pillar 3: BEHIND THE SCENES (10%)

The brand behind the product.

  • Photoshoot day content
  • Order packing and fulfilment glimpses
  • Founder and team content (humanises the brand)
  • New product development sneak peeks

Specific post ideas:

  • Photoshoot day TikTok: "A day building the brand"

Goal: Brand trust, authenticity, and anticipation-building

Pillar 4: COMMUNITY & UGC (20%)

The women who move together.

  • Community shoutouts and tags
  • "Bella Rhyder Woman" spotlight features
  • Ambassador and influencer content reshares
  • Testimonials and review highlights in branded frames

Specific post ideas:

  • Monthly "Community Spotlight" — one real customer featured in the feed
  • "Share your move" ongoing challenge: tag us in your active moment

  • Review carousel: "What they're saying about [Product]"

Useful. Shareable. Worth saving.

  • Size and fit guides (build confidence in purchasing online)

Goal: Shareability, saves, and SEO value; positions brand as a trusted expert

Influencer Partnership Framework

Philosophy

Bella Rhyder builds genuine relationships with creators who actually live the lifestyle the brand is built for. Authentic alignment is prioritised over follower count. A 6,000-follower fitness creator in Brisbane who genuinely uses activewear is more valuable than a 200,000-follower fashion influencer who doesn't.

Tier Structure

Tier 1: Gifting Partners (5K–15K followers)

  • Arrangement: Product gifting only, no payment
  • Expectation: 1–2 posts or Stories within 4 weeks (communicated, not contractual)
  • Volume target: 20–30 active gifting partners at any time
  • Selection: Active, authentic, Queensland/coastal/fitness focus, genuine engagement (3%+)
  • What to gift: Hero pieces from current season (value $80–$150 per influencer)

Tier 2: Gifting + Affiliate Partners (15K–50K followers)

  • Arrangement: Product gifting + 15% commission on tracked sales (via Shopify affiliate app)
  • Volume target: 10–20 active affiliates
  • Selection: Track record of content that converts; engaged community; brand-aligned aesthetic
  • What to gift: Full outfit or bundle (value $150–$250)

Tier 3: Paid Brand Ambassadors (20K+ engaged followers) — Year 2+

  • Arrangement: Monthly fee ($500–$2,000/month) + product + affiliate commission
  • Deliverables: Minimum 4 posts per month (2 feed, 2 Stories minimum), product tagging
  • Contract: 3–6 month minimum
  • Volume: 2–3 ambassadors initially
  • Selection: Values alignment, audience quality, proven conversion track record

Gifting vs. Paid Decision Framework

Outreach Process

  1. Identify target via Instagram location tags, niche hashtags, or follower analysis
  2. Engage organically for 2–4 weeks (genuine likes and comments) before reaching out
  3. Send personalised DM or email — reference their specific content
  4. No mass copy-paste pitches — every outreach is individual
  5. Follow up once after 7 days if no response; do not follow up a third time
  6. On agreement, send gifting form and brief

Influencer Brief Template

  • Brand overview and tagline
  • Product being gifted and why it was chosen for them
  • Content suggestions (not mandatory, but helpful)
  • What to tag: @bellaRhyder + #BellaRhyder #MoveBoldlyLiveFree
  • Timeline: Post within 4 weeks of receipt
  • Disclosure: Must use #ad or #gifted per ACMA guidelines

Content Production System

Monthly Batching Process

  1. Week 1: Plan next month's content calendar (pillars, themes, campaigns, briefing)
  2. Week 2: Asset preparation (brief photographer/videographer, props, locations, outfits)
  3. Week 3: Content shoot days — batch all images and video for the following month
  4. Week 4: Edit, write captions, load into scheduling tool, quality check

Monthly Content Requirements

  • 20–30 lifestyle and product images (mix of on-model and flat lays)
  • 4–8 short video clips for Reels and TikTok
  • 5–10 Story-specific assets (polls, Q&A frames, product graphics)
  • 2–3 designed graphic posts (educational carousels, quote posts)
