Week 1–2: Tease & Brand Reveal (May 4–17)

Theme: "Something is coming."

Build anticipation and a following before anyone can buy. Make launch day feel like an event worth waiting for. Early followers should feel like insiders who discovered something before it was mainstream.

Day 1 — Monday, May 4: Account Goes Live

Instagram Feed Post:

  • A single, perfectly lit close-up detail shot: fabric texture, swing tag, or waistband detail — no full product reveal
  • Caption: "Coming soon. #BellaRhyder"
  • Bio set up: "Move Boldly. Live Freely. | Queensland | Launching June 1 | Link below"
  • Link-in-bio pointing to email sign-up landing page with copy: "Be first. Get exclusive launch access + 10% off."

TikTok:

  • 10-second atmospheric clip — no talking, no product reveal. Trending audio.
  • Text overlay: "Bella Rhyder. Coming June 1. Move boldly. Live freely."

Email Landing Page Goes Live:

  • Headline: "She's coming. June 1."
  • Subheadline: "Sign up for first access to the Bella Rhyder launch + 10% off your first order."
  • CTA: "I'm in — notify me"

Day 2 — Tuesday, May 5

Instagram Story:

  • Close-up of a brand label, swing tag, or product silhouette — extreme close-up, cryptic
  • Poll sticker: "Guessing game — what are we?" (Activewear / Swimwear / Both)
  • Second Story: "Follow us — something good is coming."

Day 3 — Wednesday, May 6

Instagram Feed Post:

  • Lifestyle image — a woman running along the Brisbane esplanade at golden hour. No product visible. Natural, aspirational, beautiful.
  • Caption: "This is what we're built for. Brisbane, we're coming for you. #BellaRhyder #MoveBoldlyLiveFree"

TikTok:

  • Behind-the-scenes of photoshoot preparation — location scouting, golden hour lighting, no product reveal yet
  • Audio: something warm and coastal

Day 4 — Thursday, May 7

Instagram Story:

  • "What does your perfect activewear look like?" — open question sticker
  • Save answers publicly and in DMs for product insights and future content

Day 5 — Friday, May 8

Instagram Feed Post:

  • Dramatically lit product silhouette — backlit so shape is visible but details are not
  • Caption: "She moves differently. #MoveBoldlyLiveFree #BellaRhyder"

TikTok:

  • 15 seconds: hands holding a sealed poly mailer bag, pulling out tissue paper — no product reveal, just the unboxing anticipation
  • Text: "Something is coming. Very soon."

Day 6 — Saturday, May 9

Instagram Feed Post:

  • First partial product reveal — cropped image showing ONE detail: the waistband of the leggings, the strap of the sports bra, or the buckle of the bag
  • Caption: "This is just the beginning. #BellaRhyder"

Instagram Story:

  • Share email sign-up milestone: "X women have already signed up for first access. Are you in?"
  • Swipe-up or link sticker to sign-up page

Day 7 — Sunday, May 10

Instagram Story:

  • Sunday lifestyle content: coffee, sunny window, beach walk — warm and unhurried
  • "How are you moving this Sunday? Tell us in the comments."
  • Spend the day replying to all comments and DMs from the week — build individual connections

Day 8 — Monday, May 11: First Product Reveal + Email 1

Instagram Feed Post:

  • First proper product image — one hero piece (leggings), beautifully shot in natural light. No price yet.
  • Caption: "Meet her. Crafted for the woman who doesn't stop. Bella Rhyder arrives June 1. Link in bio to be first."

TikTok:

  • 20-second video: a woman in activewear at the beach — natural, warm, real
  • Music: something coastal and building
  • Text overlay: "This is what we're built for. June 1. #BellaRhyder"

EMAIL 1 — Waitlist Teaser (Send to sign-up list):

  • Subject: "Something we've been working on..."
  • Preview text: "June 1. You're going to love it."
  • Body:
"We've been working on something. Something made for the woman who moves through life with intention — at the gym, at the beach, at the café, everywhere in between. Bella Rhyder is almost here. On June 1, we launch our full collection: activewear, swimwear, bags, hats, and accessories built for the Queensland woman. You're on the list. That means first access — and 10% off your first order. Stay close. We'll see you June 1."
  • One product image. Countdown to launch. CTA: "Make sure you're on the list — you'll be first to shop."

Day 9 — Tuesday, May 12

Instagram Feed Post:

  • Lifestyle image — woman at the beach, natural light, relaxed and confident. No obvious product focus.
  • Caption: "Born from the Queensland coastline. Built for the women who live here. #QLDFitness #CoastalLiving"

Day 10 — Wednesday, May 13

Instagram Feed Post:

  • Product flat lay — multiple pieces laid out beautifully on sand or white linen. No prices, no CTA. Just the visual.
  • Caption: "The full collection. 3 weeks. #BellaRhyder"

Instagram Story:

  • Behind-the-scenes of photoshoot day — candid, fun, real

Day 11 — Thursday, May 14

Instagram Feed Post:

  • Founder story post — "We built Bella Rhyder because..." A personal, genuine caption from the founder
  • Example caption: "We built Bella Rhyder because we were tired of choosing between activewear that performed and activewear that looked good. Between beach pieces that were resort-ready and gym pieces that survived a real workout. We live here, in Queensland, in the sun, near the water. We built this for us. And for you. June 1."

TikTok:

  • "Why I started Bella Rhyder" — founder on camera, talking directly, personal and real
  • No script — authentic delivery

Note: This is also the week influencer gifting packages should be arriving with recipients. Brief them: post from May 28 onward.

Day 12 — Friday, May 15: Countdown 17 Days + Email 2

Instagram Feed Post:

  • Countdown graphic or product image with text overlay: "17 days until Bella Rhyder goes live."
  • Show two more product pieces

EMAIL 2 — Product Preview:

  • Subject: "Here's what's coming June 1."
  • Preview text: "Preview the collection before anyone else."
  • Body: Preview of 4–5 hero products with images and names (no prices yet)
  • CTA: "Save this email — you'll want to shop the moment we go live. June 1, 9 AM AEST."

Day 13 — Saturday, May 16

Instagram Feed Post:

  • First full collection image — team shot or flat lay showing the breadth of the range: activewear, swimwear, bags, hats, accessories
  • Caption: "Everything arrives June 1. She's ready. Are you? Link in bio."

Instagram Story:

  • Ask: "Which category are you most excited for?" (Poll: Activewear / Swimwear / Bags / All of them)
  • Countdown sticker set for June 1, 9:00 AM AEST

Day 14 — Sunday, May 17

Instagram Feed Post:

  • A single, powerful lifestyle image. Aspirational. No product in frame.
  • Caption: "June 1. 9am. bellaRhyder.com.au — link in bio."

TikTok:

  • 30-second "brand story" video — atmospheric, emotional, builds to "June 1. We're coming."
  • Music: Building, cinematic

Instagram Story:

  • Behind-the-scenes of campaign photoshoot day (if not yet shared)

TikTok:

Theme: "Get ready. She's almost here."

Countdown urgency, final email list push, influencer posts live, social proof building pre-launch.

Day 22 — Monday, May 25: 7 Days Out

Instagram Feed Post:

  • "7 days." — clean, confident countdown image
  • Caption: "One week. 9am AEST. June 1. bellaRhyder.com.au — link in bio. Are you on the list?"

TikTok:

  • "One week until we launch" — founder on camera, excited and personal

EMAIL 4 — Final Countdown:

  • Subject: "7 days. You'll want to be ready."
  • Body: Full product preview (6–8 hero products with images and names), launch offer preview ("free shipping + 10% off for waitlist members on launch day only")
  • CTA: "Set a reminder. June 1. 9 AM AEST."

Day 23 — Tuesday, May 26

Instagram:

  • "5 reasons we built Bella Rhyder" — brand values carousel
  • Shares the founder's why, the product philosophy, and the lifestyle vision

Day 24 — Wednesday, May 27

Instagram:

  • Product story: highlight the most anticipated product based on poll data from earlier in the month

TikTok:

Launch Day — Monday, June 1, 2026

Minute-by-Minute Content Calendar

7:00 AM — Final Story countdown

  • "2 hours. Set your alarms."
  • Show packing station / launch day setup behind-the-scenes

8:30 AM — "30 minutes" Story

  • "Almost time. Link in bio. 9am AEST."

9:00 AM — STORE GOES LIVE

  • All products published on Shopify
  • Launch email triggered automatically (EMAIL 6 — Launch Email)

EMAIL 6 — Launch Email (9:00 AM AEST):

  • Subject: "It's here. Bella Rhyder is live."
  • Preview text: "Shop the full collection now — free shipping today only."
  • Body: Hero image, brand intro (50 words), full product grid (8 hero products), launch offer
  • Offer: Free shipping on all orders over $100, first 24 hours only. 10% off for waitlist members (auto-applied)
  • CTA: "Shop Now" — links directly to homepage

9:02 AM — Instagram Feed Post (Launch Hero)

  • The best image in the collection. One product or lifestyle hero shot.
  • Caption: "She's here. Bella Rhyder is officially live. Move boldly. Live freely. Shop the full collection now — link in bio. #BellaRhyder #MoveBoldlyLiveFree #AussieActivewear"
  • 15 hashtags loaded in caption or first comment

9:15 AM — TikTok Launch Video

  • High energy, full product showcase, 30–45 seconds, trending audio
  • Text overlay: "WE ARE LIVE. Shop now — link in bio. #BellaRhyder"

9:30 AM — Instagram Story Sequence (7 frames)

  • Frame 1: "SHE'S LIVE." — bold text on navy background
  • Frame 2: Product 1 (leggings) — hero shot + price + link sticker
  • Frame 3: Product 2 (sports bra) — same format
  • Frame 4: Product 3 (bikini set) — same format
  • Frame 5: Product 4 (gym bag) — same format
  • Frame 6: "FREE SHIPPING today on orders over $100. LINK IN BIO."
  • Frame 7: Behind-the-scenes of founder at launch: "Thank you for being here from Day 1."

10:00 AM — Influencer posts coordinated

  • Influencers briefed to post between 9–11 AM
  • Reshare first influencer post immediately to Stories

11:00 AM — First sale story

  • "First order is in. Thank you." — authentic, real, personal

12:00 PM — Mid-day Story check-in

  • "It's been 3 hours and we're overwhelmed. Thank you."
  • Show order notification feed if comfortable (or describe the response)

1:00 PM — TikTok Post 2

  • Founder on camera: what this moment means, genuine emotion, thank you
  • Real, unscripted, 30–60 seconds

3:00 PM — Instagram Story: product spotlight

  • Pick one product with strongest early traction — spotlight it with link sticker

5:00 PM — "Selling fast" Story

  • One product near low stock: "These are going fast — link in bio."

6:00 PM — Instagram Live (Optional but recommended)

  • 20–30 minute live with the founder
  • Product walkthrough and try-on, Q&A with live audience
  • Invite influencer guest to join
  • Exclusive launch day discount code shared during Live
  • Save replay to Stories for 24 hours

8:00 PM — Evening Story wrap-up

  • "Day 1. Thank you. We're just getting started."
  • Countdown sticker: "Free shipping ends in X hours"

11:00 PM — Final Day 1 Story

  • "Less than 1 hour left for free shipping. Link in bio."
