Bella Rhyder -- Pre-Launch Content Plan

Platform: Instagram (primary)

Cadence: 3 posts per week (Mon / Wed / Fri)

Duration: 5 weeks (15 posts)

Goal: Build audience, establish brand identity, drive waitlist signups

Assets: 160 existing product photos

Rules: No prices. No fake customers. No fabricated testimonials. No launch date promises.

WEEK 1 -- Introducing the Brand

Post 1 (Mon) -- BRAND ANNOUNCEMENT

Type: Static image (hero product shot -- best piece from the 160 photos)

Caption:

Something is coming.

Born on the Queensland coastline. Designed for women who move boldly and live freely.

Bella Rhyder -- activewear and lifestyle pieces for the woman who doesn't separate her workout from her life.

Waitlist is open. Link in bio.

Hashtags: #BellaRhyder #MoveBoldlyLiveFree #AussieActivewear #QueenslandFitness #ActiveLifestyle #BrisbaneActive #CoastalFitness #ActiveWomen #ComingSoon

CTA: Link in bio to waitlist signup

PHOTO SELECTION NOTES

Pull from the 160 existing product photos. Prioritise:

  • Golden hour / warm natural lighting
  • Movement and energy over static poses
  • Fabric detail and texture close-ups for teaser posts
  • Queensland coastal settings where available
  • Variety of product categories across the 5 weeks

Apply the approved Lightroom preset direction from the Brand Bible:

  • Exposure: +0.3 / Highlights: -20 / Shadows: +15 / Warmth: +18
  • Saturation: -5 / Teal HSL: desaturated / Orange: +8 Luminance

STORIES STRATEGY (Supplement)

Run Instagram Stories 3-5x per week alongside feed posts:

  • Polls: "Which colourway?" / "Gym or beach first?"
  • Countdown stickers for launch
  • Behind-the-scenes video snippets
  • Waitlist link sticker on every story
  • Background: Midnight Navy (#1A1A2E) or white
  • Text: DM Sans, white or Warm Gold (#E8B86D)
