BELLA RHYDER — ELEVATOR PITCHES

Ready-to-Use Brand Pitches for Every Situation

10-SECOND PITCH

For quick brand intros, podcast intros, event name tags

"Bella Rhyder is a Queensland-born active lifestyle brand — premium activewear and swimwear for women who move boldly and live freely."

30-SECOND PITCH

For networking events, market stalls, casual conversations

"Bella Rhyder is a Brisbane-based active lifestyle brand built for Australian women who live at the intersection of fitness and the coast. We design premium activewear, swimwear, and lifestyle accessories — sports bras, leggings, one-pieces, bags, hats — built to perform and styled to turn heads.

We're for the woman who goes from a 6am session to brunch to the beach without changing her energy — just her shoes. Think Lululemon quality with a Queensland soul.

We ship nationally across Australia and we're growing fast. You can find us at bellaRhyder.com.au."

60-SECOND PITCH

For investors, press interviews, pitch competitions

"Bella Rhyder is a premium active lifestyle brand born from the Queensland coastline. We make high-performance activewear, swimwear, and lifestyle accessories for Australian women aged 22 to 45 — women who are serious about how they move and serious about how they look while doing it.

The Australian activewear market is worth over $1.2 billion and growing, driven by a health and wellness boom that hasn't slowed. But the market is dominated by American and global brands that don't truly understand the Australian lifestyle — the heat, the coast, the culture of being outdoors year-round.

Bella Rhyder fills that gap. We are genuinely, specifically Australian — coastal, vibrant, and community-led. Our product range spans sports bras, tanks, leggings, shorts, one-piece swimwear, bikinis, bags, and hats. We operate direct-to-consumer on Shopify and ship nationally, with a wholesale program in development and an influencer community growing every week.

We're targeting $500K ARR by Year 3, with a clear path through e-commerce growth, wholesale partnerships, and limited-edition collaborative drops. The opportunity to build Australia's next great homegrown activewear brand is wide open — and Bella Rhyder is built to take it."

PRESS & MEDIA DESCRIPTION

For PR submissions, media kits, press releases, About sections

Short Description (50 words):

Bella Rhyder is a Brisbane-based premium active lifestyle brand designing high-performance activewear, swimwear, and lifestyle accessories for Australian women. Founded on Queensland's coastal identity and the belief that quality and style are not a compromise, the brand ships nationally with an e-commerce-first model through Shopify.

Medium Description (100 words):

Bella Rhyder is a Queensland-born active lifestyle brand crafting premium activewear, swimwear, and accessories for Australian women who live at the intersection of fitness and the coast. The range spans high-support sports bras, performance leggings, one-piece swimwear, bikinis, bags, and hats — each piece designed to perform at the highest level and look extraordinary doing it. Built on the belief that active women shouldn't have to choose between function and beauty, Bella Rhyder ships nationally across Australia and is building a community of women who move boldly, live freely, and never settle for average. Find them at bellaRhyder.com.au.

Long Description (200 words — for media kits and features):

Bella Rhyder was born from the Queensland coastline and the women who call it home. Founded in Brisbane, the brand designs premium activewear, swimwear, and lifestyle accessories for Australian women aged 22 to 45 — women who are as serious about how they move as they are about how they live.

The collection includes high-support sports bras, performance tanks and leggings, training shorts, one-piece swimwear, bikinis, structured bags, and casual hats — every piece built to the standard that active women actually demand. Bella Rhyder believes that performance and beauty have never been at odds, and that a sports bra should hold up in a high-intensity session and still look exceptional at brunch.

The brand is unapologetically Australian. Where global activewear giants bring a generic, polished aesthetic, Bella Rhyder brings Queensland warmth, coastal energy, and a community-first philosophy. The brand's growing influencer network, loyal social following, and direct-to-consumer model through Shopify allow it to build genuine relationships with the women it serves.

Bella Rhyder ships nationally across Australia. The brand is available exclusively at bellaRhyder.com.au, with wholesale and pop-up activations in development.

Move Boldly. Live Freely.
