Bella Rhyder -- Source of Truth

Last Updated: 2026-04-05

Identity

  • What: Premium active lifestyle e-commerce fashion brand (activewear, swimwear, bags, hats, accessories)
  • Mission: Empower Australian women to move freely, feel confident, and live fully -- every day
  • Vision: Be Australia's most loved active lifestyle brand; the brand in every Queensland woman's wardrobe, gym bag, and beach holiday
  • Tagline: Move Boldly. Live Freely.
  • Name meaning: Bella (beautiful) + Rhyder (a rider -- someone who moves through life with momentum and intention)
  • Founded: 2026
  • Location: Brisbane, Queensland, Australia
  • Revenue status: Pre-launch. Targeting June 1, 2026 store launch
  • Funding: Bootstrapped / self-funded, revenue reinvestment model
  • Estimated launch capital: $58,500--$92,000 AUD

Brand

  • Positioning: Premium coastal lifestyle activewear. Above fast fashion ($30--70), below luxury ($200+). "Lululemon quality with a Queensland soul." No Australian activewear brand authentically owns Queensland -- that is the strategic gap
  • Personality (5 words): Bold, Vibrant, Authentic, Empowering, Effortless
  • Tone of voice: Empowering not prescriptive. Energetic not exhausting. Confident not arrogant. Conversational not casual. Queensland-honest
  • Language to avoid: "Slay," "girlboss," "hustle," "flattering," "slimming," weight-loss language, excessive exclamation marks, ALL CAPS in running copy
  • Target demographic: Active women 22--45, primarily Queensland (Brisbane, Gold Coast, Sunshine Coast), household income $65K--$130K+. Fitness-forward, shops online, discovers via Instagram/TikTok, values quality over quantity

Visual Identity

  • Primary colors: Midnight Navy (#1A1A2E, 60%), White (#FFFFFF, 25%), Warm Gold (#E8B86D, 10%)
  • Accent colors: Coral Terracotta (#D4654A), Sand (#F2E8D9), Soft Blush (#F0D9D2), Dark Charcoal (#2C2C3E) -- 5% combined, campaign/seasonal use only
  • Fonts: Playfair Display (headlines), DM Sans (body/UI), Cormorant Garamond Italic (taglines/accents)
  • Photography: Natural light, golden hour, warm tones, Queensland outdoor locations, movement-led, diverse models, no stock photography, no cold/blue tones, no heavy retouching
  • Logo variants: Primary (Navy), Reversed (White), Gold, Monochrome Black, Monochrome White. BR monogram for favicon/small use

  • Pricing strategy: Value-based, mid-premium. Psychological pricing ($89 not $90). Bundle discounts 10--15%
  • Gross margin target: 55--65% (accessories higher ~70%, swimwear ~58%)
  • Average order value target: $145--$165 AUD
