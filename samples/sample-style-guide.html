02 — Logo Usage

Clear Space & Usage Rules

1x
1x

Maintain clear space equal to 1x the logo mark height on all sides.

Minimum Sizes

✓ 120px — Recommended
✗ 48px — Too Small

Do’s

  • Use approved colour variations only
  • Maintain clear space around the logo
  • Place on solid or simple backgrounds
  • Use the white version on dark backgrounds
  • Scale proportionally — never stretch

Don’ts

  • Do not rotate or skew the logo
  • Do not change the logo colours
  • Do not add drop shadows or effects
  • Do not place on busy or clashing backgrounds
  • Do not crop or partially obscure the logo
  • Do not rearrange logo elements

06 — Graphics

Brand Elements

Brand Pattern
Brand Texture
Decorative Element

Use these elements as backgrounds, borders, and accents across all brand materials. Apply at reduced opacity (10–20%) for subtle texture, or at full strength as feature panels. The pattern works well as section dividers; the texture adds warmth to plain surfaces; the decorative element is ideal for certificates, invitations, and premium collateral.

7
07 — Photography

Photography

Do’s

  • Warm, natural lighting conditions
  • Earthy, on-brand colour tones
  • Real people in authentic settings
  • Soft focus backgrounds with depth
  • Consistent editing and colour grading

Don’ts

  • Generic stock photography clichés
  • Harsh direct flash or flat lighting
  • Neon or off-brand colour palettes
  • Overly posed or artificial compositions
  • Low resolution or pixelated images
