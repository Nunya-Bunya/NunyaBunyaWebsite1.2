Do's and Don'ts

Do

  • Use on white or light neutral backgrounds
  • Maintain clear space around the logo
  • Use provided colour variations for dark backgrounds
  • Keep the logo proportions — never stretch
  • Use high-resolution files for print

Don't

  • Don't change the logo colours
  • Don't add drop shadows, glows, or effects
  • Don't rotate or skew the logo
  • Don't place on busy or patterned backgrounds
  • Don't rearrange the icon and text
  • Don't use low-resolution versions for print
