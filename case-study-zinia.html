Strategy — Process

How We Did It

1

Discovery & Audit

Identified critical issues including template pollution, pricing confusion, and missing contact information across Brisbane Zinia Massage's digital presence. Analysed competitor landscape in Brisbane CBD massage therapy market and Mary's unique positioning with traditional Chinese massage expertise.

2

Brand Bible (BFK)

Developed comprehensive brand foundation showcasing Mary's specialized skills in remedial, deep tissue, and traditional Chinese massage for Brisbane professionals. Created messaging framework emphasizing stress relief, sports recovery, and mobile hotel outcall services for Queen Street CBD location.

3

AI Photoshoot

Generated 15 professional wellness images featuring traditional Chinese massage techniques, remedial therapy sessions, and Brisbane CBD professional clientele scenarios. Created diverse visual library showing Mary's expertise across different massage modalities and mobile outcall services.

4

Website Build

Built 5-page responsive website with streamlined booking system, clear service descriptions, and prominent Queen Street CBD location details. Integrated mobile massage booking functionality and removed all template content while highlighting Mary's traditional Chinese massage specialization.

5

Asset Library

Created 10 branded social media assets targeting Brisbane CBD professionals, hotel guests, and wellness seekers requiring specialized massage therapy. Developed consistent visual identity reflecting Mary's expertise in traditional Chinese techniques and professional remedial treatments.
